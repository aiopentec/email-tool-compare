7 Best ActiveCampaign Alternatives in 2025

While ActiveCampaign excels at combining email marketing with advanced automation and CRM, it may not be the right fit for every business—whether due to pricing, complexity, or specific feature needs. We've compiled the best alternatives that offer similar functionality or better options for particular use cases, from budget-friendly solutions to industry-specific platforms.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Cost constraints: ActiveCampaign's $15/month starting price may be steep for small teams, while many alternatives offer free tiers or lower entry points
  • Simplicity: Some businesses need a more straightforward email marketing tool without the complexity of full CRM and automation systems
  • Industry-specific features: E-commerce brands, creators, and newsletter publishers may benefit from platforms tailored to their specific needs

Best ActiveCampaign Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: Small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite offers the most affordable paid plans starting at just $9/month, combined with a generous free tier supporting 1,000 subscribers. It delivers automation and landing pages without the complexity of a full CRM, making it ideal for businesses wanting essential marketing tools at lower costs.

Brevo
All-in-one CRM suite  ·  Best for: Transactional email and multi-channel marketing
Brevo matches ActiveCampaign's CRM capabilities while adding SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat on a single platform. Its free tier supports up to 100,000 subscribers and includes unlimited transactional emails, making it exceptional for businesses needing multi-channel reach beyond traditional email marketing.

ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: Creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit is purpose-built for creators with visual automation, subscriber tagging, and integrated commerce features that ActiveCampaign lacks. It offers a free tier with 1,000 subscribers and emphasizes simplicity, making it better suited for content creators than traditional businesses.

Klaviyo
The platform for intelligent marketing automation  ·  Best for: E-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce
Klaviyo specializes in e-commerce marketing with predictive analytics, SMS integration, and native Shopify/WooCommerce connections that provide deeper insights than ActiveCampaign. For online retailers, it offers superior segmentation and flow automation specifically designed for customer lifecycle marketing.

Mailchimp
The all-in-one marketing platform  ·  Best for: Beginners and small businesses
Mailchimp's free tier with 500 subscribers and user-friendly drag-and-drop interface makes it the easiest entry point for businesses new to email marketing. While it lacks ActiveCampaign's advanced automation depth, it covers essential features like A/B testing and audience segmentation at no cost.

Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: Newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv uniquely combines email marketing with newsletter monetization, an ad network, and referral programs—features ActiveCampaign doesn't offer. It's the ideal alternative for publishers seeking to generate revenue directly from their audience while managing growth efficiently.

GetResponse
Complete online marketing platform  ·  Best for: Online course creators and webinar hosts
GetResponse integrates webinars, online courses, and conversion funnels alongside email automation—creating a complete ecosystem for digital product creators that ActiveCampaign doesn't support. This makes it superior for businesses running webinars or selling digital courses.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to ActiveCampaign?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with up to 100,000 subscribers and includes email campaigns, SMS, live chat, and CRM capabilities—matching many of ActiveCampaign's core features at no cost. For simplicity, Mailchimp or MailerLite provide more straightforward free options with 500-1,000 subscriber limits and essential automation features.

What is the best ActiveCampaign alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the best choice for small businesses, combining affordability ($9/month entry point), ease of use, and robust automation without unnecessary complexity. It includes landing pages, pop-ups, and e-commerce integrations while remaining accessible to teams without advanced marketing expertise.

Is there a cheaper alternative to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, MailerLite starts at $9/month (compared to ActiveCampaign's $15/month), and both Brevo and Mailchimp offer free tiers that eliminate startup costs entirely. If you need advanced features similar to ActiveCampaign's CRM, Brevo's free plan with 100,000 subscribers provides exceptional value without any paid commitment required.
