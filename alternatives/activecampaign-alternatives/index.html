Best ActiveCampaign Alternatives in 2025

While ActiveCampaign excels at combining email marketing with advanced automation and CRM, it may not be the right fit for every business—whether due to pricing, feature set, or specific use case requirements. We've compiled the best alternatives that offer comparable or specialized functionality across different business needs and budgets.

Why look for a ActiveCampaign alternative?

  • Need a free tier or lower starting price for budget-conscious teams
  • Looking for industry-specific features (e-commerce, newsletters, creators)
  • Want simpler automation workflows without enterprise complexity

Best ActiveCampaign alternatives

MailerLite

Simple email marketing for small businesses

Best for: Small businesses and freelancers on a budget

MailerLite offers a similar feature set to ActiveCampaign at a fraction of the cost, starting at just $9/month with a generous free tier for up to 1,000 subscribers. If you need automation and landing pages without enterprise pricing, MailerLite delivers excellent value with intuitive drag-and-drop tools and reliable customer support.

Brevo

All-in-one CRM suite

Best for: Transactional email and multi-channel marketing

Brevo matches ActiveCampaign's CRM capabilities while adding SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat in one platform, making it ideal for businesses needing omnichannel communication. Its free tier supports up to 100,000 contacts, which is unmatched in the industry, perfect for growing teams testing multi-channel strategies.

ConvertKit

Email platform built for creators

Best for: Creators, bloggers, and course sellers

While ActiveCampaign is business-focused, ConvertKit is purpose-built for creators with visual automation, subscriber tagging, and built-in commerce features. If you're monetizing content or selling digital products, ConvertKit's creator-centric approach beats ActiveCampaign's general-purpose design.

Compare ActiveCampaign vs ConvertKit

Beehiiv

The newsletter platform built for growth

Best for: Newsletter publishers and media operators

Beehiiv specializes in newsletter monetization with an integrated ad network and referral programs that ActiveCampaign doesn't offer. For publishers focused on growing and monetizing newsletters, Beehiiv's 2,500-subscriber free tier and growth-focused features provide superior value.

Compare ActiveCampaign vs Beehiiv

Klaviyo

The platform for intelligent marketing automation

Best for: E-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce

Klaviyo outpaces ActiveCampaign in e-commerce-specific features, including deep Shopify/WooCommerce integrations, predictive analytics, and review collection. If your business depends on e-commerce, Klaviyo's specialized tooling and pre-built flows for online stores make it the superior choice.

Mailchimp

The all-in-one marketing platform

Best for: Beginners and small businesses

Mailchimp offers a free tier with 500 contacts and an intuitive interface that's more beginner-friendly than ActiveCampaign's steeper learning curve. For teams new to marketing automation, Mailchimp's simplicity and free access to essential features make it an excellent starting point.

Compare ActiveCampaign vs Mailchimp

GetResponse

Complete online marketing platform

Best for: Online course creators and webinar hosts

GetResponse integrates webinars, online courses, and landing pages directly into its platform in ways ActiveCampaign requires third-party tools for. If hosting webinars or selling courses is central to your business, GetResponse's all-in-one approach eliminates integration complexity.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free alternative to ActiveCampaign?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with support for up to 100,000 contacts, plus email, SMS, and live chat features. If you need a smaller free audience, MailerLite and ConvertKit both support 1,000 free subscribers with automation and landing pages included, making them excellent free alternatives depending on your specific needs.

What is the best ActiveCampaign alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the top choice for small businesses, offering automation, landing pages, and pop-ups starting at just $9/month with a free tier. Brevo is also excellent for small teams needing CRM plus multi-channel marketing (email, SMS, live chat) at competitive pricing without compromising on features.

Is there a cheaper alternative to ActiveCampaign?

Yes—MailerLite starts at $9/month compared to ActiveCampaign's $15/month, and both Mailchimp and Brevo offer free tiers with paid plans starting at $13 and $25/month respectively. For budget-conscious teams, MailerLite provides the best balance of cost and features without sacrificing core automation capabilities.
