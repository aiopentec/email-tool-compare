7 Best Constant Contact Alternatives in 2025

While Constant Contact offers solid email marketing features, alternatives may provide better value, superior user experience, or specialized features for your specific needs. Whether you're looking for a free tier, advanced automation, or industry-specific tools, there's likely a better fit for your business.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Many alternatives offer free tiers with generous subscriber limits, letting you start at $0 instead of $12/month
  • Competitors like MailerLite and Beehiiv have significantly higher user satisfaction ratings on review platforms
  • Specialized platforms like ConvertKit and Klaviyo offer better automation and features tailored to creators and e-commerce businesses

Best Constant Contact Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: Small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite starts at just $9/month with a free tier supporting 1,000 subscribers, making it the most affordable option for growing businesses. It offers drag-and-drop editing, automation, and landing pages—matching Constant Contact's core features at a lower price point.

Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: Newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv excels at newsletter monetization with built-in ad networks and referral programs, unlike Constant Contact. With a 4.7 G2 rating and 4.4 Trustpilot score, it's ideal if you want to generate revenue from your subscriber list.

ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: Creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit's visual automation builder and subscriber tagging system are purpose-built for creators managing complex workflows. It includes commerce features and sequences that make it easier to sell digital products than Constant Contact.

Mailchimp
The all-in-one marketing platform  ·  Best for: Beginners and small businesses
Mailchimp's free tier supports 500 subscribers at no cost, making it perfect for testing email marketing before spending money. It includes A/B testing, audience segmentation, and automation without requiring a paid plan.

ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: Businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with a full CRM, lead scoring, and site tracking in one platform—going far beyond Constant Contact's capabilities. It's ideal for sales-focused businesses that need deep customer relationship management.

Klaviyo
The platform for intelligent marketing automation  ·  Best for: E-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce
Klaviyo specializes in e-commerce with native Shopify integration, predictive analytics, and review collection tools. If you're selling products online, Klaviyo's specialized features outperform Constant Contact's general e-commerce capabilities.

Brevo
All-in-one CRM suite  ·  Best for: Transactional email and multi-channel marketing
Brevo offers an exceptionally generous free tier with 100,000 emails/month and includes SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat alongside email. It's the most feature-rich free option and best for businesses wanting omnichannel communication beyond email.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Constant Contact?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with 100,000 emails per month and includes SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat. Mailchimp and MailerLite also provide solid free options with 500 and 1,000 subscriber limits respectively, making them ideal starting points for budget-conscious users.

What is the best Constant Contact alternative for small business?

MailerLite combines affordability ($9/month to start), ease of use, and strong customer satisfaction (4.5 Trustpilot rating). It provides drag-and-drop templates, automation, and landing pages without overwhelming small business owners with unnecessary complexity.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Constant Contact?

Yes, MailerLite starts at $9/month—$3 less than Constant Contact. Better yet, Mailchimp, Brevo, ConvertKit, and Beehiiv all offer free plans that cost nothing, allowing you to test features before committing to a paid plan.
