Best Constant Contact Alternatives for Email Marketing

While Constant Contact offers solid email marketing features, many users seek alternatives that provide better value, more advanced automation, or specialized tools for their specific needs. Whether you're looking for a free option, superior customer support, or industry-specific features, there are several compelling alternatives worth considering.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Lower starting prices or free tiers with higher subscriber limits
  • Better ratings on customer review platforms like Trustpilot and G2
  • More specialized features for e-commerce, creators, or newsletters
  • More intuitive automation and segmentation capabilities

Best Constant Contact Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite starts at just $9/month with a generous free tier supporting 1,000 subscribers at no cost. It offers an excellent balance of affordability, ease of use, and essential features like drag-drop editing, automation, and landing pages.

Mailchimp
The all-in-one marketing platform  ·  Best for: beginners and small businesses
Mailchimp's free tier allows up to 500 contacts with no time limit, making it perfect for testing before committing financially. Its intuitive interface and automation features make it ideal for businesses just starting their email marketing journey.

ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit is specifically designed for content creators with features like subscriber tagging, visual automations, and integrated commerce capabilities. Its free tier supports up to 1,000 subscribers, perfect for growing creators before monetization.

Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv excels at newsletter monetization with built-in ad networks and referral programs, plus it has the highest Trustpilot rating (4.4) of all alternatives. It supports 2,500 free subscribers and offers superior analytics for newsletter growth.

ActiveCampaign

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Constant Contact?

MailerLite and Mailchimp both offer excellent free tiers. MailerLite provides better value with 1,000 free subscribers compared to Mailchimp's 500, plus lower paid pricing starting at $9/month. For newsletter publishers, Beehiiv offers 2,500 free subscribers with monetization features.

What is the best Constant Contact alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the top choice for budget-conscious small businesses, combining affordability (starting at $9/month), ease of use, and essential marketing features. For those needing CRM and advanced automation, ActiveCampaign offers more power at a comparable price point.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Constant Contact?

Yes, MailerLite at $9/month is $3 cheaper than Constant Contact's $12/month starting price. Brevo also offers exceptional value with unlimited free sends, while Mailchimp provides completely free service for up to 500 subscribers.
