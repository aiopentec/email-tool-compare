7 Best Brevo Alternatives in 2025

While Brevo offers an impressive all-in-one suite with multi-channel marketing capabilities, you might be looking for alternatives that specialize in specific use cases or offer different pricing structures. Whether you need creator-focused tools, advanced automation, or budget-friendly options, there are several excellent email marketing platforms that can better serve your unique business needs.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Find a platform more tailored to your specific industry (e-commerce, newsletters, courses)
  • Access better customer support and user experience ratings on some alternatives
  • Get more competitive pricing for your subscriber count or feature requirements

Best Brevo Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite offers the lowest starting price at just $9/month and provides a generous free tier with 1,000 subscribers, making it ideal for bootstrapped startups. It combines ease-of-use with essential features like automation, landing pages, and pop-ups without overwhelming complexity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Brevo?

MailerLite and Mailchimp both offer excellent free tiers, but MailerLite provides better value with 1,000 free subscribers compared to Mailchimp's 500. If you're specifically looking for newsletter growth, Beehiiv's free tier supports up to 2,500 subscribers, making it the most generous option for growing audiences.

What is the best Brevo alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the top choice for small businesses due to its affordable $9 starting price, intuitive interface, and feature-rich free tier. It offers all essential email marketing capabilities without the complexity of enterprise platforms, making it perfect for solopreneurs and small teams.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Brevo?

Yes, MailerLite starts at $9/month compared to Brevo's $25/month entry price, offering 40% savings on paid plans. GetResponse at $19/month and Mailchimp at $13/month are also more affordable options, though MailerLite provides the best value for the features included.
