7 Best Brevo Alternatives in 2025

While Brevo offers an impressive all-in-one suite with multi-channel marketing capabilities, you might be looking for alternatives that specialize in specific use cases or offer different pricing structures. Whether you need creator-focused tools, advanced automation, or budget-friendly options, there are several excellent email marketing platforms that can better serve your unique business needs.

Why look for a Brevo alternative?

  • Find a platform more tailored to your specific industry (e-commerce, newsletters, courses)
  • Access better customer support and user experience ratings on some alternatives
  • Get more competitive pricing for your subscriber count or feature requirements

Best Brevo alternatives

MailerLite

Simple email marketing for small businesses

Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget

MailerLite offers the lowest starting price at just $9/month and provides a generous free tier with 1,000 subscribers, making it ideal for bootstrapped startups. It combines ease-of-use with essential features like automation, landing pages, and pop-ups without overwhelming complexity.

ConvertKit

Email platform built for creators

Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers

ConvertKit is purpose-built for content creators with visual automation workflows, commerce integrations, and subscriber tagging that make monetization and audience management seamless. Unlike Brevo's generalist approach, ConvertKit prioritizes the unique workflows of creators and course sellers.

Beehiiv

The newsletter platform built for growth

Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators

Beehiiv specializes in newsletter growth with built-in monetization, an ad network, and referral programs that help publishers generate revenue directly from subscribers. It's optimized for the creator-to-subscriber relationship in ways that generic email platforms cannot match.

Compare Brevo vs Beehiiv

ActiveCampaign

Email, automation, and CRM combined

Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM

ActiveCampaign excels with enterprise-grade automation, advanced lead scoring, and sophisticated CRM features that rival dedicated CRM platforms. If you need deeper customer intelligence and complex automation workflows, ActiveCampaign outperforms Brevo in these specific areas.

Mailchimp

The all-in-one marketing platform

Best for: beginners and small businesses

Mailchimp offers the most generous free tier with 500 free subscribers and an extremely user-friendly interface perfect for marketing newcomers. It's the industry standard for simplicity, though recent user reviews suggest some dissatisfaction with platform changes and support.

Klaviyo

The platform for intelligent marketing automation

Best for: e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce

Klaviyo is purpose-built for e-commerce with deep Shopify and WooCommerce integrations, predictive analytics, and review management that cater specifically to online retailers. If email is part of your e-commerce strategy, Klaviyo's specialized features far exceed what Brevo provides.

Compare Brevo vs Klaviyo

GetResponse

Complete online marketing platform

Best for: online course creators and webinar hosts

GetResponse uniquely combines email marketing with webinar hosting, online course management, and conversion funnels in a single platform. This integrated approach makes it superior to Brevo for creators who need multiple content delivery channels.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free alternative to Brevo?

MailerLite and Mailchimp both offer excellent free tiers, but MailerLite provides better value with 1,000 free subscribers compared to Mailchimp's 500. If you're specifically looking for newsletter growth, Beehiiv's free tier supports up to 2,500 subscribers, making it the most generous option for growing audiences.

What is the best Brevo alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the top choice for small businesses due to its affordable $9 starting price, intuitive interface, and feature-rich free tier. It offers all essential email marketing capabilities without the complexity of enterprise platforms, making it perfect for solopreneurs and small teams.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Brevo?

Yes, MailerLite starts at $9/month compared to Brevo's $25/month entry price, offering 40% savings on paid plans. GetResponse at $19/month and Mailchimp at $13/month are also more affordable options, though MailerLite provides the best value for the features included.
