7 Best Brevo Alternatives in 2025

While Brevo offers comprehensive CRM and multi-channel marketing capabilities, you might be looking for alternatives that better suit your specific needs, budget, or use case. Whether you need simpler email marketing, better e-commerce integrations, or more advanced automation features, there are several strong competitors worth considering.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Better specialization for your industry (e-commerce, creators, newsletters)
  • Superior user experience and interface design for your skill level
  • More competitive pricing or higher subscriber limits for your business size

Best Brevo Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite offers the lowest entry price at just $9/month with a free tier supporting 1,000 subscribers, making it ideal if you want affordability without sacrificing features. It includes drag-drop editor, automation, landing pages, and surveys—all at a fraction of Brevo's cost.

2
ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign is the best alternative if you need enterprise-grade automation and CRM without Brevo's multi-channel approach. It excels at lead scoring, dynamic content, and sophisticated workflows for sales-driven businesses.

3
Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv is purpose-built for newsletter creators with built-in monetization, ad networks, and referral programs that Brevo doesn't offer. If you're running a subscription newsletter business, Beehiiv's growth-focused features and generous 2,500 free subscriber limit make it superior.

4
ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit specializes in creator economics with visual automations, subscriber tagging, and integrated commerce features that Brevo lacks. It's the clear winner for bloggers and online course creators who need subscriber segmentation without complexity.

5
Mailchimp
The all-in-one marketing platform  ·  Best for: beginners and small businesses
Mailchimp remains the most recognizable alternative with strong A/B testing, audience segmentation, and landing pages built for marketing teams. It's ideal for beginners seeking an intuitive interface, though its 500 free subscriber limit is restrictive compared to Brevo's 100,000.

6
Klaviyo
The platform for intelligent marketing automation  ·  Best for: e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce
Klaviyo dominates e-commerce email marketing with deep Shopify/WooCommerce integrations, predictive analytics, and SMS that specifically target online stores. Choose Klaviyo over Brevo if your primary focus is maximizing revenue from e-commerce customer relationships.

7
GetResponse
Complete online marketing platform  ·  Best for: online course creators and webinar hosts
GetResponse uniquely bundles email marketing with webinars, online courses, and landing pages, offering better value for educators and course creators than Brevo. Its conversion funnels and integrated webinar platform make it ideal if you need more than just email marketing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Brevo?

MailerLite is the best free alternative with 1,000 free subscribers and comprehensive features including automation, landing pages, and surveys. However, Brevo's free tier supports up to 100,000 subscribers, so if you have a large contact list, Beehiiv (2,500 free subscribers) is the next best option for maintaining a generous free tier.

What is the best Brevo alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the top choice for small businesses, combining affordability ($9/month), ease of use, and essential marketing tools without overwhelming complexity. If you need CRM capabilities alongside email, ActiveCampaign offers better automation at a comparable price point.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Brevo?

Yes, MailerLite starts at just $9/month, making it $16 cheaper than Brevo's $25 entry price. GetResponse also undercuts Brevo at $19/month with webinar capabilities included, offering better value if you need functionality beyond email marketing.
