Best MailerLite Alternatives

While MailerLite excels as a budget-friendly email marketing platform for small businesses, other tools may better suit specific needs like e-commerce, newsletter publishing, or advanced automation. Whether you're outgrowing MailerLite's features or seeking specialized functionality, we've identified the top alternatives worth considering.

Why look for a MailerLite alternative?

  • Need advanced automation and CRM capabilities beyond email marketing
  • Looking for industry-specific features like e-commerce integrations or webinar hosting
  • Require higher subscriber limits or different pricing structures for your growth stage

Best MailerLite alternatives

Beehiiv

The newsletter platform built for growth

Best for: Newsletter publishers and media operators

Beehiiv stands out with built-in monetization features, an ad network, and referral programs specifically designed for newsletter publishers. It offers 2,500 free subscribers compared to MailerLite's 1,000, making it ideal for growing newsletters focused on revenue generation.

Brevo

All-in-one CRM suite

Best for: Transactional email and multi-channel marketing

Brevo offers an exceptional free tier with 100,000 contacts and includes SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat alongside email. It's the best choice if you need multi-channel communication and transactional email APIs without breaking the budget.

ConvertKit

Email platform built for creators

Best for: Creators, bloggers, and course sellers

ConvertKit specializes in serving creators with visual automation workflows, subscriber tagging, and integrated commerce features. It provides 1,000 free subscribers and superior tools for building audience relationships and selling digital products.

ActiveCampaign

Email, automation, and CRM combined

Best for: Businesses needing advanced automation and CRM

ActiveCampaign combines powerful marketing automation with integrated CRM, lead scoring, and dynamic content personalization. It's the top choice for growing businesses ready to invest in enterprise-level features beyond basic email marketing.

Mailchimp

The all-in-one marketing platform

Best for: Beginners and small businesses

Mailchimp is the most established player in email marketing with 20+ years of history and robust A/B testing capabilities. While it has a smaller free tier (500 contacts), its platform maturity and extensive integrations make it a reliable alternative for growing businesses.

GetResponse

Complete online marketing platform

Best for: Online course creators and webinar hosts

GetResponse uniquely bundles email marketing with native webinar hosting, online course creation, and conversion funnels. It's ideal for educators and entrepreneurs who want a comprehensive platform for building and monetizing digital products.

Klaviyo

The platform for intelligent marketing automation

Best for: E-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce

Klaviyo specializes in e-commerce with deep Shopify and WooCommerce integrations, predictive analytics, and SMS capabilities. It's the best choice if you're running an online store and need sophisticated customer journey automation tied to purchase behavior.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free alternative to MailerLite?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with 100,000 contacts plus multi-channel features including SMS and WhatsApp. If you prefer a pure email solution, Beehiiv provides 2,500 free subscribers with newsletter-specific features, or ConvertKit offers 1,000 free subscribers optimized for creators.

What is the best MailerLite alternative for small business?

Mailchimp and Brevo are excellent for small businesses, offering established platforms with strong feature sets. Brevo edges ahead with lower costs and multi-channel capabilities, while Mailchimp provides more integrations and platform maturity if budget is less of a concern.

Is there a cheaper alternative to MailerLite?

Brevo is significantly cheaper for growing lists, offering up to 100,000 free contacts versus MailerLite's 1,000. Beehiiv and ConvertKit also provide 2,500+ free subscribers at no cost, though their paid tiers start higher ($39 and $29 respectively vs. MailerLite's $9).
