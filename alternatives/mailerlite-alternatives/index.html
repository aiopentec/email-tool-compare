7 Best MailerLite Alternatives in 2025

While MailerLite excels as a budget-friendly email marketing solution, different businesses have different needs. Whether you need advanced CRM features, e-commerce integrations, or monetization capabilities, these alternatives offer compelling reasons to switch. We've compared the top tools to help you find the perfect fit for your marketing goals.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Need advanced automation and CRM capabilities beyond basic email campaigns
  • Looking for industry-specific features like e-commerce integrations or webinar hosting
  • Want better pricing for larger subscriber lists or additional marketing channels

Best MailerLite Alternatives in 2025

1
Brevo
All-in-one CRM suite  ·  Best for: transactional email and multi-channel marketing
Brevo offers an exceptionally generous free tier with up to 100,000 subscribers, making it ideal for growing businesses that don't want to pay until they truly scale. Its multi-channel approach—combining email, SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat—provides more communication options than MailerLite in a single platform.

2
Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv specializes in newsletter monetization with built-in features like ad networks and referral programs, perfect for creators earning directly from subscribers. Its 2,500 free subscriber limit and superior growth-focused tools make it a better choice than MailerLite for ambitious newsletter publishers.

3
ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit is purpose-built for creators with intuitive visual automations, subscriber tagging, and seamless course selling capabilities. Its focus on creator workflows and commerce integration makes it significantly better than MailerLite for bloggers, podcasters, and online educators.

4
ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign combines powerful email marketing with enterprise-grade CRM, lead scoring, and site tracking—capabilities that far exceed MailerLite's core offering. It's the superior choice for businesses that need sophisticated automation workflows and integrated customer relationship management.

5
Mailchimp
The all-in-one marketing platform  ·  Best for: beginners and small businesses
Mailchimp offers an established, free tier with landing pages, A/B testing, and audience segmentation for small businesses. While it has lower Trustpilot ratings than MailerLite, its brand recognition and comprehensive free features make it a familiar alternative for budget-conscious startups.

6
Klaviyo
The platform for intelligent marketing automation  ·  Best for: e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce
Klaviyo is specifically designed for e-commerce with native Shopify and WooCommerce integrations, predictive analytics, and SMS capabilities. If you run an online store, Klaviyo's specialized features and e-commerce focus significantly outperform MailerLite's generic approach.

7
GetResponse
Complete online marketing platform  ·  Best for: online course creators and webinar hosts
GetResponse is the standout choice for course creators and webinar hosts, offering integrated landing pages, automation, online courses, and webinar functionality in one platform. Its comprehensive toolset for course marketing is far more robust than what MailerLite provides.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to MailerLite?

Brevo is the best free alternative, offering up to 100,000 free subscribers—significantly more than MailerLite's 1,000 limit. It includes email campaigns, SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat in its free tier, making it exceptionally valuable for businesses looking to avoid costs as they grow. For newsletter publishers specifically, Beehiiv provides 2,500 free subscribers with dedicated monetization features.

What is the best MailerLite alternative for small business?

Brevo is excellent for small businesses due to its massive free tier and affordable paid plans starting at $25. If you need e-commerce integrations, Klaviyo serves Shopify and WooCommerce stores better, while ActiveCampaign is superior if you need CRM and advanced automation alongside email marketing.

Is there a cheaper alternative to MailerLite?

Brevo is cheaper, with a free tier supporting up to 100,000 subscribers and paid plans from just $25. Mailchimp and GetResponse also offer competitive free tiers, though they have lower subscriber limits. However, Brevo provides the best value for cost-conscious businesses that need more than just email marketing.
