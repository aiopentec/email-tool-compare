7 Best Sendinblue Alternatives in 2025

While Sendinblue (now Brevo) offers a solid all-in-one CRM suite, you might be looking for alternatives that better suit your specific needs, budget, or use case. Whether you need better creator tools, more affordable pricing, or specialized e-commerce features, there are several excellent email marketing platforms worth considering.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Find a platform with better pricing for your subscriber count or feature set
  • Access specialized tools designed for your industry (creators, e-commerce, courses)
  • Get improved customer support and user experience ratings

Best Sendinblue Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: Small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite offers one of the most affordable entry points at just $9/month while supporting 1,000 free subscribers, making it ideal if you want professional features without the cost. Its intuitive drag-and-drop editor and strong Trustpilot rating (4.5) indicate reliable service and user satisfaction.

2
ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: Creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit is purpose-built for creators with visual automation workflows, subscriber tagging, and integrated commerce capabilities that make selling digital products seamless. Unlike Sendinblue's general-purpose approach, ConvertKit's features are specifically designed around creator workflows.

3
Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: Newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv uniquely combines email marketing with built-in monetization features, an ad network, and referral programs, making it perfect for growing newsletter businesses. It offers the highest free tier (2,500 subscribers) and excellent ratings (4.7 G2, 4.4 Trustpilot).

4
Mailchimp
The all-in-one marketing platform  ·  Best for: Beginners and small businesses
Mailchimp offers a completely free tier with up to 500 subscribers and strong automation and segmentation features, making it ideal if you want zero-cost entry. Its landing pages and A/B testing capabilities provide advanced marketing features even on the free plan.

5
ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: Businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign combines email, advanced automation, and CRM in a single platform starting at just $15/month, matching Sendinblue's price point but with more sophisticated automation workflows. It includes lead scoring and site tracking that give you deeper customer insights.

6
Klaviyo
The platform for intelligent marketing automation  ·  Best for: E-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce
Klaviyo is purpose-built for e-commerce with deep integrations, predictive analytics, and SMS capabilities tailored to online stores, making it superior to Sendinblue for Shopify merchants. Its customer reviews feature and flow automation are optimized for product-based businesses.

7
GetResponse
Complete online marketing platform  ·  Best for: Online course creators and webinar hosts
GetResponse stands out with built-in webinar hosting, online course creation, and marketing funnel tools, offering more value than Sendinblue if you conduct webinars or sell courses. At $19/month, it provides better all-in-one functionality for educational entrepreneurs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Sendinblue?

Beehiiv offers the most generous free tier with 2,500 subscribers and newsletter monetization features, though it's more specialized for publishers. If you need a general-purpose platform, Mailchimp remains an excellent free option with 500 subscribers, full automation, and landing pages included.

What is the best Sendinblue alternative for small business?

MailerLite is ideal for budget-conscious small businesses, offering professional features at just $9/month with support for 1,000 free subscribers. For slightly more advanced CRM needs, ActiveCampaign at $15/month provides better automation and lead scoring while maintaining affordability.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Sendinblue?

Yes, MailerLite starts at $9/month (half Sendinblue's $25 entry price) with comparable features, and both Mailchimp and GetResponse offer paid plans starting at $13 and $19 respectively. For completely free options, Mailchimp and Beehiiv provide substantial free tiers with professional capabilities.
