Best Sendinblue Alternatives for Email Marketing & CRM

Whether you're looking for better pricing, more advanced features, or a platform tailored to your specific industry, there are several excellent alternatives to Sendinblue available. We've compared the top options to help you find the right email marketing solution for your business needs.

Why look for an alternative?
  • More affordable pricing for your subscriber count or feature needs
  • Industry-specific features (e-commerce, newsletters, webinars, courses)
  • Better user experience or customer support ratings
  • Advanced automation capabilities or superior CRM integration

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Sendinblue?

MailerLite and Mailchimp both offer strong free tiers with unlimited campaigns. MailerLite provides up to 1,000 contacts free with no daily send limits, while Mailchimp offers 500 contacts with more advanced automation features. For pure value, MailerLite's unlimited monthly sends make it the better free option.

What is the best Sendinblue alternative for small business?

MailerLite is ideal for small businesses due to its affordable pricing ($9/month), user-friendly interface, and comprehensive feature set including automation, landing pages, and pop-ups. ActiveCampaign is a better choice if you need advanced CRM and lead scoring for serious sales operations.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Sendinblue?

Yes, MailerLite at $9/month and GetResponse at $19/month both undercut Sendinblue's $25/month entry price. For the absolute lowest cost with powerful features, MailerLite offers the best value, while still providing automation and landing pages comparable to Sendinblue.
