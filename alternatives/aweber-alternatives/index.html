7 Best AWeber Alternatives in 2025

While AWeber remains a solid choice for beginners, you might be looking for alternatives that offer better pricing, more advanced features, or specialized tools for your specific use case. Whether you're a content creator, e-commerce store owner, or small business looking to scale, there's likely a better-suited email marketing platform for your needs.

Why look for an alternative?
  • More generous free tiers with higher subscriber limits
  • Better feature sets for specialized use cases like e-commerce or newsletters
  • Superior customer ratings and user satisfaction scores
  • More competitive pricing, especially as your list grows

Best AWeber Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite undercuts AWeber on price at just $9/month and offers 1,000 free subscribers instead of 500, making it ideal if you're budget-conscious. With a 4.5 Trustpilot rating, it consistently outperforms AWeber on customer satisfaction while providing drag-and-drop simplicity.

2
ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit is purpose-built for content creators and bloggers with visual automation workflows and 1,000 free subscribers. Its subscriber tagging and broadcast capabilities are better suited to creators than AWeber's more generic feature set.

3
Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv offers 2,500 free subscribers and built-in monetization features like an ad network and referral program, making it perfect for newsletter publishers. Its 4.7 G2 rating and 4.4 Trustpilot score reflect superior user satisfaction compared to AWeber.

4
Mailchimp
The all-in-one marketing platform  ·  Best for: beginners and small businesses
Mailchimp offers 500 free subscribers like AWeber but with more robust automation, A/B testing, and audience segmentation features. Its 4.3 G2 rating reflects strong performance, though it's better suited to businesses wanting an all-in-one marketing suite.

5
ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with a full CRM, lead scoring, and dynamic content capabilities, making it ideal for businesses outgrowing simple email tools. Its advanced automation features justify the $15 starting price for teams that need sophisticated workflows.

6
Brevo
All-in-one CRM suite  ·  Best for: transactional email and multi-channel marketing
Brevo's free tier supports 100,000 subscribers—vastly more than AWeber's 500—and includes SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat alongside email. This makes it the best alternative if you need multi-channel communication without paying per subscriber.

7
GetResponse
Complete online marketing platform  ·  Best for: online course creators and webinar hosts
GetResponse combines email marketing with webinar hosting, online courses, and conversion funnels, making it perfect for educators and course sellers. Its integrated approach eliminates the need for multiple tools and offers better value than AWeber for creators.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to AWeber?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with 100,000 subscribers at no cost, compared to AWeber's 500. If you need a more traditional email marketing experience with similar subscriber limits, Beehiiv (2,500 free) and MailerLite (1,000 free) are excellent alternatives that also offer superior customer ratings.

What is the best AWeber alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the top choice for small businesses, offering the lowest paid pricing at $9/month with excellent simplicity and support. For businesses needing CRM and advanced automation, ActiveCampaign provides more sophisticated tools at a similar price point, though it requires a paid plan from the start.

Is there a cheaper alternative to AWeber?

Yes—MailerLite starts at $9/month versus AWeber's $15, while Mailchimp and GetResponse begin at $13 and $19 respectively. If you want free entirely, Brevo's free tier is the most substantial, supporting up to 100,000 subscribers with email campaigns, SMS, and WhatsApp included.
