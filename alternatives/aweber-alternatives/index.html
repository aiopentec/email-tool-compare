7 Best AWeber Alternatives in 2025

While AWeber remains a solid choice for beginners, several newer platforms offer more modern interfaces, better automation, and superior customer support. Whether you're outgrowing AWeber's feature set or looking for a better pricing structure, these alternatives deliver comparable or superior functionality at competitive rates.

Why look for an alternative?
  • More intuitive drag-and-drop editors and visual automation builders
  • Better customer support ratings and more active community engagement
  • Enhanced features like SMS, landing pages, and e-commerce integrations included at lower price points

Best AWeber Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite offers the lowest entry price at just $9/month while providing 1,000 free subscribers compared to AWeber's 500-limit free tier. It includes drag-and-drop editing, automation, landing pages, and popups—giving you more features for less money.

2
ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit allows 1,000 free subscribers and emphasizes creator-focused features like subscriber tagging, landing pages, and built-in commerce. It's specifically designed for bloggers and content creators who want a platform that understands their unique needs.

3
Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv boasts the highest G2 rating (4.7) and includes unique monetization features like an ad network and referral program. With 2,500 free subscribers, it's ideal if you want to turn your newsletter into a revenue-generating asset.

4
Mailchimp
The all-in-one marketing platform  ·  Best for: beginners and small businesses
Mailchimp is the most recognizable alternative with a slightly lower starting price of $13/month and advanced features like A/B testing and audience segmentation. Its massive user base means extensive documentation and community support, though recent reviews show mixed customer satisfaction.

5
ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign integrates CRM functionality directly into its platform with lead scoring and dynamic content capabilities. If you need powerful automation combined with customer relationship management, ActiveCampaign's comprehensive feature set justifies its $15/month starting price.

6
Brevo
All-in-one CRM suite  ·  Best for: transactional email and multi-channel marketing
Brevo stands out with an exceptionally generous free tier supporting up to 100,000 contacts and 300 emails per day. It's the best choice if you need transactional email capabilities, SMS, WhatsApp integration, or multi-channel marketing beyond traditional email.

7
GetResponse
Complete online marketing platform  ·  Best for: online course creators and webinar hosts
GetResponse is the only alternative that bundles webinars, online courses, and conversion funnels alongside email marketing. If you're building an online education or service business, GetResponse's integrated approach eliminates the need for multiple platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to AWeber?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with 100,000 contacts and 300 daily emails, making it ideal if you want unlimited growth without paying. However, if you prefer a more traditional email marketing experience similar to AWeber, both MailerLite and ConvertKit offer 1,000 free subscribers with full access to core features.

What is the best AWeber alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the best choice for cost-conscious small businesses, starting at just $9/month with robust features. For slightly more advanced needs, Mailchimp and ActiveCampaign both offer excellent automation and customer support tailored to growing small businesses.

Is there a cheaper alternative to AWeber?

Yes—MailerLite starts at $9/month compared to AWeber's $15/month, and both Brevo and Beehiiv offer free plans with significantly higher contact limits. If you're willing to start free, Brevo's unlimited free tier is unmatched in the industry.
