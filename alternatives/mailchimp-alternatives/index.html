Best Mailchimp Alternatives for Email Marketing in 2025

While Mailchimp is a popular choice for beginners, its recent pricing changes and mixed customer reviews have left many users searching for better options. Whether you need more advanced automation, better customer support, or a platform tailored to your industry, there are several excellent alternatives that offer superior features at competitive prices.

Why look for a Mailchimp alternative?

  • Recent pricing increases and policy changes have made Mailchimp less attractive for growing businesses
  • Many alternatives offer better customer support ratings and more intuitive user experiences
  • Specialized platforms often provide superior features for specific use cases like e-commerce, newsletters, or courses

Best Mailchimp alternatives

MailerLite

Simple email marketing for small businesses

Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget

MailerLite offers similar core features to Mailchimp but at lower starting prices ($9/month vs $13) and includes 1,000 free subscribers compared to Mailchimp's 500. Its 4.5 Trustpilot rating significantly outperforms Mailchimp's 1.5, reflecting superior customer satisfaction.

Beehiiv

The newsletter platform built for growth

Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators

Beehiiv is purpose-built for newsletter creators with native monetization features, an advertising network, and referral programs built in. With 2,500 free subscribers and an exceptional 4.4 Trustpilot rating, it's ideal if you're focused on growing and monetizing a newsletter audience.

Compare Mailchimp vs Beehiiv

ConvertKit

Email platform built for creators

Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers

ConvertKit specializes in serving digital creators with intuitive visual automations, commerce integrations, and subscriber tagging tailored to course and content sales. Its free tier supports 1,000 subscribers and includes landing pages, making it more creator-friendly than Mailchimp.

Compare Mailchimp vs ConvertKit

ActiveCampaign

Email, automation, and CRM combined

Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM

ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with comprehensive CRM, lead scoring, and advanced automation capabilities that far exceed Mailchimp's offerings. If you need enterprise-level features like site tracking and dynamic content personalization, ActiveCampaign is the superior choice.

Compare Mailchimp vs ActiveCampaign

Brevo

All-in-one CRM suite

Best for: transactional email and multi-channel marketing

Brevo stands out with an incredibly generous free tier allowing 100,000 subscribers and includes multi-channel capabilities (SMS, WhatsApp, live chat) that Mailchimp doesn't offer. It's particularly strong for businesses requiring transactional email APIs and omnichannel communication.

Compare Mailchimp vs Brevo

GetResponse

Complete online marketing platform

Best for: online course creators and webinar hosts

GetResponse uniquely combines email marketing with webinar hosting, online courses, and conversion funnels in one platform, making it ideal for educators and course creators. Its integrated approach eliminates the need for multiple tools that Mailchimp users typically require.

Compare Mailchimp vs GetResponse

Klaviyo

The platform for intelligent marketing automation

Best for: e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce

Klaviyo is purpose-built for e-commerce with deep integrations, predictive analytics, SMS capabilities, and review management tailored to online stores. If you're running an e-commerce business, Klaviyo's specialized features significantly outperform Mailchimp's general approach.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free alternative to Mailchimp?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with 100,000 subscribers and includes multi-channel features like SMS and WhatsApp. For newsletter-focused users, Beehiiv provides 2,500 free subscribers with monetization capabilities, while MailerLite and ConvertKit each offer 1,000 free subscribers with intuitive interfaces.

What is the best Mailchimp alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the best overall alternative for small businesses, offering nearly identical features to Mailchimp at lower prices ($9/month starting), a free tier with 1,000 subscribers, and significantly better customer support ratings. It provides the easiest migration path from Mailchimp with comparable functionality.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Mailchimp?

Yes, MailerLite is more affordable at $9/month compared to Mailchimp's $13/month, while Brevo offers a free tier supporting up to 100,000 subscribers. GetResponse starts at $19/month but includes webinar hosting, making it better value if you need that functionality alongside email marketing.
