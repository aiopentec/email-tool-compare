Best Mailchimp Alternatives in 2025

While Mailchimp remains popular, many users seek alternatives due to pricing changes, limited automation features, or better platform-specific solutions. Whether you're a creator, e-commerce brand, or small business, several competitors offer superior features, better customer support, and more transparent pricing.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Mailchimp's free tier limitations and recent pricing increases make alternatives more cost-effective
  • Specialized platforms offer better features for specific use cases like e-commerce, newsletters, or course creation
  • Many alternatives have significantly higher customer satisfaction ratings and better support

Best Mailchimp Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite offers the best value for money with a free tier supporting up to 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at just $9/month—significantly cheaper than Mailchimp. It includes all essential features like drag-drop editing, automation, and landing pages without the limitations of Mailchimp's free plan.

Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv is purpose-built for newsletter creators with built-in monetization, ad networks, and referral programs that Mailchimp lacks. With a free tier supporting 2,500 subscribers and outstanding 4.4 Trustpilot rating, it's ideal for writers looking to grow and monetize their audience.

ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit specializes in creator economics with native support for digital products, commerce integrations, and visual automations tailored for online businesses. ActiveCampaign

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Mailchimp?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with support for up to 100,000 contacts, compared to Mailchimp's 500-contact limit. For newsletter-focused creators, Beehiiv provides 2,500 free subscribers with built-in monetization features. Both platforms offer essential marketing tools without requiring an immediate upgrade.

What is the best Mailchimp alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the best overall alternative for small businesses, combining affordability (starting at $9/month), a generous free tier (1,000 subscribers), and all the essential features small businesses need. If you're looking for a more advanced solution, Brevo offers excellent value with its massive free contact limit and multi-channel capabilities.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Mailchimp?

Yes, MailerLite starts at just $9/month (cheaper than Mailchimp's $13) and offers a free plan with 1,000 subscribers. Brevo also provides a free tier with unlimited contacts, making it completely free for businesses under 100,000 contacts. Both options provide better value than Mailchimp's pricing structure.
