7 Best Klaviyo Alternatives in 2025

While Klaviyo excels for e-commerce automation, it may not be the best fit for every business—whether due to pricing, feature set, or specific use case requirements. We've compiled the top alternatives that offer competitive features, better pricing, or specialized capabilities for different types of businesses.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Lower cost for basic email marketing needs without sacrificing core features
  • Better fit for specific niches like newsletters, creators, or small businesses
  • More generous free tiers with higher subscriber limits

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Klaviyo?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with 100,000 subscribers, while Mailchimp and MailerLite provide 500 and 1,000 free subscribers respectively with full automation features. If you need advanced e-commerce features specifically, you'll need to move to a paid plan, but these three offer excellent free starting points.

What is the best Klaviyo alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the top choice for small businesses due to its $9/month starting price, intuitive drag-and-drop editor, and robust automation capabilities. Mailchimp is another excellent option if you prefer an even simpler interface and want to start completely free.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Klaviyo?

Yes, MailerLite starts at just $9/month compared to Klaviyo's $45/month, and both Mailchimp ($13/month) and GetResponse ($19/month) are significantly cheaper. All three offer free tiers and strong automation features, though they may lack some of Klaviyo's advanced e-commerce-specific analytics.
