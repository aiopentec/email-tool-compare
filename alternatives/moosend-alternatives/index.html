Best Moosend Alternatives: Compare Top Email Marketing Tools

While Moosend offers excellent value with unlimited emails and a generous free tier, you might be looking for alternatives that specialize in specific features, industries, or use cases. Whether you need advanced CRM capabilities, better creator-focused tools, or superior customer support, there's a perfect email marketing platform for your needs.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Specialized tools designed for your industry (e-commerce, creators, newsletters)
  • Advanced features like predictive analytics, SMS, or integrated CRM
  • Better customer support and platform reliability ratings

Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: Small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite matches Moosend's $9 starting price with a cleaner drag-drop editor and includes pop-ups and surveys out of the box. It has slightly better Trustpilot ratings (4.5 vs 4.4) and an equally generous free tier supporting 1,000 subscribers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Moosend?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with up to 100,000 subscribers and includes email, SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat. If you prefer a dedicated email platform, MailerLite and ConvertKit both offer free tiers for 1,000 subscribers with excellent feature sets comparable to Moosend's free plan.

What is the best Moosend alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the strongest alternative for small businesses, matching Moosend's $9 starting price while offering better overall ratings and a polished user experience. It includes landing pages, pop-ups, and automation workflows that small businesses need to grow without complexity.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Moosend?

Brevo and Mailchimp both offer completely free plans with no time limits, making them technically cheaper if you stay within their free tiers. For paid plans, MailerLite matches Moosend at $9/month, while Brevo's paid plans start at $25/month with significantly more features like SMS and WhatsApp.
