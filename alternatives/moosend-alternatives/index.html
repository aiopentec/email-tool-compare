Best Moosend Alternatives in 2025

While Moosend offers solid email marketing at a low cost, you might be looking for alternatives that better fit your specific needs—whether that's advanced automation, specialized features for creators, or multi-channel capabilities. We've compared the top alternatives to help you find the right platform for your business.

Why look for a Moosend alternative?

  • Need specialized features like webinars, SMS, or e-commerce integrations not available in Moosend
  • Looking for better customer support or improved user experience with a more intuitive interface
  • Require advanced automation, CRM functionality, or analytics tailored to your industry

Best Moosend alternatives

MailerLite

Simple email marketing for small businesses

Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget

MailerLite matches Moosend's $9 starting price with the same 1,000 free subscriber limit, but offers a more intuitive drag-and-drop editor and better customer support. It's the closest direct competitor if you want similar pricing without sacrificing usability.

ConvertKit

Email platform built for creators

Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers

ConvertKit is purpose-built for content creators with visual automation workflows and subscriber tagging that exceed Moosend's capabilities. If you're monetizing your audience through digital products or memberships, ConvertKit's creator-focused features provide better value despite the higher $29 starting price.

Beehiiv

The newsletter platform built for growth

Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators

Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers with built-in monetization tools, an ad network, and referral programs that Moosend doesn't offer. Its more generous 2,500 free subscriber limit makes it an excellent choice if you're serious about building a profitable newsletter business.

Compare Moosend vs Beehiiv

Brevo

All-in-one CRM suite

Best for: transactional email and multi-channel marketing

Brevo stands out with an exceptionally generous free tier (100,000 subscribers) plus multi-channel capabilities including SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat. It's ideal if you need to communicate across multiple channels or send high volumes of transactional emails.

Compare Moosend vs Brevo

ActiveCampaign

Email, automation, and CRM combined

Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM

ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with a full CRM, lead scoring, and advanced automation workflows that far exceed Moosend's capabilities. While it starts at $15 without a free tier, it's the right choice if you need enterprise-grade marketing automation and sales integration.

Mailchimp

The all-in-one marketing platform

Best for: beginners and small businesses

Mailchimp is the most established option with the broadest feature set, though its smaller free tier (500 subscribers) and higher starting price ($13) may offset the value for budget-conscious users. It's best if you value brand recognition and want to grow into an integrated marketing platform.

Compare Moosend vs Mailchimp

GetResponse

Complete online marketing platform

Best for: online course creators and webinar hosts

GetResponse uniquely bundles email marketing with webinars, online courses, and conversion funnels at a $19 starting price. It's the ideal alternative if you need webinar hosting or course delivery functionality alongside your email campaigns.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free alternative to Moosend?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with 100,000 subscribers at no cost, plus multi-channel features like SMS and WhatsApp. If you want a closer Moosend equivalent with 1,000 free subscribers, both MailerLite and ConvertKit match that limit while offering more polished user interfaces.

What is the best Moosend alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the top choice for small businesses, offering identical pricing ($9/month starting), the same 1,000 free subscriber limit, and comparable features with better ease of use. For slightly more advanced needs, ActiveCampaign provides superior automation and CRM capabilities starting at $15/month.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Moosend?

Brevo is significantly cheaper, offering unlimited free emails to 100,000 subscribers—far exceeding Moosend's free tier. MailerLite matches Moosend's $9 starting price exactly, making it equally affordable with better features. If you need the absolute cheapest option, Brevo's free plan is unbeatable.
