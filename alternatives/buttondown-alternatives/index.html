Best Buttondown Alternatives: Top Email Marketing Tools for 2025

While Buttondown excels for developers and markdown enthusiasts, you might need an alternative if you require more advanced features, better visual editing, or specific integrations. Whether you're a creator, small business owner, or e-commerce brand, these alternatives offer robust email marketing capabilities tailored to different needs.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Need visual email builders instead of markdown-only editing
  • Require advanced automation, CRM, or e-commerce integrations
  • Want higher free tier limits or different pricing structures

Best Buttondown Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: Small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite offers the same affordable $9 starting price as Buttondown but with a much more generous free tier of 1,000 subscribers (vs. 100) and an intuitive drag-drop editor alongside automation. If you need visual email design without paying premium prices, MailerLite is the most direct competitor.

2
ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: Creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit is purpose-built for content creators with features like visual automations, subscriber tagging, and integrated commerce capabilities. It allows up to 1,000 free subscribers and excels at managing creator monetization, making it ideal if you're building an audience and want to sell products or courses.
Buttondown vs ConvertKit →
3
Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: Newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv is specifically designed for newsletter growth with built-in monetization, an ad network, and referral programs that Buttondown lacks. With 2,500 free subscribers and strong analytics focused on newsletter metrics, it's the best choice for publishers prioritizing growth and revenue.
Buttondown vs Beehiiv →
4
Mailchimp
The all-in-one marketing platform  ·  Best for: Beginners and small businesses
Mailchimp provides a comprehensive marketing suite with landing pages, A/B testing, and advanced segmentation beyond basic email sending. While it started with 500 free subscribers, it's more feature-rich for growing businesses that need multi-channel marketing capabilities.
Buttondown vs Mailchimp →
5
ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: Businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with a full CRM, lead scoring, and advanced automation workflows that go far beyond Buttondown's capabilities. If you need to manage sales processes alongside email marketing, ActiveCampaign's integrated approach justifies its $15 minimum investment.
Buttondown vs ActiveCampaign →
6
Klaviyo
The platform for intelligent marketing automation  ·  Best for: E-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce
Klaviyo specializes in e-commerce with deep integrations for Shopify and WooCommerce, plus predictive analytics and intelligent flows that Buttondown doesn't offer. If your business sells products online, Klaviyo's e-commerce focus and advanced segmentation make it worth the higher $45 starting price.
Buttondown vs Klaviyo →
7
Brevo
All-in-one CRM suite  ·  Best for: Transactional email and multi-channel marketing
Brevo stands out with an exceptionally generous free tier of 100,000 subscribers and includes multi-channel tools like SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat alongside email. For businesses needing transactional email APIs and integrated communications, Brevo offers unmatched value at the free level.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Buttondown?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with 100,000 subscribers and multi-channel marketing (SMS, WhatsApp). If you want something closer to Buttondown's newsletter focus, Beehiiv provides 2,500 free subscribers with growth-focused features, while MailerLite offers 1,000 free subscribers with a better visual editor.

What is the best Buttondown alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the best choice for small businesses, offering the same affordable $9 starting price as Buttondown but with 1,000 free subscribers, drag-drop email building, automation, and landing pages. It strikes the perfect balance between simplicity and functionality without breaking the budget.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Buttondown?

Yes, several alternatives match or beat Buttondown's $9 starting price: MailerLite at $9, GetResponse at $19, and Brevo at $25. However, for free options with better free tiers, Brevo (100K subscribers), Beehiiv (2,500), ConvertKit (1,000), and MailerLite (1,000) all offer more generous free plans than Buttondown's 100-subscriber limit.
