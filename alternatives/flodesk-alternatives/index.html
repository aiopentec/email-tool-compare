Best Flodesk Alternatives: 7 Top Email Marketing Tools

While Flodesk excels at beautiful email design with unlimited subscribers at a flat rate, you may be searching for alternatives if you want free options, more advanced automation, or specialized features for your business type. Whether you're looking to reduce costs, access more integrations, or find tools tailored to creators, e-commerce, or newsletters, there are several compelling options available.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Access free tiers or lower starting prices to test before committing
  • Get advanced automation, CRM, or SMS capabilities beyond email design
  • Find industry-specific tools optimized for creators, e-commerce, or newsletters

Best Flodesk Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite offers a free tier with 1,000 subscribers and starts at just $9/month, making it significantly cheaper than Flodesk while still providing a drag-and-drop editor and automation. It's the ideal choice if budget is your primary concern but you still want professional email marketing capabilities.

2
ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit is purpose-built for content creators with features like landing pages, commerce functionality, and visual automations, plus a free tier supporting up to 1,000 subscribers. If you're a creator selling digital products or building an audience, ConvertKit's creator-focused approach outshines Flodesk's design-heavy platform.

3
Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv uniquely combines email marketing with newsletter monetization through an ad network and referral program, plus it offers a free tier with 2,500 subscribers. For newsletter publishers wanting to generate revenue from their audience, Beehiiv provides built-in tools Flodesk simply doesn't offer.

4
ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with a full CRM, advanced automation workflows, lead scoring, and SMS capabilities starting at just $15/month. If you need sophisticated customer relationship management and complex automation sequences beyond what Flodesk offers, ActiveCampaign is a more robust solution.

5
Mailchimp
The all-in-one marketing platform  ·  Best for: beginners and small businesses
Mailchimp is the most established email marketing platform with a generous free tier (up to 500 subscribers), plus landing pages, A/B testing, and audience segmentation. For beginners wanting to start completely free with proven, reliable tools and extensive integrations, Mailchimp remains a solid choice despite Flodesk's superior design templates.

6
Klaviyo
The platform for intelligent marketing automation  ·  Best for: e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce
Klaviyo specializes in e-commerce with deep Shopify and WooCommerce integrations, predictive analytics, SMS flows, and review management specifically designed for online stores. If you're an e-commerce brand, Klaviyo's commerce-focused features and automation far exceed what a design-centric tool like Flodesk can provide.

7
GetResponse
Complete online marketing platform  ·  Best for: online course creators and webinar hosts
GetResponse includes webinar hosting, online course creation, and landing page builders alongside email marketing, making it ideal for creators selling digital products. If you need an all-in-one platform for courses and webinars beyond just email design, GetResponse eliminates the need for multiple tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Flodesk?

MailerLite is the best free alternative, offering a free tier with up to 1,000 subscribers, a drag-and-drop editor, automation, and landing pages. If you need more generous free subscriber limits, Beehiiv and Brevo both allow significantly more free contacts, though Beehiiv focuses on newsletters while Brevo emphasizes multi-channel marketing.

What is the best Flodesk alternative for small business?

MailerLite is ideal for small businesses due to its low starting price ($9/month), free tier, and essential features like automation and landing pages. For small businesses that also need CRM capabilities, ActiveCampaign offers better automation and customer management at a comparable price point.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Flodesk?

Yes, MailerLite starts at $9/month (compared to Flodesk's $38/month) and offers a free tier with 1,000 subscribers. GetResponse also starts at $19/month and includes webinars and courses, while Brevo begins at $25/month with SMS and WhatsApp included.
