7 Best Flodesk Alternatives in 2025

While Flodesk excels at beautiful email design with flat-rate pricing, it may not suit every business's needs or budget. Whether you're looking for a free tier, advanced automation, e-commerce integrations, or lower costs, there are several excellent alternatives worth considering. We've compared the top options to help you find the perfect fit for your email marketing strategy.

Why look for a Flodesk alternative?

  • You need a free tier or freemium pricing model to get started
  • You require advanced automation, CRM features, or e-commerce integrations
  • You want to pay per subscriber instead of a flat monthly fee

Best Flodesk alternatives

ConvertKit

Email platform built for creators

Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers

ConvertKit offers a generous free tier supporting up to 1,000 subscribers and is purpose-built for content creators with visual automation workflows and integrated landing pages. It's the ideal choice if you want creator-focused features without Flodesk's commitment to paid plans.

Beehiiv

The newsletter platform built for growth

Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators

Beehiiv stands out with built-in monetization options, an ad network, and referral programs—features essential for newsletter publishers looking to generate revenue. With a free tier supporting 2,500 subscribers, it's perfect for growing your audience before scaling.

Compare Flodesk vs Beehiiv

MailerLite

Simple email marketing for small businesses

Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget

MailerLite combines an intuitive drag-and-drop editor with an unbeatable price point starting at just $9 per month and a free tier for 1,000 subscribers. It's the most budget-friendly option without sacrificing essential features like automation and landing pages.

Compare Flodesk vs MailerLite

ActiveCampaign

Email, automation, and CRM combined

Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM

ActiveCampaign integrates email marketing with a powerful CRM, advanced lead scoring, and dynamic content capabilities starting at $15/month. Choose this if you need sophisticated automation workflows and customer relationship management beyond email alone.

Klaviyo

The platform for intelligent marketing automation

Best for: e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce

Klaviyo specializes in e-commerce with predictive analytics, SMS integration, and native Shopify/WooCommerce connections that Flodesk doesn't offer. It's essential if your business relies on online sales and customer behavior-driven email campaigns.

Compare Flodesk vs Klaviyo

Mailchimp

The all-in-one marketing platform

Best for: beginners and small businesses

Mailchimp offers one of the most accessible free tiers with support for 500 contacts and includes A/B testing, audience segmentation, and landing pages. It's ideal for businesses just starting with email marketing who want to test features risk-free.

Compare Flodesk vs Mailchimp

GetResponse

Complete online marketing platform

Best for: online course creators and webinar hosts

GetResponse uniquely combines email marketing with native webinar hosting, online course creation, and conversion funnels—a complete ecosystem for educational entrepreneurs. It's the best all-in-one solution if you need more than just email tools.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free alternative to Flodesk?

ConvertKit and Beehiiv offer the most generous free tiers, supporting 1,000 and 2,500 subscribers respectively. ConvertKit is best for creators, while Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers. Both provide powerful features without requiring payment until you're ready to scale.

What is the best Flodesk alternative for small business?

MailerLite and Mailchimp are the top choices for small businesses, offering affordability and ease of use. MailerLite starts at $9/month with a free tier, while Mailchimp's free plan includes automation and A/B testing—making both accessible for businesses on tight budgets.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Flodesk?

Yes, MailerLite starts at just $9/month compared to Flodesk's $38/month flat rate, making it significantly cheaper. Many alternatives also offer free tiers, including Mailchimp, ConvertKit, and Beehiiv, so you can test features before committing to paid plans.
