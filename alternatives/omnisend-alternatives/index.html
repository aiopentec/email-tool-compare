Best Omnisend Alternatives: Top Email & SMS Marketing Tools

While Omnisend excels as an omnichannel platform for e-commerce, you might be exploring alternatives due to pricing, specific feature needs, or integration requirements. Whether you're looking for more advanced automation, better free tier limits, or industry-specific functionality, there are several compelling options available.

Why look for a Omnisend alternative?

  • Need SMS and push notifications without omnichannel complexity or at different price points
  • Require industry-specific features like CRM integration, webinar hosting, or newsletter monetization
  • Want higher free tier subscriber limits or lower starting prices for your budget

Best Omnisend alternatives

MailerLite

Simple email marketing for small businesses

Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget

MailerLite offers the lowest starting price at just $9/month with a generous 1,000 free subscriber limit, making it ideal for budget-conscious e-commerce stores. It includes essential features like automation, landing pages, and e-commerce integrations without the complexity of a full omnichannel platform.

ActiveCampaign

Email, automation, and CRM combined

Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM

ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with powerful CRM and lead scoring capabilities, making it the best choice for businesses that need deeper customer relationship management alongside SMS. Its advanced automation and site tracking features provide more sophisticated marketing capabilities than Omnisend.

Compare Omnisend vs ActiveCampaign

Klaviyo

The platform for intelligent marketing automation

Best for: e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce

Klaviyo is purpose-built for e-commerce with superior Shopify and WooCommerce integrations, predictive analytics, and sophisticated segmentation that rivals Omnisend's capabilities. If you're deeply invested in these platforms and want industry-leading email and SMS performance metrics, Klaviyo is the most specialized alternative.

Compare Omnisend vs Klaviyo

Brevo

All-in-one CRM suite

Best for: transactional email and multi-channel marketing

Brevo stands out with an exceptional free tier supporting 100,000 subscribers and includes WhatsApp and live chat alongside email and SMS. It's the best omnichannel alternative to Omnisend if you need more communication channels and don't require complex e-commerce-specific features.

Compare Omnisend vs Brevo

ConvertKit

Email platform built for creators

Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers

ConvertKit specializes in digital product delivery with built-in commerce features, subscriber tagging, and visual automations designed for creators. If you sell courses or digital products alongside physical goods, ConvertKit's creator-focused approach provides better alignment than Omnisend's e-commerce orientation.

Compare Omnisend vs ConvertKit

Beehiiv

The newsletter platform built for growth

Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators

Beehiiv excels in newsletter monetization with built-in ad networks and referral programs, making it the clear winner for publishers and media companies. While not traditional e-commerce focused, it's ideal if you're combining product sales with newsletter revenue.

Compare Omnisend vs Beehiiv

GetResponse

Complete online marketing platform

Best for: online course creators and webinar hosts

GetResponse uniquely combines email marketing with robust webinar hosting and online course capabilities, making it superior for businesses that need to deliver educational content. It's the best alternative if your e-commerce business includes webinars or digital courses.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free alternative to Omnisend?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with support for up to 100,000 subscribers, compared to Omnisend's 250-subscriber limit. It includes email, SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat, making it a comprehensive omnichannel alternative at no cost. If you need lower subscriber limits, MailerLite and ConvertKit both offer 1,000 free subscribers.

What is the best Omnisend alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the top choice for small businesses due to its ultra-affordable $9 starting price, 1,000 free subscribers, and essential automation and e-commerce features. It delivers professional marketing capabilities without unnecessary complexity, making it perfect for bootstrapped entrepreneurs and small teams.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Omnisend?

Yes, MailerLite starts at just $9/month, significantly undercutting Omnisend's $16 entry point. Additionally, Brevo's free tier with 100,000 subscribers provides unlimited free usage if your subscriber base doesn't exceed that threshold, making it a cost-effective option for many small e-commerce stores.
