7 Best Omnisend Alternatives in 2025

While Omnisend offers a solid ecommerce email and SMS platform, you might be looking for alternatives that better fit your budget, business model, or specific feature needs. Whether you're seeking more affordable pricing, specialized features for creators, or advanced CRM functionality, there's a platform out there that could be a perfect fit.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Find more affordable pricing tiers that better match your subscriber count and budget
  • Access specialized features tailored to your industry, whether ecommerce, newsletters, or course creation
  • Get better customer support ratings or more intuitive user interfaces for your team

Best Omnisend Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: Budget-conscious businesses seeking affordability and ease of use
MailerLite starts at just $9/month and offers a free tier with up to 1,000 subscribers, making it the most affordable option for small teams. It combines core email features with drag-drop editing, automation, and ecommerce integrations without the complexity of pricier alternatives.

Klaviyo
The platform for intelligent marketing automation  ·  Best for: Ecommerce brands looking for deeper platform integrations and predictive analytics
Klaviyo specializes in ecommerce with native integrations for Shopify and WooCommerce, plus predictive analytics that Omnisend doesn't offer. If your priority is maximizing revenue from existing customers through intelligent segmentation and flows, Klaviyo is the stronger choice.

ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: Businesses needing advanced automation combined with full CRM and lead scoring capabilities
ActiveCampaign goes beyond email with integrated CRM, lead scoring, and advanced automation workflows that rival enterprise platforms. This makes it ideal if you're outgrowing Omnisend and need a unified system for sales and marketing alignment.

Brevo
All-in-one CRM suite  ·  Best for: Businesses wanting omnichannel marketing including WhatsApp, live chat, and transactional email
Brevo uniquely offers WhatsApp marketing alongside email and SMS, plus an industry-leading free tier with 100,000 contacts. It's the best choice if you need broader communication channels beyond traditional email and SMS.

ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: Content creators, bloggers, and course sellers seeking creator-focused features
ConvertKit is purpose-built for creators with built-in commerce, subscriber tagging, and visual automation designed around content monetization. If you're selling courses or digital products, ConvertKit's creator-centric approach beats Omnisend's ecommerce focus.

Beehiiv

Mailchimp

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Omnisend?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with support for 100,000 contacts and includes email, SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat. If you need fewer features but prefer a simpler interface, MailerLite and ConvertKit both offer free tiers with up to 1,000 subscribers, making them excellent budget-friendly alternatives.

What is the best Omnisend alternative for small business?

MailerLite is ideal for small businesses due to its $9/month starting price and intuitive drag-drop builder. For slightly larger teams needing CRM alongside email, ActiveCampaign at $15/month offers advanced automation and lead scoring that grows with your business.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Omnisend?

Yes, MailerLite starts at $9/month compared to Omnisend's $16/month minimum. Both offer free tiers, but MailerLite provides 1,000 free subscribers versus Omnisend's 250, making it the more cost-effective choice for growing businesses.
