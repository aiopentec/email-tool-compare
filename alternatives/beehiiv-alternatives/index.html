The 7 Best Beehiiv Alternatives for Newsletter Publishers

While Beehiiv excels at newsletter monetization and growth features, it may not be the right fit for every creator or business. Whether you're looking for lower pricing, better creator tools, or advanced automation, several compelling alternatives offer unique strengths. We've compared the top options to help you find the perfect email marketing platform for your needs.

Why look for a Beehiiv alternative?

  • Lower pricing tiers or more generous free plans with higher subscriber limits
  • Better creator-focused features like landing pages, courses, or webinar hosting
  • More advanced automation and CRM capabilities for growing teams

Best Beehiiv alternatives

ConvertKit

Email platform built for creators

Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers

ConvertKit is purpose-built for creators with visual automation workflows, subscriber tagging, and integrated commerce features that rival Beehiiv's monetization focus. It offers comparable free tier limits (1,000 subscribers) and pricing ($29/month) while emphasizing creator-friendly design and community.

MailerLite

Simple email marketing for small businesses

Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget

MailerLite is significantly cheaper than Beehiiv, starting at just $9/month with a 1,000-subscriber free tier, making it ideal for budget-conscious publishers. Its drag-drop editor, automation, and landing pages provide all essential features without the premium price tag.

Compare Beehiiv vs MailerLite

Mailchimp

The all-in-one marketing platform

Best for: beginners and small businesses

Mailchimp is the industry-standard choice for beginners, offering comprehensive features like A/B testing, landing pages, and audience segmentation at competitive pricing starting at $13/month. Its 20+ year track record and extensive integrations make it a reliable alternative for growing publishers.

Compare Beehiiv vs Mailchimp

ActiveCampaign

Email, automation, and CRM combined

Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM

ActiveCampaign is the best choice for publishers with sophisticated marketing needs, offering advanced automation, built-in CRM, lead scoring, and SMS all from $15/month. If you're scaling beyond email and need deeper customer relationship management, ActiveCampaign outperforms Beehiiv's feature set.

Compare Beehiiv vs ActiveCampaign

Brevo

All-in-one CRM suite

Best for: transactional email and multi-channel marketing

Brevo stands out with an exceptionally generous free tier supporting up to 100,000 subscribers and offering multi-channel capabilities including SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat. This makes it excellent for publishers wanting to expand beyond email without immediate costs.

Compare Beehiiv vs Brevo

GetResponse

Complete online marketing platform

Best for: online course creators and webinar hosts

GetResponse is specifically designed for creators monetizing through webinars and courses, offering native webinar hosting, online course tools, and conversion funnels starting at $19/month. It's more specialized than Beehiiv for educational content creators.

Compare Beehiiv vs GetResponse

Substack

Start a paid newsletter

Best for: writers monetising directly from subscribers

Substack eliminates platform fees in favor of a 10% revenue share on paid subscriptions, making it ideal for newsletter writers focused purely on direct reader monetization. Unlike Beehiiv's ad network approach, Substack's model benefits writers with high paid subscription rates.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free alternative to Beehiiv?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with support for up to 100,000 subscribers, compared to Beehiiv's 2,500 limit. If you need a more creator-focused alternative, ConvertKit and MailerLite both offer free tiers supporting 1,000 subscribers with solid email marketing features.

What is the best Beehiiv alternative for small business?

MailerLite and Mailchimp are the top choices for small businesses, offering simple interfaces, affordable pricing starting at $9-13/month, and essential features like automation and landing pages. Both have strong Trustpilot ratings and are proven platforms for growing small business email lists.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Beehiiv?

Yes, MailerLite starts at just $9/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month, making it significantly more affordable for publishers on a budget. Mailchimp ($13/month) and GetResponse ($19/month) also offer more cost-effective plans while maintaining quality email marketing features.
