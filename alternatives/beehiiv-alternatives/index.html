7 Best Beehiiv Alternatives in 2025

While Beehiiv excels at newsletter monetization and growth features, it may not be the perfect fit for every creator or business. Whether you're looking for more affordable pricing, different monetization models, or specialized features for e-commerce or automation, several strong alternatives offer compelling benefits. Here are the top Beehiiv competitors to consider.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Find a platform with lower starting costs or larger free tier subscriber limits
  • Access specialized features like e-commerce integrations, webinars, or multi-channel marketing
  • Discover better monetization options if direct sponsorships aren't your focus

Best Beehiiv Alternatives in 2025

1
ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit is purpose-built for creators with visual automation workflows and strong commerce features for selling digital products. Like Beehiiv, it prioritizes creator needs but emphasizes course and product sales over ad network monetization.

MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite offers the best value with pricing starting at just $9/month and a free tier supporting up to 1,000 subscribers. It includes drag-and-drop automation, landing pages, and pop-ups—making it ideal for budget-conscious creators who don't need specialized monetization.

Substack
Start a paid newsletter  ·  Best for: writers monetising directly from subscribers
Substack offers unlimited subscribers on the free tier and built-in paid subscription infrastructure with a revenue-share model. It's the closest competitor to Beehiiv for newsletter monetization but focuses on reader relationships over ad networks.

ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with full CRM capabilities, advanced lead scoring, and site tracking starting at $15/month. It's ideal for businesses seeking sophisticated automation and customer relationship management beyond Beehiiv's capabilities.

Brevo

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Beehiiv?

Substack and Brevo both offer the most generous free tiers—Substack provides unlimited subscribers with no restrictions, while Brevo allows 100,000 subscribers. For creators focused on monetization, Substack is the better choice, while Brevo suits those needing multi-channel marketing capabilities.

What is the best Beehiiv alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the top choice for small businesses, offering affordable pricing starting at $9/month with automation, landing pages, and pop-ups included. Its combination of simplicity, affordability, and core marketing features makes it ideal for resource-conscious small teams.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Beehiiv?

Yes, MailerLite ($9/month) and GetResponse ($19/month) both undercut Beehiiv's $39 entry price. However, if cost is your primary concern, Substack and Brevo offer free plans with no paid tier requirement, though they target different use cases than Beehiiv's paid offerings.
