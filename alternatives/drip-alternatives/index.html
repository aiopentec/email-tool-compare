Best Alternatives to Drip in 2025

While Drip excels at e-commerce email marketing, it may not be the best fit for every business—particularly those on a tight budget or with different marketing needs. Whether you're looking for more affordable options, free tiers, or specialized features for creators and bloggers, there are several compelling alternatives worth considering.

Why look for an alternative?
  • You need a free or freemium option to minimize upfront costs
  • You require specialized features like webinars, landing pages, or course hosting
  • You want deeper CRM or advanced automation capabilities beyond email

Best Drip Alternatives in 2025

1
Klaviyo
The platform for intelligent marketing automation  ·  Best for: e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce
Klaviyo offers superior e-commerce integration and predictive analytics similar to Drip, making it the ideal choice for Shopify stores seeking advanced segmentation and SMS capabilities. With a free tier supporting 500 contacts, it's perfect for growing e-commerce businesses looking to test before upgrading.

ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with a full CRM suite, offering more advanced automation workflows and lead scoring than Drip at a comparable price point. If you need to manage customer relationships beyond email alone, ActiveCampaign provides greater depth and integration options.

3
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite delivers powerful automation and e-commerce integrations starting at just $9/month, significantly undercutting Drip's $39 entry point. Its free tier supports 1,000 subscribers with full access to automation, making it ideal for growing businesses seeking affordability without sacrificing core features.

ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit specializes in serving creators and online educators with a free plan supporting 1,000 subscribers and built-in landing pages and sequences. If your business focuses on content creation or digital products rather than e-commerce, ConvertKit's creator-first approach offers better alignment than Drip.

Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv uniquely combines email marketing with built-in monetization tools, an ad network, and referral programs—features Drip doesn't offer. It's the clear winner for publishers and newsletter creators seeking to generate revenue directly from their subscriber base.

Brevo
All-in-one CRM suite  ·  Best for: transactional email and multi-channel marketing
Brevo offers an exceptionally generous free tier with unlimited contacts and adds SMS, WhatsApp, live chat, and a full CRM—providing more channels than Drip at no cost. It's the best choice for businesses needing multi-channel marketing and transactional email capabilities beyond standard campaigns.

GetResponse
Complete online marketing platform  ·  Best for: online course creators and webinar hosts
GetResponse bundles email marketing with native webinar hosting, online courses, and conversion funnels—making it superior for educators and course sellers. While Drip focuses on e-commerce, GetResponse is purpose-built for digital product creators seeking an all-in-one platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Drip?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with unlimited contacts and access to email, SMS, WhatsApp, and CRM features. For smaller subscriber lists, MailerLite (1,000 free contacts) and ConvertKit (1,000 free contacts) both provide excellent free alternatives with automation and landing pages included.

What is the best Drip alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the best choice for small businesses due to its affordability ($9/month), generous free tier, and powerful automation features. If you need CRM capabilities alongside email, ActiveCampaign at $15/month offers better value than Drip's $39 starting price while delivering more comprehensive customer management tools.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Drip?

Yes—MailerLite ($9/month), GetResponse ($19/month), and ActiveCampaign ($15/month) all undercut Drip's $39 entry price. Additionally, several alternatives like Brevo, Mailchimp, and ConvertKit offer free tiers, allowing you to start completely free before upgrading to a paid plan.
