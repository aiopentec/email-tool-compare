Best Drip Alternatives for Email Marketing in 2025

While Drip excels at e-commerce email marketing, it may not be the right fit for every business—whether due to pricing, features, or specific industry needs. Whether you're looking for a more budget-friendly option, better e-commerce integrations, or a platform tailored to creators and publishers, there are several excellent alternatives worth considering.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Lower starting costs or free tiers for testing before committing
  • Better specialized features for your specific use case (creators, newsletters, agencies)
  • Superior e-commerce integrations with Shopify, WooCommerce, or other platforms
  • More advanced automation and CRM capabilities for complex workflows
  • Better customer support ratings and user satisfaction scores

Best Drip Alternatives in 2025

1
Klaviyo
The platform for intelligent marketing automation  ·  Best for: e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce
Klaviyo is purpose-built for e-commerce with deeper Shopify integration, predictive analytics, and dedicated review management features. If you're running a Shopify store, Klaviyo offers tighter integration and more sophisticated product recommendation capabilities than Drip.

ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign combines email, CRM, and sales automation in one platform, offering lead scoring and dynamic content that rivals Drip's capabilities. It's ideal if you need sophisticated marketing automation without switching between multiple tools, and starts at just $15/month.

MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite offers a free tier for up to 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $9/month, making it significantly cheaper than Drip while still including automation, landing pages, and e-commerce integrations. It's the best budget-friendly option without sacrificing essential features.

ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit is specifically designed for creators and course sellers with built-in commerce features, subscriber tagging, and visual automations. If you're selling digital products or courses rather than physical goods, ConvertKit's creator-focused approach outperforms Drip's e-commerce focus.

5
Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv uniquely combines email marketing with newsletter monetization, an ad network, and referral programs—features Drip doesn't offer. It's the clear winner if you're publishing newsletters and want built-in revenue opportunities alongside marketing automation.

6
Mailchimp
The all-in-one marketing platform  ·  Best for: beginners and small businesses
Mailchimp's free tier supports 500 contacts with full automation access, making it perfect for testing email marketing without investment. While less e-commerce-focused than Drip, it's unbeatable for getting started with no financial commitment.

Brevo
All-in-one CRM suite  ·  Best for: transactional email and multi-channel marketing
Brevo stands out with unlimited free contacts (100,000 limit) and built-in SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat alongside email—making it ideal if you need omnichannel marketing beyond email. Its transactional email API also excels for e-commerce order confirmations and notifications.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Drip?

Mailchimp and Brevo both offer generous free tiers without contact limits (or 500 contacts for Mailchimp). Brevo's free tier is particularly impressive with support for up to 100,000 contacts, though Mailchimp's free plan includes more advanced automation features. For creators specifically, ConvertKit offers 1,000 free subscribers.

What is the best Drip alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the top choice for small businesses, offering a free tier for 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at just $9/month, with all essential features like automation, landing pages, and e-commerce integrations. If you need CRM functionality alongside email, ActiveCampaign at $15/month provides excellent value for growing businesses.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Drip?

Yes, several alternatives are more affordable than Drip's $39 starting price. MailerLite ($9/month), ActiveCampaign ($15/month), and GetResponse ($19/month) all offer robust email marketing at lower price points. If budget is your primary concern, Mailchimp, Brevo, and ConvertKit all have free tiers that require no upfront investment.
