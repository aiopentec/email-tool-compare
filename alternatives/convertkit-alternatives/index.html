Best ConvertKit Alternatives for Creators & Bloggers

While ConvertKit excels at serving creators, its pricing and feature set may not suit everyone's needs. Whether you're looking for more affordable options, specialized features, or different pricing models, several strong alternatives offer competitive email marketing capabilities tailored to different creator types and budgets.

Why look for a ConvertKit alternative?

  • Lower pricing tiers starting from as little as $9/month compared to ConvertKit's $29 entry point
  • Specialized features for specific creator types, such as newsletter monetization, e-commerce integration, or webinar hosting
  • Different free tier limits—some offering up to 100,000 free subscribers versus ConvertKit's 1,000

Best ConvertKit alternatives

Beehiiv

The newsletter platform built for growth

Best for: Newsletter publishers and media operators

Beehiiv stands out with built-in newsletter monetization, an ad network, and referral program features specifically designed for publishers. With a generous 2,500 subscriber free tier and strong Trustpilot ratings (4.4), it's ideal for creators focused on building sustainable newsletter businesses.

MailerLite

Simple email marketing for small businesses

Best for: Small businesses and freelancers on a budget

MailerLite offers an unbeatable entry price starting at just $9/month with a full feature set including automation, landing pages, and pop-ups. It combines affordability with excellent user reviews (4.5 Trustpilot rating), making it perfect for budget-conscious creators.

Compare ConvertKit vs MailerLite

Mailchimp

The all-in-one marketing platform

Best for: Beginners and small businesses

Mailchimp is one of the most established platforms with a robust free tier and starting paid plans at $13/month. Its simplicity and extensive integrations make it ideal for creators transitioning from free tools who need straightforward email marketing without advanced features.

Compare ConvertKit vs Mailchimp

ActiveCampaign

Email, automation, and CRM combined

Best for: Businesses needing advanced automation and CRM

ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with a full CRM system, lead scoring, and site tracking starting at $15/month. It's the best choice for creators who need sophisticated automation workflows and customer relationship management beyond basic email capabilities.

Compare ConvertKit vs ActiveCampaign

GetResponse

Complete online marketing platform

Best for: Online course creators and webinar hosts

GetResponse uniquely bundles webinar hosting, online course creation, and email marketing into one platform starting at $19/month. For course creators and educators, this integrated approach eliminates the need for multiple tool subscriptions.

Compare ConvertKit vs GetResponse

Brevo

All-in-one CRM suite

Best for: Transactional email and multi-channel marketing

Brevo offers the largest free tier with 100,000 free subscribers and includes SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat alongside email. It's perfect for creators wanting to build a comprehensive communication platform beyond email marketing alone.

Compare ConvertKit vs Brevo

Klaviyo

The platform for intelligent marketing automation

Best for: E-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce

Klaviyo specializes in e-commerce with native Shopify/WooCommerce integration, predictive analytics, and review management features. If you're a creator selling physical or digital products, Klaviyo's e-commerce focus provides superior functionality.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free alternative to ConvertKit?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with up to 100,000 subscribers, compared to ConvertKit's 1,000. Mailchimp and MailerLite also provide excellent free options with 500 and 1,000 subscribers respectively. However, if you need stronger free features specifically for newsletters, Beehiiv allows 2,500 free subscribers.

What is the best ConvertKit alternative for small business?

MailerLite is ideal for small businesses due to its low $9/month starting price, intuitive interface, and strong feature set including automation and landing pages. Mailchimp is another excellent choice with its established reputation and extensive integrations. Both offer superior value for small teams with limited budgets compared to ConvertKit.

Is there a cheaper alternative to ConvertKit?

Yes, MailerLite ($9/month), Mailchimp ($13/month), and ActiveCampaign ($15/month) all offer lower starting prices than ConvertKit's $29/month. GetResponse at $19/month adds webinar functionality for just $10 less. For the most budget-conscious creators, both Mailchimp and MailerLite provide solid free tiers that may eliminate the need for paid plans initially.
