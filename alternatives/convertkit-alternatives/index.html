7 Best ConvertKit Alternatives for Email Marketing in 2025

ConvertKit is an excellent platform for creators, but it may not be the perfect fit for everyone. Whether you're looking for more affordable pricing, different features, or specialized functionality, there are several strong alternatives worth considering. We've reviewed the top options to help you find the best email marketing platform for your needs.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Find more affordable pricing plans starting from as low as $9/month
  • Access specialized features like SMS, webinars, or e-commerce integrations
  • Get better customer support ratings and user satisfaction scores

Best ConvertKit Alternatives in 2025

1
Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv is purpose-built for newsletter creators with built-in monetization, an ad network, and referral program features. With a higher free tier (2,500 subscribers) and excellent customer ratings (4.7 on G2), it's ideal if you want to grow and monetize your audience directly.

MailerLite

MailerLite offers the lowest paid-tier pricing at $9/month while maintaining powerful features like automation, landing pages, and pop-ups. Its exceptional Trustpilot rating (4.5) and user-friendly interface make it the best budget-friendly alternative.

ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with a full CRM, advanced automation, and lead scoring capabilities. If you need sophisticated customer relationship management alongside email marketing, this all-in-one platform eliminates the need for multiple tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to ConvertKit?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with 100,000 subscribers and multi-channel features (email, SMS, WhatsApp). If you prefer a more traditional email marketing approach, Mailchimp and MailerLite both offer robust free plans with up to 500-1,000 subscribers and powerful automation features.

What is the best ConvertKit alternative for small business?

MailerLite is ideal for small businesses, combining affordability ($9/month starting price), ease of use, and excellent customer support. For businesses needing CRM functionality, ActiveCampaign provides advanced automation and customer relationship tools at a comparable price point.

Is there a cheaper alternative to ConvertKit?

Yes, MailerLite starts at just $9/month compared to ConvertKit's $29/month minimum. Mailchimp and Brevo both offer free plans indefinitely, and GetResponse starts at $19/month, making them all more budget-friendly options depending on your subscriber count and feature needs.
