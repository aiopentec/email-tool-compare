  • You need features that are not available on this plan
  • Pricing does not scale well for your subscriber count

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to ConvertKit?

MailerLite and Brevo both offer generous free plans that are worth considering as free alternatives to ConvertKit. The best choice depends on your list size and the features you need most.

What is the best ConvertKit alternative for small business?

For small businesses, MailerLite is frequently recommended as a ConvertKit alternative due to its low cost and ease of use. ActiveCampaign is worth considering if you need CRM features alongside email marketing.

Is there a cheaper alternative to ConvertKit?

Yes — ConvertKit is not the cheapest option in the market. Moosend and MailerLite consistently rank as the most affordable options with a comparable feature set.
