Best ConvertKit Alternatives for Creators & Bloggers

While ConvertKit excels at serving creators, its pricing and feature set may not suit everyone's needs. Whether you're looking for more affordable options, specialized features, or different pricing models, several strong alternatives offer competitive email marketing capabilities tailored to different creator types and budgets.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Lower pricing tiers starting from as little as $9/month compared to ConvertKit's $29 entry point
  • Specialized features for specific creator types, such as newsletter monetization, e-commerce integration, or webinar hosting
  • Different free tier limits—some offering up to 100,000 free subscribers versus ConvertKit's 1,000

Best ConvertKit Alternatives in 2025

Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: Newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv stands out with built-in newsletter monetization, an ad network, and referral program features specifically designed for publishers. With a generous 2,500 subscriber free tier and strong Trustpilot ratings (4.4), it's ideal for creators focused on building sustainable newsletter businesses.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to ConvertKit?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with up to 100,000 subscribers, compared to ConvertKit's 1,000. Mailchimp and MailerLite also provide excellent free options with 500 and 1,000 subscribers respectively. However, if you need stronger free features specifically for newsletters, Beehiiv allows 2,500 free subscribers.

What is the best ConvertKit alternative for small business?

MailerLite is ideal for small businesses due to its low $9/month starting price, intuitive interface, and strong feature set including automation and landing pages. Mailchimp is another excellent choice with its established reputation and extensive integrations. Both offer superior value for small teams with limited budgets compared to ConvertKit.

Is there a cheaper alternative to ConvertKit?

Yes, MailerLite ($9/month), Mailchimp ($13/month), and ActiveCampaign ($15/month) all offer lower starting prices than ConvertKit's $29/month. GetResponse at $19/month adds webinar functionality for just $10 less. For the most budget-conscious creators, both Mailchimp and MailerLite provide solid free tiers that may eliminate the need for paid plans initially.
