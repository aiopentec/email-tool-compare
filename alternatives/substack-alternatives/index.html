Best Substack Alternatives in 2025: Honest Comparison

While Substack is a capable platform, it is not the right fit for everyone. Whether you need a lower price point, a different feature set, or a platform better suited to your audience, there are strong alternatives worth considering. This guide covers the best Substack alternatives in 2025.

Why look for a Substack alternative?

  • You have outgrown Substack's free tier limits
  • You need features that are not available on this plan
  • Pricing does not scale well for your subscriber count

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free alternative to Substack?

MailerLite and Brevo both offer generous free plans that are worth considering as free alternatives to Substack. The best choice depends on your list size and the features you need most.

What is the best Substack alternative for small business?

For small businesses, MailerLite is frequently recommended as a Substack alternative due to its low cost and ease of use. ActiveCampaign is worth considering if you need CRM features alongside email marketing.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Substack?

Most competitors are similarly priced or higher at scale. Moosend and MailerLite consistently rank as the most affordable options with a comparable feature set.
