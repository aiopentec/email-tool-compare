7 Best GetResponse Alternatives in 2025

While GetResponse is a solid all-in-one marketing platform, you might be looking for alternatives that better match your specific needs, budget, or feature set. Whether you need stronger creator tools, better pricing, or advanced automation capabilities, there are several excellent alternatives worth considering.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Find more affordable pricing starting from $9/month instead of $19
  • Access specialized features for your industry (e-commerce, newsletters, creators)
  • Get better customer support ratings and user satisfaction scores
  • Enjoy more generous free tiers with higher subscriber limits

Best GetResponse Alternatives in 2025

1
MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: Small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite offers one of the most affordable entry points at just $9/month with a generous 1,000 subscriber free tier and excellent Trustpilot ratings (4.5). It combines simplicity with powerful automation, landing pages, and pop-ups—making it ideal for budget-conscious marketers.

2
ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: Creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit is purpose-built for creators with visual automation workflows, subscriber tagging, and built-in commerce features that rival GetResponse's course capabilities. It allows up to 1,000 free subscribers and offers 30% recurring affiliate commissions for 24 months.

3
Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: Newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv stands out with its newsletter monetization features, built-in ad network, and referral program capabilities—perfect for publishers wanting to generate revenue. With a 4.7 G2 rating and 4.4 Trustpilot score, it's the highest-rated option and offers 2,500 free subscribers.

4
ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: Businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign provides enterprise-grade automation, CRM, lead scoring, and site tracking that far exceed GetResponse's capabilities for serious marketers. While it lacks a free tier, it starts at just $15/month and offers 20-30% recurring affiliate commissions.

5
Brevo
All-in-one CRM suite  ·  Best for: Transactional email and multi-channel marketing
Brevo's free tier allows 100,000 subscribers—the most generous limit available—and includes SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat alongside email. It's ideal for businesses needing multi-channel communication without breaking the budget at $25/month for paid plans.

Mailchimp
The all-in-one marketing platform  ·  Best for: Beginners and small businesses
Mailchimp is the most well-known email marketing platform with a 4.3 G2 rating and offers comprehensive features like A/B testing, audience segmentation, and automation starting at just $13/month. Its drag-drop editor and analytics make it perfect for marketing newcomers.

Klaviyo
The platform for intelligent marketing automation  ·  Best for: E-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce
Klaviyo specializes in e-commerce with predictive analytics, pre-built flows, SMS integration, and deep Shopify/WooCommerce connections that GetResponse can't match. It's the best choice for online stores prioritizing conversion optimization over course creation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to GetResponse?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with up to 100,000 subscribers and includes email, SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat. If you prefer a creator-focused option, ConvertKit provides 1,000 free subscribers with visual automation designed specifically for bloggers and course creators.

What is the best GetResponse alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the best choice for small businesses, starting at just $9/month with a 1,000 subscriber free tier and excellent customer ratings (4.5 Trustpilot). It includes all essential features like automation, landing pages, and pop-ups without unnecessary complexity.

Is there a cheaper alternative to GetResponse?

Yes—MailerLite at $9/month is $10 cheaper than GetResponse's $19 starting price. Mailchimp is also more affordable at $13/month. Both offer similar features with better free tiers and comparable automation capabilities at lower price points.
