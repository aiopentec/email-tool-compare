7 Best GetResponse Alternatives in 2025

While GetResponse offers a comprehensive all-in-one platform, it may not be the best fit for every business. Whether you're looking for more affordable pricing, specialized features, or a better user experience, there are several strong alternatives worth considering. We've compared the top options to help you find the perfect email marketing tool for your specific needs.

Why look for an alternative?
  • More affordable pricing plans starting from $9/month instead of $19/month
  • Better specialized features for specific use cases like e-commerce, newsletters, or course creation
  • Improved user ratings and customer satisfaction scores across review platforms

Best GetResponse Alternatives in 2025

1
ConvertKit
Email platform built for creators  ·  Best for: Creators, bloggers, and course sellers
ConvertKit is specifically designed for content creators with built-in tools for monetization, subscriber management, and visual automation workflows. If you're focused on building an audience and selling courses or digital products, ConvertKit's creator-first approach offers better usability than GetResponse's generalist platform.

MailerLite
Simple email marketing for small businesses  ·  Best for: Small businesses and freelancers on a budget
MailerLite delivers powerful email marketing features at a fraction of GetResponse's cost, starting at just $9/month with 1,000 free subscribers. It's the ideal choice if you want drag-and-drop simplicity combined with automation and landing pages without the premium price tag.

Beehiiv
The newsletter platform built for growth  ·  Best for: Newsletter publishers and media operators
Beehiiv excels at newsletter monetization with built-in ad networks, referral programs, and audience analytics that GetResponse doesn't offer. If your primary goal is growing and monetizing a newsletter, Beehiiv's specialized features and superior ratings (4.7 G2, 4.4 Trustpilot) make it the stronger choice.

ActiveCampaign
Email, automation, and CRM combined  ·  Best for: Businesses needing advanced automation and CRM
ActiveCampaign combines robust email marketing with enterprise-grade CRM, lead scoring, and dynamic content capabilities that go beyond GetResponse's offering. Mailchimp

Mailchimp offers an intuitive drag-drop editor, strong automation features, and A/B testing at an affordable $13/month starting price. Brevo stands out with an incredibly generous free tier (up to 100,000 contacts) plus multi-channel capabilities including SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat in one platform. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to GetResponse?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with support for up to 100,000 contacts, making it ideal for growing businesses at no cost. If you prefer a more feature-rich free experience with lower subscriber limits, both ConvertKit (1,000 free subscribers) and MailerLite (1,000 free subscribers) offer excellent free plans with professional-grade tools.

What is the best GetResponse alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the best choice for small businesses, offering an unbeatable combination of affordability (starting at $9/month), ease of use, and essential features like automation, landing pages, and pop-ups. It provides everything a growing small business needs without the complexity or cost of larger platforms.

Is there a cheaper alternative to GetResponse?

Yes, MailerLite starts at just $9/month compared to GetResponse's $19/month entry price, and Mailchimp begins at $13/month. For the most budget-conscious choice, Brevo offers unlimited contacts on its free plan, making it the best option if you want to avoid paid plans entirely while still accessing powerful marketing tools.
