Best Campaign Monitor Alternatives

Campaign Monitor is a solid choice for agencies managing multiple brands, but it lacks a free tier and may be overkill for smaller teams. Whether you're looking for more affordable options, better free tiers, or specialized features like e-commerce integrations or CRM capabilities, there are several excellent alternatives worth considering.

Why look for a Campaign Monitor alternative?

  • Access free plans with generous subscriber limits before paying
  • Save money with lower starting prices or more feature-rich free tiers
  • Get specialized features for e-commerce, creators, or advanced automation

Best Campaign Monitor alternatives

MailerLite

Simple email marketing for small businesses

Best for: Small businesses and freelancers on a budget

MailerLite offers similar multi-brand capabilities and template building at a lower starting price ($9 vs $11), plus a free tier supporting 1,000 subscribers. It's the most direct alternative for teams seeking affordability without sacrificing core features like automation and landing pages.

Compare Campaign Monitor vs MailerLite

ActiveCampaign

Email, automation, and CRM combined

Best for: Businesses needing advanced automation and CRM

ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with integrated CRM, lead scoring, and advanced automation workflows that Campaign Monitor doesn't offer natively. It's ideal for agencies wanting to manage customer relationships alongside email campaigns in a unified platform.

Compare Campaign Monitor vs ActiveCampaign

Brevo

All-in-one CRM suite

Best for: Transactional email and multi-channel marketing

Brevo's free tier includes 100,000 emails per month, making it exceptional for transactional email needs that Campaign Monitor handles less efficiently. It also adds SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat to your marketing toolkit in one platform.

Compare Campaign Monitor vs Brevo

ConvertKit

Email platform built for creators

Best for: Creators, bloggers, and course sellers

ConvertKit specializes in creator monetization with built-in commerce features and subscriber tagging designed for content creators, whereas Campaign Monitor focuses on traditional email marketing. It's better for managing audience growth and content distribution rather than multi-brand agency work.

Compare Campaign Monitor vs ConvertKit

Beehiiv

The newsletter platform built for growth

Best for: Newsletter publishers and media operators

Beehiiv's standout feature is its built-in ad network and referral program that monetize newsletters directly, something Campaign Monitor completely lacks. The free tier supports 2,500 subscribers, making it ideal for independent publishers seeking growth-focused tools.

Compare Campaign Monitor vs Beehiiv

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free alternative to Campaign Monitor?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with 100,000 emails per month and unlimited contacts, making it best for transactional needs. If you prefer a full-featured builder, Beehiiv (2,500 subscribers) and MailerLite (1,000 subscribers) both offer strong free plans with automation and landing pages.

What is the best Campaign Monitor alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the best affordable alternative for small teams, starting at just $9/month with a free tier and includes all essentials like automation, landing pages, and pop-ups. It offers the closest feature parity to Campaign Monitor at a lower price point.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Campaign Monitor?

Yes, MailerLite starts at $9/month (vs Campaign Monitor's $11), and several alternatives offer free tiers where you pay nothing to start: Mailchimp, ConvertKit, Beehiiv, Brevo, and GetResponse all have no-cost options. Brevo stands out with the most generous free plan offering 100,000 emails monthly.
