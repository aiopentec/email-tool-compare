7 Best Campaign Monitor Alternatives in 2025

While Campaign Monitor excels at multi-brand management for agencies, other email marketing platforms may offer better value, more advanced features, or lower costs depending on your business needs. Whether you're looking for a free tier, advanced automation, or industry-specific tools, there's likely a better fit available today.

Why look for an alternative?
  • Find more affordable pricing options with free tiers and lower starting costs
  • Access specialized features for e-commerce, creators, or transactional email
  • Get better customer support ratings and user satisfaction scores

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Campaign Monitor?

Brevo offers the most generous free tier with 100,000 emails monthly and multi-channel capabilities including SMS and WhatsApp. If you prefer a more traditional email-focused platform, MailerLite and ConvertKit both provide free tiers supporting 1,000 subscribers with solid automation features.

What is the best Campaign Monitor alternative for small business?

MailerLite is the best choice for small businesses, combining the lowest starting price ($9) with an intuitive drag-drop editor, landing pages, and automation. It delivers professional features without the complexity, making it ideal for lean teams managing modest email lists.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Campaign Monitor?

Yes, MailerLite starts at just $9 per month compared to Campaign Monitor's $11. Brevo and Mailchimp both offer completely free tiers, making them cost-effective starting points before you scale to paid plans with advanced features.
