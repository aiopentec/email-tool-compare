Flodesk vs Constant Contact: Detailed Comparison

Flodesk and Constant Contact are both established email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences. Flodesk focuses on design-first marketing for creative brands, while Constant Contact emphasizes simplicity and multi-channel features for local businesses and nonprofits. Understanding their key differences will help you choose the right tool for your marketing needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Flodesk better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your priorities. Flodesk excels in design quality and simplicity with unlimited subscribers, earning higher ratings (4.8 vs 4.0 on G2). However, Constant Contact offers more features like SMS and social media ads at a lower starting price, making it better for feature-hungry businesses on tight budgets.

Which is cheaper, Flodesk or Constant Contact?

Constant Contact has a lower starting price at $12/month compared to Flodesk's $38/month. However, Flodesk's flat-rate pricing means you get unlimited subscribers regardless of list size, while Constant Contact charges based on subscriber count, which could become more expensive as you grow.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Flodesk?

Yes, both platforms support list imports, so you can migrate your subscribers. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your contacts as a CSV file and import them into Flodesk. Plan your transition during a natural billing cycle to minimize overlap and extra costs.
