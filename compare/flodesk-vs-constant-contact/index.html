Frequently Asked Questions

Is Flodesk better than Constant Contact?

Flodesk excels for design-focused businesses with higher customer satisfaction ratings (4.7 vs 2.8 on Trustpilot), while Constant Contact offers more integrated features and lower entry costs. The better choice depends on your priorities: design quality and simplicity versus affordability and marketing tools.

Which is cheaper, Flodesk or Constant Contact?

Constant Contact starts at $12/month, making it significantly cheaper upfront than Flodesk's $38/month entry point. However, Constant Contact's pricing scales with subscriber count, while Flodesk charges a flat rate for unlimited subscribers, potentially favoring growing businesses.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Flodesk?

Yes, both platforms support contact list imports, making migration straightforward. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your subscriber list and import it into another platform, though you may need to redesign campaigns using Flodesk's templates.
