Your choice depends on whether you're building a subscriber-driven publication or running an online store.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Drip?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your use case. Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers with its monetization features and free tier, while Drip is superior for e-commerce businesses needing advanced store integration. Beehiiv has higher user ratings (4.7 vs 4.4 on G2), but Drip offers more specialized e-commerce features.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Drip?

Beehiiv is cheaper because it offers a free tier for up to 2,500 subscribers, making it ideal for startups. Drip requires a paid subscription starting at $39/month with no free option. If you need paid features, both start at the same $39/month price point.

Can I switch from Drip to Beehiiv?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber list and email campaigns between platforms. Most email marketing tools support CSV exports and imports, so the technical process is straightforward. However, you'll need to rebuild automation workflows and customize templates in Beehiiv, as these don't typically transfer directly.
