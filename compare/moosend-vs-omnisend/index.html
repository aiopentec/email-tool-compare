Moosend vs Omnisend: Detailed Comparison

Both Moosend and Omnisend are popular email marketing platforms offering freemium models, but they serve different audiences. Moosend focuses on affordable, unlimited email campaigns for small businesses, while Omnisend specializes in omnichannel solutions combining email, SMS, and push notifications for e-commerce brands. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right tool for your marketing needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Moosend better than Omnisend?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. Moosend wins on price and email-only simplicity, making it ideal for small businesses. Omnisend excels for e-commerce brands needing SMS, push notifications, and advanced segmentation alongside email. Compare based on whether you need omnichannel capabilities.

Which is cheaper, Moosend or Omnisend?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to Omnisend's $16/month. Moosend also offers a more generous free tier with 1,000 subscribers versus Omnisend's 250. However, Omnisend's pricing reflects its broader feature set including SMS and push notifications.

Can I switch from Omnisend to Moosend?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber lists and campaign data from Omnisend to Moosend. Both platforms support standard data export formats. However, you'll lose SMS and push notification capabilities since Moosend focuses on email, so evaluate whether this trade-off makes sense for your business.
