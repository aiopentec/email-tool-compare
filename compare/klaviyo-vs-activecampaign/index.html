Frequently Asked Questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Klaviyo?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your use case. ActiveCampaign wins for businesses needing advanced automation, CRM, and lead scoring across multiple industries. Klaviyo is superior for e-commerce brands prioritizing predictive analytics and Shopify integration. Compare your specific needs against each platform's strengths.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Klaviyo?

Klaviyo is cheaper for small businesses, with a free tier for up to 500 contacts. ActiveCampaign starts at $15/month with no free option but may offer better value if you need CRM and advanced automation. For growing e-commerce, Klaviyo's paid plans ($45+) often provide better pricing.

Can I switch from Klaviyo to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, both platforms support data migration, though the process requires manual export/import of contacts and automation workflows. Most users can migrate their email lists relatively easily, but complex automation flows may need rebuilding. Contact each platform's support team for specific migration assistance.
