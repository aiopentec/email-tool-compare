Frequently asked questions

Is Brevo better than Campaign Monitor?

Brevo is better for most users due to its generous free tier, lower entry price, and broader feature set including SMS and WhatsApp. However, Campaign Monitor may be better if you're an agency that prioritizes design quality and multi-brand management capabilities over cost savings.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Campaign Monitor?

Brevo is significantly cheaper, offering a free plan with up to 100,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $25/month. Campaign Monitor has no free tier and starts at $11/month, making it paid-only. For most businesses, Brevo's free tier alone provides excellent value.

Can I switch from Campaign Monitor to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms typically support importing subscriber lists and email templates, though the process may require manual setup or data migration. We recommend exporting your contacts and campaigns from Campaign Monitor and importing them into Brevo. Contact support for assistance if you encounter compatibility issues.
