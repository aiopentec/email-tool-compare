Frequently asked questions

Is Brevo better than Campaign Monitor?

It depends on your use case. Brevo wins for transactional email and multi-channel marketing, while Campaign Monitor is stronger for agencies and teams managing multiple brands. Neither is objectively better — the right choice depends on your specific needs.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Campaign Monitor?

Campaign Monitor starts at a lower price point ($11/mo vs $25/mo). However, pricing scales with subscriber count, so the total cost depends on your list size.

Can I switch from Campaign Monitor to Brevo?

Yes, switching from Campaign Monitor to Brevo is possible. Most platforms support CSV import/export for subscriber lists. Plan for some time to recreate automations and templates, as these rarely transfer directly between platforms.
