Which Should You Choose?

Choose Beehiiv if…

  • You're building a paid newsletter or media publication and need monetization features
  • You want access to an ad network to generate revenue from your audience
  • You prioritize newsletter growth tools and have a larger subscriber base

Choose MailerLite if…

  • You're a small business or freelancer on a tight budget with limited email subscribers
  • You need landing pages, pop-ups, and e-commerce integrations for marketing campaigns
  • You want powerful automation and survey tools without high upfront costs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than MailerLite?

Neither is universally better—they serve different needs. Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers seeking monetization and growth, while MailerLite is superior for small businesses needing affordable automation and e-commerce features. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize revenue generation or cost-effective marketing automation.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or MailerLite?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, starting at just $9/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month. Additionally, MailerLite's free tier supports 1,000 subscribers while Beehiiv offers 2,500, but MailerLite's paid plans scale more affordably for budget-conscious businesses.

Can I switch from MailerLite to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms typically support subscriber list imports and exports. However, you may lose some campaign history and advanced segmentation data during migration. It's recommended to plan your transition carefully and test with a small segment first to ensure all data transfers correctly.
