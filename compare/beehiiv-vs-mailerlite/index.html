Frequently asked questions

Is Beehiiv better than MailerLite?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your use case. Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers wanting monetization and growth features. MailerLite is superior for small businesses needing affordable, all-in-one email marketing with landing pages and e-commerce tools. Choose based on your primary needs and budget.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or MailerLite?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month. MailerLite's free tier also supports 1,000 subscribers, while Beehiiv allows 2,500. For budget-conscious businesses, MailerLite offers better value at entry-level pricing.

Can I switch from MailerLite to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support subscriber list exports and imports, making migration possible. However, you'll lose MailerLite-specific features like landing pages and pop-ups in the transfer. Plan your transition carefully and test in Beehiiv's free tier before committing to paid plans.
