Frequently Asked Questions

Is Omnisend better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your business type. Omnisend excels for e-commerce brands needing omnichannel marketing with SMS and push notifications, while Constant Contact is better suited for nonprofits and local businesses seeking event management and social media tools. For most e-commerce use cases, Omnisend's specialized features and higher customer ratings make it the stronger choice.

Which is cheaper, Omnisend or Constant Contact?

Omnisend offers a free tier for up to 250 subscribers, making it cheaper for startups testing the platform. Both start at roughly $12-16/month for paid plans, but Omnisend's freemium model gives you more runway before paying. Omnisend also offers better affiliate commissions (20% recurring vs. $80 per signup), which can offset costs.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Omnisend?

Yes, you can migrate your email lists and contacts from Constant Contact to Omnisend. Most email marketing platforms support CSV imports or direct integrations, making the transition straightforward. However, you'll need to recreate automation workflows and templates in your new platform, so plan time for setup.
