Frequently Asked Questions

Is Substack better than Moosend?

Neither is universally better—they serve different purposes. Substack excels for individual creators monetizing newsletters, while Moosend is superior for businesses needing marketing automation and advanced features. Your choice depends on your primary goal: direct audience monetization or comprehensive email marketing.

Which is cheaper, Substack or Moosend?

Substack is technically free with unlimited subscribers, taking only a 10% cut of paid subscriptions. Moosend is free up to 1,000 subscribers, then starts at $9/month. For small lists, Moosend's free tier is cheaper; for larger audiences, Substack's no-upfront-cost model wins.

Can I switch from Moosend to Substack?

Yes, you can export your subscriber list from Moosend and import it into Substack, though the platforms have different architectures. Note that Substack is designed primarily for newsletters, while Moosend handles broader email marketing needs, so some features won't transfer directly.
