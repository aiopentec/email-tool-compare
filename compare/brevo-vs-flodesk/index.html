Brevo excels as an all-in-one CRM suite with transactional email capabilities, while Flodesk focuses on beautiful design and unlimited subscriber scaling. Understanding their key differences will help you choose the right tool for your business.

Which Should You Choose?

Choose Brevo if…

  • You need a free tier with substantial subscriber limits (up to 100K)
  • You require multi-channel marketing (SMS, WhatsApp, live chat alongside email)
  • You prioritize transactional email API and CRM functionality for ecommerce or SaaS

Choose Flodesk if…

  • Design and visual appeal are critical to your email marketing success
  • You want unlimited subscribers included in every plan
  • You need flat-rate pricing with no scaling surprises

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo better than Flodesk?

It depends on your priorities. Brevo is better for businesses needing comprehensive CRM features, multi-channel marketing, and transactional emails at a lower cost. Flodesk is better for creative professionals and brands where email design quality is paramount. Neither is universally 'better'—they serve different market segments.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Flodesk?

Brevo is significantly cheaper with a free tier supporting up to 100,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $25/month. Flodesk charges a flat $38/month with no free option, but includes unlimited subscribers from day one. For budget-conscious startups, Brevo wins; for growing brands wanting cost predictability, Flodesk may be better.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Brevo?

Yes, most email marketing platforms support list exports and migrations. Both Brevo and Flodesk allow you to download your subscriber lists and import them into the other platform. However, you'll need to manually rebuild workflows and templates, as these don't always transfer automatically.
