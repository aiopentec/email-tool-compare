Brevo vs Flodesk: Complete Comparison Guide

Brevo and Flodesk are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and use cases. Brevo excels as an all-in-one CRM with multi-channel capabilities, while Flodesk specializes in visually stunning emails for creative professionals. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right tool for your business.

Frequently asked questions

Is Brevo better than Flodesk?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Brevo is superior for businesses requiring multi-channel marketing, CRM features, and cost-effective scaling. Flodesk wins for visual-first brands and creative professionals who value design and simplicity. Check your specific requirements against each platform's strengths.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Flodesk?

Brevo is significantly cheaper, offering a free tier with up to 100,000 contacts and paid plans starting at $25/month. Flodesk has no free tier and starts at $38/month but includes unlimited subscribers. For large contact lists, Brevo's freemium model makes it more budget-friendly.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Brevo?

Yes, you can export your contact list and campaigns from Flodesk and import them into Brevo. Both platforms support standard CSV exports and email list imports. However, you may lose some design elements during migration, as Brevo's template approach differs from Flodesk's visual editor.
