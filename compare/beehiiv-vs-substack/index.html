Beehiiv vs Substack: Detailed Comparison for Newsletter Creators

Both Beehiiv and Substack are popular newsletter platforms, but they cater to different creator priorities. Beehiiv emphasizes growth tools and monetization flexibility, while Substack focuses on direct writer-to-reader relationships. Choosing between them depends on your goals, audience size, and preferred revenue model.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Substack?

It depends on your priorities. Beehiiv excels for creators seeking multiple revenue streams and advanced growth tools, earning higher customer satisfaction scores (4.7 vs 4.0 on G2). Substack is better if you prefer simplicity and unlimited free subscribers, though its lower Trustpilot rating (2.9) suggests some user satisfaction concerns.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Substack?

Substack is cheaper overall since it operates on a revenue-share model with no subscription fees—you only pay when readers subscribe to your paid content. Beehiiv requires $39/month minimum for paid plans, though its free tier supports 2,500 subscribers before upgrading is necessary.

Can I switch from Substack to Beehiiv?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber list from Substack to Beehiiv, though the process requires manual export and import. Both platforms support standard email list formats, making the transition feasible, though you may lose some Substack-specific features like its built-in discovery network.
