GetResponse vs Drip: Head-to-Head Comparison

GetResponse and Drip are both established email marketing platforms, but they serve different business models. GetResponse is a comprehensive all-in-one marketing suite designed for online course creators and webinar hosts, while Drip specializes in e-commerce email marketing with deep store integration. Your choice depends on whether you need broad marketing tools or laser-focused e-commerce features.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GetResponse better than Drip?

It depends on your business type. GetResponse excels for course creators and digital marketers with its comprehensive feature set and free tier, while Drip is superior for e-commerce businesses that need deep store integration and revenue attribution. GetResponse offers better overall value and flexibility, but Drip is specifically optimized for online retailers.

Which is cheaper, GetResponse or Drip?

GetResponse is significantly cheaper, starting at $0 with a free tier supporting up to 500 subscribers, while Drip's entry price is $39/month with no free option. If you're budget-conscious or just starting out, GetResponse's freemium model provides better value, though Drip may offer better ROI for established e-commerce stores.

Can I switch from Drip to GetResponse?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list imports and exports, making migration possible. However, you may lose some Drip-specific data like revenue attribution and advanced e-commerce tracking during the switch. Plan your migration carefully and consider testing GetResponse's free tier before making the full switch from a paid Drip plan.
