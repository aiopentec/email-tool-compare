Frequently asked questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Drip?

It depends on your use case. ActiveCampaign is better for general businesses needing comprehensive automation and CRM features, with a higher G2 rating (4.5 vs 4.4) and lower starting price. Drip is superior for e-commerce stores specifically, offering specialized features like revenue attribution and e-commerce tracking that ActiveCampaign doesn't emphasize.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Drip?

ActiveCampaign is significantly cheaper, starting at $15/month compared to Drip's $39/month entry point. This makes ActiveCampaign the more cost-effective choice for budget-conscious businesses, though pricing scales with subscriber count for both platforms.

Can I switch from Drip to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, most email marketing platforms including ActiveCampaign support importing subscriber lists and contact data from competitors like Drip. You'll want to plan your automation workflows carefully during the migration, as the two platforms have different automation builders and features that may require reconfiguration.
