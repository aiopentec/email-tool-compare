Frequently Asked Questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Drip?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign is better for businesses requiring comprehensive CRM functionality and advanced automation at a lower price point. Drip is superior if you're an e-commerce business that needs specialized features like revenue attribution and seamless store integration.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Drip?

ActiveCampaign is significantly cheaper, starting at $15/month compared to Drip's $39/month. This makes ActiveCampaign the more budget-friendly option for businesses just starting with email marketing automation.

Can I switch from Drip to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, most email marketing platforms support data migration and export. Both ActiveCampaign and Drip allow you to export your contacts and campaign data, though you may lose some automation workflows that don't have direct equivalents during the transition.
