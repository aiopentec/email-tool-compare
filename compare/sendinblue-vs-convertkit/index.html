ConvertKit vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison for 2025

ConvertKit and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and use cases. ConvertKit focuses on creators and course sellers with intuitive automation and landing pages, while Sendinblue excels at transactional email, SMS, and multi-channel communication. Understanding their strengths will help you choose the right tool for your business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertKit better than Sendinblue?

It depends on your needs. ConvertKit excels for creators with its intuitive automation and landing pages, while Sendinblue offers more comprehensive features including SMS, transactional email, and CRM in one platform. For most general use cases, Sendinblue provides better value, but ConvertKit may be better if you're a creator focused on email sequences and subscriber management.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is more affordable overall. It offers a larger free tier with 100,000 subscribers compared to ConvertKit's 1,000, plus 300 free emails daily. Paid plans start at $25/month for Sendinblue versus $29/month for ConvertKit. However, pricing varies based on your subscriber count and specific needs.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms support email list imports and exports. You can export your subscribers from Sendinblue and import them into ConvertKit. However, you'll lose automation workflows and custom fields during the transfer, so plan your migration carefully and test thoroughly before switching your live campaigns.
