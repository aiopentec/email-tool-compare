Frequently Asked Questions

Is Klaviyo better than Campaign Monitor?

It depends on your use case. Klaviyo is superior for e-commerce brands requiring predictive analytics and SMS integration, earning higher ratings (4.6 vs 4.1 on G2). However, Campaign Monitor excels for agencies managing multiple brands and offers better value at lower price points, making it better for cost-conscious teams.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or Campaign Monitor?

Campaign Monitor starts at $11/month with no free tier, while Klaviyo offers a free plan for up to 500 subscribers. For small lists, Klaviyo's free tier is cheaper. Once you exceed 500 subscribers, Klaviyo's pricing ($45+/month) becomes comparable to Campaign Monitor, depending on your subscriber count and features needed.

Can I switch from Campaign Monitor to Klaviyo?

Yes, switching is straightforward. Both platforms support subscriber list imports via CSV or API connections. You can export your contacts and email templates from Campaign Monitor and import them into Klaviyo. We recommend planning the migration during a low-traffic period and testing your automation workflows before fully transitioning.
