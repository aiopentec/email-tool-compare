Which Should You Choose?

Choose Campaign Monitor if…

  • You're building a newsletter and want to monetize through ads or sponsorships
  • You're starting out and need a completely free tier with up to 2,500 subscribers
  • You prefer a growth-focused platform with affiliate partnership opportunities

Choose Beehiiv if…

  • You manage email campaigns for multiple clients or brands
  • You need advanced automation features like journey designer and transactional emails
  • You want a more established platform with 20+ years of experience in email marketing

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Campaign Monitor?

Neither is objectively 'better'—it depends on your needs. Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers seeking growth and monetization, while Campaign Monitor is superior for agencies managing multiple brands with advanced automation needs. Beehiiv has higher user satisfaction ratings (4.7 on G2 vs 4.1), but Campaign Monitor offers more mature, agency-focused features.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Campaign Monitor?

Beehiiv is significantly cheaper for small publishers, offering a free tier up to 2,500 subscribers with no payment required. Campaign Monitor starts at $11/month with no free option. However, both platforms' pricing scales with subscriber count, so the gap narrows as you grow larger lists.

Can I switch from Campaign Monitor to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list imports. You can typically export your subscriber data from Campaign Monitor and import it into Beehiiv. However, your email templates and automation workflows won't transfer automatically, so you'll need to recreate those within Beehiiv's interface.
