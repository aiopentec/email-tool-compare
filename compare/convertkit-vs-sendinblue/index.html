ConvertKit vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison for 2025

ConvertKit and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences. ConvertKit focuses on creators and course sellers with robust automation and landing pages, while Sendinblue excels at transactional email, SMS, and multi-channel campaigns. Choosing between them depends on your business model and communication needs.

Frequently asked questions

Is ConvertKit better than Sendinblue?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. ConvertKit excels for creators with its landing pages and visual automation builder. Sendinblue (Brevo) is superior for businesses needing multi-channel marketing, SMS, and transactional email at a lower cost. Check your specific requirements to decide.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is significantly cheaper if you're starting out, offering 100,000 free subscribers versus ConvertKit's 1,000. Paid plans are comparable ($25 vs $29/month), but Sendinblue's free tier with 300 emails/day makes it better for budget-conscious businesses. ConvertKit offers better long-term affiliate earnings at 30% recurring commission.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms support CSV imports and integrations with migration tools. Most email service providers allow you to export your subscriber lists. However, you'll need to rebuild automations and landing pages manually in ConvertKit, as these aren't typically transferable between platforms.
