Frequently asked questions

Is ConvertKit better than GetResponse?

It depends on your needs. ConvertKit excels for creators focused on email and simple automation with an intuitive interface. GetResponse offers more comprehensive features including webinars and courses at a lower price. For most creators, GetResponse provides better overall value, but ConvertKit is superior if simplicity and email focus matter most.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or GetResponse?

GetResponse is cheaper, starting at $19/month compared to ConvertKit's $29/month. Additionally, GetResponse's free tier supports up to 500 subscribers while ConvertKit offers 1,000, making ConvertKit more generous for bootstrapped creators. However, GetResponse includes more advanced features in lower-tier plans.

Can I switch from GetResponse to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and email content. Most email marketing platforms provide standard export formats (CSV, etc.) that the other can import. However, you'll need to manually recreate automations and sequences, as these don't transfer directly between platforms.
