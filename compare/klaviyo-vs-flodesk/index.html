Frequently asked questions

Is Klaviyo better than Flodesk?

It depends on your use case. Klaviyo wins for e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce, while Flodesk is stronger for visual brands, photographers, and designers. Neither is objectively better — the right choice depends on your specific needs.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or Flodesk?

Flodesk starts at a lower price point ($38/mo vs $45/mo). However, pricing scales with subscriber count, so the total cost depends on your list size.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Klaviyo?

Yes, switching from Flodesk to Klaviyo is possible. Most platforms support CSV import/export for subscriber lists. Plan for some time to recreate automations and templates, as these rarely transfer directly between platforms.
