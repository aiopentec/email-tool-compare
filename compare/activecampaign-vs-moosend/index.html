Frequently asked questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Moosend?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign is superior for businesses requiring advanced automation, CRM, and lead scoring capabilities. However, Moosend is better for startups and small businesses prioritizing affordability and ease of use with a free tier option.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Moosend?

Moosend is more budget-friendly, offering a free plan with up to 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $9/month. ActiveCampaign has no free tier and starts at $15/month, but includes more advanced features like CRM and lead scoring.

Can I switch from Moosend to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, both platforms support data exports and migrations. ActiveCampaign provides import tools to help you transfer your contact lists and email campaigns, though you may need to rebuild complex automation workflows manually.
