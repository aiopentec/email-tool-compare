GetResponse vs Sendinblue: Complete Comparison

GetResponse and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are both popular freemium email marketing platforms, but they serve different purposes. GetResponse excels as a complete marketing automation suite for course creators and webinar hosts, while Sendinblue specializes in transactional email, SMS, and CRM functionality. Choosing between them depends on whether you need comprehensive marketing automation or a multi-channel communication platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GetResponse better than Sendinblue?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. GetResponse is superior for course creators and webinar hosts due to its integrated educational features and conversion funnels. Sendinblue excels for businesses needing transactional email, SMS, and multi-channel communication. Check the comparison table to match your specific use case.

Which is cheaper, GetResponse or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue's free tier is significantly more generous with 100,000 contacts versus GetResponse's 500. However, GetResponse's paid plans start at $19/month compared to Sendinblue's $25/month. For budget-conscious users starting out, Sendinblue offers better free value.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to GetResponse?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your contact lists and email templates. Migration is straightforward for basic email lists, though you may need to reconfigure automation workflows and integrations in your new platform. Most users can complete the switch within a few hours.
