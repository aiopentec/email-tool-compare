Brevo vs Omnisend: Head-to-Head Comparison

Brevo and Omnisend are both freemium email marketing platforms with SMS capabilities, but they cater to different business needs. Brevo emphasizes comprehensive CRM and multi-channel marketing, while Omnisend specializes in e-commerce automation. Understanding their strengths will help you choose the right tool for your marketing strategy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo better than Omnisend?

It depends on your needs. Brevo is better for businesses requiring comprehensive CRM functionality, transactional email, and multi-channel communication including WhatsApp. Omnisend is better for e-commerce brands specifically looking for streamlined email and SMS automation with pre-built workflows and lower entry pricing.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Omnisend?

Omnisend starts at $16/month versus Brevo's $25/month, making it cheaper at entry-level pricing. However, Brevo's free tier accommodates 100,000 contacts compared to Omnisend's 250, making Brevo more cost-effective for businesses staying on the free plan longer.

Can I switch from Omnisend to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms support data exports and standard email list formats. Most email marketing platforms allow CSV imports, so migrating your contacts and basic campaign data is straightforward. However, you may lose some automation workflows and custom configurations during the transition, so plan the migration carefully.
