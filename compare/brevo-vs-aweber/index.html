Brevo and AWeber are both established email marketing platforms offering freemium pricing models, but they serve different needs. Brevo positions itself as an all-in-one CRM suite with multi-channel capabilities, while AWeber focuses on simplicity for bloggers and solopreneurs. Frequently asked questions

Is Brevo better than AWeber?

Brevo is better for businesses needing omnichannel marketing and CRM features, while AWeber excels for bloggers and solopreneurs prioritizing simplicity. Your choice depends on whether you need multi-channel capabilities or straightforward email marketing.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or AWeber?

AWeber's paid plans start at $15/month compared to Brevo's $25/month, making it cheaper for paid tiers. However, Brevo's free tier allows 100,000 contacts versus AWeber's 500, providing much better value if you stay on the free plan.

Can I switch from AWeber to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms support list imports and exports, making migration straightforward. Most email marketing tools provide CSV export functionality, which you can then import into Brevo to transfer your subscriber data.
