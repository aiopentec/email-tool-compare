Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo better than AWeber?

It depends on your needs. Brevo is superior for businesses requiring multi-channel marketing (SMS, WhatsApp, live chat) and transactional emails, making it better for ecommerce and larger operations. AWeber excels at simplicity and is ideal for bloggers and solopreneurs who want straightforward email marketing without extra features.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or AWeber?

AWeber has a lower entry price at $15/month for paid plans compared to Brevo's $25/month. However, Brevo's free tier supports 100,000 subscribers versus AWeber's 500, making Brevo more cost-effective if you're staying on the free plan for a growing audience.

Can I switch from AWeber to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your contact lists and email campaigns. Most email marketing tools, including Brevo, have import features to help you migrate your subscriber data. Always verify your data format compatibility and test the migration with a small segment first to ensure accuracy.
