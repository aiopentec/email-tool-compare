Mailchimp vs Flodesk: Detailed Comparison for 2025

Mailchimp and Flodesk are two popular email marketing platforms, but they cater to different audiences and business needs. Mailchimp excels as an affordable, feature-rich all-in-one marketing platform, while Flodesk specializes in visually stunning email designs for creative professionals. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right tool for your business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than Flodesk?

It depends on your priorities. Mailchimp is better for beginners, budget-conscious businesses, and those needing advanced automation features. Flodesk is superior if you're a visual brand that values stunning email design and willing to pay a flat monthly rate. Both are excellent tools—the best choice depends on your specific needs and budget.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Flodesk?

Mailchimp is significantly cheaper if you're starting out, with a free tier supporting up to 500 contacts at no cost. Flodesk has no free tier and starts at $38/month, but includes unlimited subscribers at every price level. For small lists, Mailchimp wins on price; for larger lists, Flodesk's flat-rate pricing becomes more competitive.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Mailchimp?

Yes, both platforms typically allow you to export your contact lists and email designs, though the process may require some manual work depending on your setup. We recommend documenting your workflows and automation sequences before switching. Contact both platforms' support teams for guidance on the migration process specific to your account.
