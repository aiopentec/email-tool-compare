ActiveCampaign vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

ActiveCampaign and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are both powerful email marketing platforms, but they serve different business needs. ActiveCampaign excels in advanced automation and CRM for mid-market businesses, while Sendinblue offers an affordable freemium option ideal for startups and transactional email. This guide helps you choose based on your specific requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Sendinblue?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign offers superior automation and CRM features for growing businesses, while Sendinblue excels at transactional email and provides better value for startups with its free tier. ActiveCampaign is better for complex workflows; Sendinblue for budget-conscious teams.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is cheaper initially, offering a free plan with up to 300 emails daily and 100K subscribers. ActiveCampaign has no free tier and starts at $15/month. However, Sendinblue's paid plans begin at $25/month, so pricing becomes competitive at higher volumes.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list exports and imports. Most data can be migrated, though you may need to reconfigure automation workflows and CRM fields since each platform has different architecture. Plan for setup time when switching between them.
