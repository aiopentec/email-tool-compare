Frequently Asked Questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Sendinblue?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign offers superior automation and lead scoring, making it better for complex marketing workflows. Sendinblue excels at transactional email and offers better value for beginners with its free tier. Choose based on your priorities.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is cheaper overall due to its free tier offering 300 emails/day and support for 100,000 contacts at no cost. ActiveCampaign has no free tier and starts at $15/month, but includes more advanced features. For budget-conscious startups, Sendinblue wins.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, both platforms support data export and import. You can migrate your contact lists, though automation workflows and complex configurations will need to be recreated in ActiveCampaign. Plan for some setup time during the transition.
