Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than GetResponse?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Beehiiv excels at newsletter monetization and has higher user satisfaction ratings, making it ideal for publishers. GetResponse offers broader functionality including webinars, courses, and automation, making it better for diverse marketing needs. Consider your primary use case when deciding.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or GetResponse?

GetResponse has a lower entry price at $19/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month. However, Beehiiv's free tier accommodates 2,500 subscribers versus GetResponse's 500, so Beehiiv may be more cost-effective longer if you're bootstrapping. The best choice depends on your subscriber count and feature requirements.

Can I switch from GetResponse to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list exports and imports. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your subscriber lists and migrate to another platform, though you'll need to reconfigure automations and templates manually. Check both platforms' documentation for specific migration steps.
