Frequently Asked Questions

Is Campaign Monitor better than AWeber?

It depends on your needs. Campaign Monitor excels for agencies with its multi-brand management and advanced automation, earning it a slight edge overall. AWeber is better for beginners due to its free tier and simpler interface. Both have strong ratings, with AWeber scoring slightly higher on Trustpilot (4.0 vs 3.2).

Which is cheaper, Campaign Monitor or AWeber?

AWeber is cheaper overall because it offers a free tier for up to 500 subscribers, making it ideal for startups. Campaign Monitor starts at $11/month with no free option. However, once you outgrow AWeber's free plan, both platforms have competitive pricing in the $15-25/month range.

Can I switch from AWeber to Campaign Monitor?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber list between platforms. Most email marketing tools support CSV export/import, allowing you to transfer your contacts and campaigns. However, you may need to recreate automation workflows, as each platform has different automation builders and features.
