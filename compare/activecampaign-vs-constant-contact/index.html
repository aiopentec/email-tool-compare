Frequently Asked Questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign is better for businesses requiring advanced automation, CRM, and lead scoring with a G2 rating of 4.5. Constant Contact suits nonprofits and local businesses seeking simplicity and affordability with lower Trustpilot ratings (2.8 vs 4.1).

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Constant Contact?

Constant Contact starts at $12/month compared to ActiveCampaign's $15/month, making it $3 cheaper per month. However, ActiveCampaign's included CRM and advanced automation may provide better value depending on your requirements.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your contact lists and email data. ActiveCampaign provides import tools to migrate your subscribers and segments, though you'll need to recreate automations and workflows manually for optimal results.
