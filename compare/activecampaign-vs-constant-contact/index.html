Frequently asked questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign excels in automation, CRM, and advanced features with higher customer ratings, making it better for growing businesses needing sophisticated tools. Constant Contact is simpler and cheaper, making it ideal for small businesses and nonprofits just starting out with email marketing.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Constant Contact?

Constant Contact has a lower starting price at $12/month compared to ActiveCampaign's $15/month. However, ActiveCampaign's advanced features may provide better value for businesses that need automation and CRM capabilities, justifying the slightly higher cost.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, switching is possible and both platforms support data migration. Most email marketing platforms allow you to export your contact lists and campaign data, though you may need to recreate automation workflows and templates in ActiveCampaign. Check each platform's migration guides for detailed instructions.
