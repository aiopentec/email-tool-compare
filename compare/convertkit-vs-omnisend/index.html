ConvertKit vs Omnisend: Detailed Comparison

ConvertKit and Omnisend are both freemium email marketing platforms with different strengths. ConvertKit excels for creators and course sellers, while Omnisend is purpose-built for e-commerce brands. Your choice depends on whether you need email-focused features or omnichannel marketing capabilities.

Frequently asked questions

Is ConvertKit better than Omnisend?

Neither is universally 'better'—they serve different audiences. ConvertKit is superior for creators and content professionals, while Omnisend excels for e-commerce businesses. Your best choice depends on your business model and whether you need SMS and push notifications alongside email.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Omnisend?

Omnisend is more affordable, starting at $16/month compared to ConvertKit's $29/month entry price. However, ConvertKit offers a larger free tier (1,000 vs 250 subscribers), making it better for bootstrapped creators testing the platform.

Can I switch from Omnisend to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list exports, making migration possible. However, you'll lose SMS and push notification data, and may need to recreate automations manually since the platforms use different workflow builders. Plan for some setup time during the transition.
