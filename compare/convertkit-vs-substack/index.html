Frequently asked questions

Is ConvertKit better than Substack?

It depends on your use case. ConvertKit wins for creators, bloggers, and course sellers, while Substack is stronger for writers monetising directly from subscribers. Neither is objectively better — the right choice depends on your specific needs.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Substack?

Substack starts at a lower price point ($0/mo vs $29/mo). However, pricing scales with subscriber count, so the total cost depends on your list size.

Can I switch from Substack to ConvertKit?

Yes, switching from Substack to ConvertKit is possible. Most platforms support CSV import/export for subscriber lists. Plan for some time to recreate automations and templates, as these rarely transfer directly between platforms.
