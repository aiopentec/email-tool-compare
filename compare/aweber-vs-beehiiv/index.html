Beehiiv focuses on newsletter publishers with monetization features, while AWeber prioritizes simplicity for bloggers and solopreneurs. Understanding their differences helps you choose the right tool for your business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than AWeber?

It depends on your goals. Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers wanting monetization and growth tools, earning higher ratings (4.7 on G2 vs 4.2). AWeber is better if you need simplicity, affordability, and landing pages for content marketing.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or AWeber?

AWeber is significantly cheaper, starting at $15/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month. However, Beehiiv's free tier supports 2,500 subscribers versus AWeber's 500, making it better value if you're starting small.

Can I switch from AWeber to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber list and import it into the other. The process is straightforward with CSV exports, though you may lose some custom field data depending on how your lists are structured.
