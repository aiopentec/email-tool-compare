Frequently Asked Questions

Is Moosend better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your needs. Moosend excels in affordability and automation with higher user satisfaction ratings (4.7 vs 4.0 on G2). However, Constant Contact offers broader integrated marketing tools including e-commerce, SMS, and event management that may benefit businesses needing a more comprehensive solution.

Which is cheaper, Moosend or Constant Contact?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, with a free tier supporting up to 1,000 subscribers and unlimited emails. Constant Contact has no free option and starts at $12/month. For cost-conscious businesses, Moosend is the clear winner.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Moosend?

Yes, both platforms support list imports, making migration straightforward. You can export your contacts from Constant Contact and import them into Moosend. However, campaign history and templates will need to be recreated manually in most cases.
