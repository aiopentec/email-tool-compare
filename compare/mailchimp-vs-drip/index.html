Frequently asked questions

Is Mailchimp better than Drip?

It depends on your use case. Mailchimp wins for beginners and small businesses, while Drip is stronger for e-commerce businesses wanting deep store integration. Neither is objectively better — the right choice depends on your specific needs.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Drip?

Mailchimp starts at a lower price point ($13/mo vs $39/mo). However, pricing scales with subscriber count, so the total cost depends on your list size.

Can I switch from Drip to Mailchimp?

Yes, switching from Drip to Mailchimp is possible. Most platforms support CSV import/export for subscriber lists. Plan for some time to recreate automations and templates, as these rarely transfer directly between platforms.
