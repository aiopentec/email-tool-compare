Mailchimp vs Drip: Detailed Comparison for 2025

Mailchimp and Drip are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and business needs. Mailchimp excels as an accessible, beginner-friendly option with a free tier, while Drip specializes in e-commerce automation with deeper store integration. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right platform for your business goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than Drip?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Mailchimp is better for beginners and small businesses due to its free tier and ease of use. Drip is better for e-commerce businesses that need sophisticated product tracking and revenue attribution. Mailchimp also has stronger Trustpilot reviews, while Drip scores slightly higher on G2.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Drip?

Mailchimp is significantly cheaper, especially if you're just starting out—it offers a free tier for up to 500 contacts. Drip has no free option and starts at $39/month. However, if you have many subscribers, Drip's pricing structure may eventually become competitive depending on your contact volume.

Can I switch from Drip to Mailchimp?

Yes, most email marketing platforms allow you to export your contact lists and import them into another service. Both Mailchimp and Drip support standard export/import formats. However, you may lose some automation workflows and custom data during the transition, so plan accordingly.
