Frequently Asked Questions

Is Klaviyo better than Drip?

Klaviyo edges ahead with a free tier, better G2 rating (4.6 vs 4.4), superior predictive analytics, and stronger Shopify integration. However, Drip excels at revenue attribution and starts $6 cheaper per month. The best choice depends on whether you prioritize cost savings or advanced features.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or Drip?

Drip has a lower entry price at $39/month versus Klaviyo's $45/month. However, Klaviyo's free tier accommodates up to 500 contacts, making it cheaper for small businesses testing email marketing. For established stores, Drip's slightly lower pricing may provide better value.

Can I switch from Drip to Klaviyo?

Yes, both platforms support contact list imports, making migration straightforward. You can export your subscriber data from Drip and import it into Klaviyo while preserving segments and custom fields. Plan your automation rebuilds in advance, as flows and workflows will need to be recreated in the new platform.
