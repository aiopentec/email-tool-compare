MailerLite vs Omnisend: Detailed Comparison

MailerLite and Omnisend are both popular email marketing platforms with free tiers, but they serve different audiences. MailerLite focuses on simplicity and affordability for small businesses, while Omnisend specializes in omnichannel marketing for e-commerce brands. Understanding their strengths will help you choose the right tool for your needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than Omnisend?

It depends on your needs. MailerLite is better for budget-conscious small businesses and freelancers seeking simplicity, with a more generous free tier and lower pricing. Omnisend is better for e-commerce brands that need integrated SMS and omnichannel capabilities. Both have strong ratings (MailerLite: 4.6/5 on G2, Omnisend: 4.6/5 on Trustpilot).

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Omnisend?

MailerLite is more affordable overall. It offers a free tier for 1,000 subscribers and starts paid plans at $9/month, while Omnisend limits free subscribers to 250 and starts at $16/month. For budget-conscious users, MailerLite provides better value.

Can I switch from Omnisend to MailerLite?

Yes, you can migrate your email list from Omnisend to MailerLite. Both platforms support standard CSV exports and imports. However, you'll lose SMS and push notification data during the switch, so plan accordingly if those features are important to your campaigns.
