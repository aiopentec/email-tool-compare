Frequently asked questions

Is Klaviyo better than Drip?

Klaviyo edges ahead with higher customer ratings (4.6 vs 4.4 on G2), a free tier for startups, and predictive analytics capabilities. However, 'better' depends on your needs—Drip excels at revenue attribution and may suit businesses prioritizing detailed ecommerce tracking. For most users, Klaviyo offers more flexibility and features at comparable price points.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or Drip?

Drip has a lower starting price at $39/month with no free option, while Klaviyo starts at $45/month but includes a free tier up to 500 subscribers. For small businesses under 500 contacts, Klaviyo's free plan makes it significantly cheaper. At larger scales, pricing becomes comparable, though the total cost depends on your subscriber count and feature needs.

Can I switch from Drip to Klaviyo?

Yes, switching is possible. Both platforms support CSV imports and integrations that allow you to migrate your subscriber lists and contact data. However, automation workflows and campaign history typically need to be manually recreated. Most migrations can be completed in a few hours to days, depending on complexity.
