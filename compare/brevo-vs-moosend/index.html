Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo better than Moosend?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Brevo excels for businesses needing multi-channel marketing, CRM, and transactional emails, while Moosend is ideal for small businesses seeking affordable, unlimited email sending with strong automation. Brevo has more features overall, while Moosend offers better value for email-only campaigns.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Moosend?

Moosend is cheaper for paid plans, starting at $9/month versus Brevo's $25/month. However, Brevo's free tier accommodates 100,000 contacts compared to Moosend's 1,000, making Brevo more cost-effective for growing businesses staying on the free plan. The best choice depends on your subscriber count and budget.

Can I switch from Moosend to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your contact lists and campaign data. Most email marketing tools support CSV/standard data formats, making migration relatively straightforward. However, you may need to manually recreate automation workflows and customize templates in your new platform.

Which platform has better customer reviews?

Moosend has slightly higher ratings on both G2 (4.7 vs 4.5) and Trustpilot (4.4 vs 4.3). These differences are marginal, suggesting both platforms deliver solid user experiences. Your experience will largely depend on your specific use case and expectations.

Does Brevo or Moosend offer unlimited emails?

Moosend offers unlimited emails on all plans, which is ideal for high-volume senders. Brevo's unlimited email feature depends on your specific plan tier. If unlimited email volume is critical, Moosend is the clearer choice.
