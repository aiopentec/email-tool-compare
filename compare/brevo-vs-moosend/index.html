Brevo vs Moosend: Detailed Comparison

Both Brevo and Moosend are popular freemium email marketing platforms with strong reputations, but they serve different business needs. Brevo excels as an all-in-one CRM suite with multi-channel capabilities, while Moosend focuses on delivering unlimited emails and automation at budget-friendly prices. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right tool for your marketing strategy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo better than Moosend?

It depends on your needs. Brevo is better for businesses wanting an all-in-one CRM suite with multi-channel marketing, while Moosend excels at providing unlimited emails and automation at lower costs. Brevo has a slightly higher G2 rating (4.5 vs 4.7), but Moosend edges ahead on Trustpilot (4.4 vs 4.3).

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Moosend?

Moosend is cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to Brevo's $25/month. Additionally, Moosend's free plan offers unlimited emails, while Brevo's free tier has usage limits. However, Brevo's free plan supports significantly more subscribers (100,000 vs 1,000).

Can I switch from Moosend to Brevo?

Yes, most email marketing platforms including Brevo support importing contacts and email lists from other services. You should export your subscriber lists, automation workflows, and campaign templates from Moosend, then import them into Brevo. Check both platforms' migration documentation for specific step-by-step instructions.
