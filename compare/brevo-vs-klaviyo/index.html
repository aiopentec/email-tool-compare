Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo better than Klaviyo?

It depends on your needs. Brevo is better for businesses wanting an affordable, multi-channel CRM with generous free tiers and transactional email capabilities. Klaviyo excels for e-commerce brands seeking specialized Shopify/WooCommerce integrations and predictive analytics. Both have strong ratings, with Klaviyo slightly higher on G2 (4.6 vs 4.5) but Brevo stronger on Trustpilot (4.3 vs 3.8).

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Klaviyo?

Brevo is significantly cheaper, starting at $25/month compared to Klaviyo's $45/month. More importantly, Brevo's free tier allows up to 100,000 contacts, while Klaviyo limits free users to just 500 contacts. This makes Brevo the clear winner for budget-conscious businesses and startups.

Can I switch from Klaviyo to Brevo?

Yes, you can migrate from Klaviyo to Brevo. Both platforms support contact list imports and standard email formats. However, you may lose some Klaviyo-specific features like predictive analytics during the switch. We recommend planning your migration carefully and testing workflows before fully transitioning.
