Frequently asked questions

Is Mailchimp better than AWeber?

Mailchimp offers more advanced features and automation capabilities, making it better for growth-focused businesses. However, AWeber excels in simplicity and has significantly higher customer satisfaction ratings. The best choice depends on whether you prioritize features or ease of use.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or AWeber?

Both platforms offer identical free tiers (up to 500 subscribers). Mailchimp's paid plans start at $13/month while AWeber starts at $15/month, giving Mailchimp a slight pricing advantage. However, both are competitively priced for small businesses and solopreneurs.

Can I switch from AWeber to Mailchimp?

Yes, both platforms support contact list imports and exports. Most migration should be straightforward, though you may need to recreate automation workflows manually. Both companies offer customer support resources to help with the transition process.
