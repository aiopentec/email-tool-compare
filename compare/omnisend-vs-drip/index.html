Which Should You Choose?

Choose Omnisend if…

  • You need deep e-commerce store integration with advanced revenue attribution tracking
  • You prefer a paid-only model without a free tier
  • Your primary focus is email and SMS without push notifications

Choose Drip if…

  • You want to start free and test before paying
  • You need omnichannel automation including push notifications
  • You prefer a lower entry price and higher customer satisfaction ratings
  • You want pre-built workflows to save setup time

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Drip better than Omnisend?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. Omnisend offers better value with a free tier, higher ratings, and more channels (including push notifications). Drip excels if you need advanced e-commerce tracking and revenue attribution. For most growing e-commerce stores, Omnisend provides more flexibility and features at a lower cost.

Which is cheaper, Drip or Omnisend?

Omnisend is significantly cheaper. It offers a free plan for up to 250 subscribers, while paid plans start at $16/month. Drip has no free tier and starts at $39/month. If you're budget-conscious or just starting out, Omnisend is the more affordable choice.

Can I switch from Omnisend to Drip?

Yes, both platforms support standard email export and data migration. Most e-commerce platforms like Shopify work with both tools, making migration straightforward. However, you may need to rebuild automation workflows from scratch, as the builders have different interfaces and features.
