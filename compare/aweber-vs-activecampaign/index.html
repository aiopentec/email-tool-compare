ActiveCampaign combines email with advanced automation and CRM capabilities, while AWeber prioritizes simplicity and accessibility for smaller businesses. Frequently Asked Questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than AWeber?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign is better for businesses requiring advanced automation, CRM, and lead scoring. AWeber is better for those prioritizing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. ActiveCampaign scores higher on review sites (4.5 vs 4.2 on G2), but this reflects its feature depth rather than overall superiority.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or AWeber?

AWeber is cheaper for small businesses, offering a free tier for up to 500 subscribers with no payment required. Both platforms start at $15/month for paid plans, but AWeber's free option makes it the more affordable choice initially. For larger subscriber bases, pricing differences vary by volume.

Can I switch from AWeber to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, both platforms support importing contacts and email lists from competitors. AWeber provides export functionality, and ActiveCampaign accepts CSV imports and integrations. However, you may lose some historical data like automation workflows, so plan your migration carefully and test before fully switching.
