ActiveCampaign vs GetResponse: Detailed Feature & Pricing Comparison

Both ActiveCampaign and GetResponse are established email marketing platforms with automation capabilities, but they serve different business needs. ActiveCampaign emphasizes CRM and advanced automation, while GetResponse focuses on webinars and course creation. Choosing between them depends on your specific use case, budget, and technical requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than GetResponse?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign is superior for CRM-focused businesses requiring advanced automation and lead scoring, with higher G2 ratings overall. GetResponse is better if you need webinars, course hosting, and landing pages in one platform. Consider your specific features and use case before deciding.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or GetResponse?

GetResponse is cheaper overall—it offers a free plan with up to 500 subscribers, while ActiveCampaign has no free tier and starts at $15/month. However, ActiveCampaign's paid plans may offer better value if advanced CRM and automation are essential to your business.

Can I switch from GetResponse to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, most email marketing platforms support data export and import. Both tools allow you to export your subscriber lists and campaign data. However, you may lose some automation workflows and dynamic content during migration, so plan the transition carefully and test thoroughly before switching completely.
