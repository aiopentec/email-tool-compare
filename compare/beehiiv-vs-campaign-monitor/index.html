Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Campaign Monitor?

It depends on your use case. Beehiiv is better for individual newsletter creators and publishers seeking monetization features, while Campaign Monitor is superior for agencies managing multiple client accounts. Beehiiv has higher user ratings overall, but Campaign Monitor offers more robust enterprise features.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Campaign Monitor?

Beehiiv is cheaper initially because it offers a free tier supporting up to 2,500 subscribers at no cost. Campaign Monitor starts at $11/month with no free option. However, once you exceed Beehiiv's free limits, pricing becomes competitive between the two platforms.

Can I switch from Campaign Monitor to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list imports and migrations. However, you'll need to manually recreate automated workflows and custom segments, as they use different automation architectures. Campaign Monitor's journey designer is more complex than Beehiiv's, so migration may require workflow redesign.
