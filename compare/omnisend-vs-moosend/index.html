Moosend vs Omnisend: Detailed Comparison

Both Moosend and Omnisend are freemium email marketing platforms, but they serve different business needs. Moosend focuses on unlimited emails at low cost for general businesses, while Omnisend specializes in omnichannel marketing (email + SMS) for e-commerce brands. Your choice depends on whether you need multi-channel capabilities or a budget-friendly email-only solution.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Moosend better than Omnisend?

It depends on your needs. Moosend is better for budget-conscious businesses wanting unlimited emails, while Omnisend is better for e-commerce brands needing omnichannel marketing. Moosend has a slight edge in overall ratings (4.7 vs 4.5 on G2), but Omnisend excels in specialized e-commerce features.

Which is cheaper, Moosend or Omnisend?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to Omnisend's $16/month. Moosend also offers a larger free tier (1,000 vs 250 subscribers) and unlimited emails at all price points, making it the more budget-friendly option.

Can I switch from Omnisend to Moosend?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list exports and integrations. However, you should verify that custom automation workflows and SMS campaigns can be manually recreated or exported. Most features transfer easily, but advanced Omnisend-specific automations may require reconfiguration.
