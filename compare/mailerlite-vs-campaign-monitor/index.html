MailerLite vs Campaign Monitor: Detailed Comparison

MailerLite and Campaign Monitor are both established email marketing platforms, but they cater to different audiences and use cases. MailerLite emphasizes affordability and ease of use for small businesses, while Campaign Monitor focuses on design quality and multi-brand management for agencies. Understanding their key differences will help you choose the right tool for your specific needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than Campaign Monitor?

It depends on your needs. MailerLite excels for small businesses seeking affordability and simplicity, with a strong free tier and better user ratings. Campaign Monitor is superior for agencies managing multiple brands with advanced segmentation and transactional email features.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Campaign Monitor?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, starting at $9/month with a free tier supporting up to 1,000 subscribers. Campaign Monitor has no free tier and starts at $11/month, making it less accessible for budget-conscious users.

Can I switch from Campaign Monitor to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms support importing subscriber lists and email data. Most users can migrate their contacts and campaigns within hours, though you may need to rebuild automation workflows depending on complexity.
