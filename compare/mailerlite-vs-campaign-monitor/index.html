Choosing between them depends on your business size, budget, and specific feature needs.

Which Should You Choose?

Choose MailerLite if…

  • You're a small business or freelancer starting with a limited budget and want to test email marketing free
  • You need e-commerce integrations, landing pages, and pop-ups built into one platform
  • You value ease of use and beginner-friendly features with strong customer support

Choose Campaign Monitor if…

  • You manage multiple client brands and need dedicated multi-brand management features
  • You require advanced journey automation and smart segmentation capabilities
  • You send transactional emails and need professional-grade deliverability features

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than Campaign Monitor?

It depends on your needs. MailerLite is better for small businesses and budget-conscious users thanks to its free tier and lower starting price. Campaign Monitor is better for agencies managing multiple brands with advanced automation needs. MailerLite has higher customer ratings overall (4.6 vs 4.1 on G2).

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Campaign Monitor?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, offering a free plan for up to 1,000 subscribers with no credit card required. Campaign Monitor has no free tier and starts at $11/month. MailerLite's paid plans begin at $9/month, making it the more affordable option for most users.

Can I switch from Campaign Monitor to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms support importing subscriber lists and email data. MailerLite provides import tools and customer support to help you migrate. The transition typically takes a few hours depending on your list size and complexity of automations.
