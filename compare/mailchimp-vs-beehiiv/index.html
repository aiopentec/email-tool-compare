Frequently asked questions

Is Mailchimp better than Beehiiv?

It depends on your use case. Mailchimp is better for small businesses and e-commerce with its broader feature set and lower pricing, while Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers wanting to monetize their audience. Beehiiv has higher customer satisfaction ratings (4.7 vs 4.3 on G2), but Mailchimp offers more traditional marketing automation features.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Beehiiv?

Mailchimp is cheaper overall, starting at $13/month for paid plans compared to Beehiiv's $39/month. However, Beehiiv offers a more generous free tier with 2,500 subscribers versus Mailchimp's 500, making Beehiiv better value if you stay on the free plan longer.

Can I switch from Beehiiv to Mailchimp?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists, though the process and data fields transferred may vary. Most email platforms support CSV exports, but you should verify that all custom fields and segments transfer correctly before switching.
