Frequently asked questions

Is Moosend better than Flodesk?

It depends on your use case. Moosend wins for small businesses wanting unlimited emails at low cost, while Flodesk is stronger for visual brands, photographers, and designers. Neither is objectively better — the right choice depends on your specific needs.

Which is cheaper, Moosend or Flodesk?

Moosend starts at a lower price point ($9/mo vs $38/mo). However, pricing scales with subscriber count, so the total cost depends on your list size.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Moosend?

Yes, switching from Flodesk to Moosend is possible. Most platforms support CSV import/export for subscriber lists. Plan for some time to recreate automations and templates, as these rarely transfer directly between platforms.
