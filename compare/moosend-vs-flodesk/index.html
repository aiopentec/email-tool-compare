Moosend vs Flodesk: Detailed Comparison

Moosend and Flodesk are both solid email marketing platforms, but they serve different needs and audiences. Moosend excels at delivering affordable automation for growing businesses, while Flodesk focuses on beautiful design-first templates for creative professionals. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize cost-effectiveness and advanced automation or stunning visual design and simplicity.

Frequently asked questions

Is Moosend better than Flodesk?

It depends on your priorities. Moosend is better for budget-conscious businesses needing advanced automation and A/B testing at low cost. Flodesk is better for creative professionals who prioritize beautiful email design and want straightforward flat-rate pricing. Both have excellent customer ratings (4.7 vs 4.8 on G2).

Which is cheaper, Moosend or Flodesk?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, starting at $0 for a free plan with 1,000 subscribers and unlimited emails. Flodesk has no free tier and starts at $38/month flat-rate regardless of subscriber count. For budget-focused businesses, Moosend is the clear winner; Flodesk's pricing only makes sense if you value its design features.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Moosend?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list exports and imports. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your subscriber list and import it elsewhere. However, you may need to manually recreate your email designs and automation workflows, so plan for some migration time.
