Mailchimp vs Omnisend: Detailed Comparison for 2025

Mailchimp and Omnisend are both freemium email marketing platforms, but they cater to different business needs. Mailchimp excels as an all-in-one marketing solution for beginners and small businesses, while Omnisend specializes in omnichannel campaigns for e-commerce brands. Understanding their key differences will help you choose the right tool for your marketing goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than Omnisend?

It depends on your business type. Mailchimp is better for beginners and general small businesses due to its user-friendly interface and larger free tier. Omnisend is better for e-commerce brands that need integrated SMS and email campaigns. Omnisend also has significantly higher customer satisfaction ratings on Trustpilot.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Omnisend?

Mailchimp has a lower starting price at $13/month compared to Omnisend's $16/month. Both offer free tiers, though Mailchimp allows 500 free contacts versus Omnisend's 250. For budget-conscious businesses, Mailchimp's free tier provides more room to grow before upgrading.

Can I switch from Omnisend to Mailchimp?

Yes, you can export your contact lists and campaign data from Omnisend and import them into Mailchimp. Both platforms support standard export formats. However, Omnisend's SMS and push notification capabilities won't transfer, so you'll need an alternative solution if those channels are critical to your strategy.
