MailerLite vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison for 2025

MailerLite and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are two popular email marketing platforms serving different business needs. MailerLite focuses on simplicity and ease-of-use for small businesses and freelancers, while Sendinblue emphasizes multi-channel communications with SMS, WhatsApp, and transactional email capabilities. Choosing between them depends on your budget, feature requirements, and whether you need advanced CRM functionality.

Which Should You Choose?

Choose MailerLite if…

  • You're a small business or freelancer prioritizing ease-of-use and affordability
  • You need landing pages, pop-ups, and surveys built into your email platform
  • You want the lowest entry price for paid plans ($9/month)

Choose Sendinblue if…

  • You need SMS and WhatsApp messaging alongside email marketing
  • You require a robust free tier that supports up to 100,000 subscribers
  • You need transactional email capabilities and integrated CRM functionality

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than Sendinblue?

It depends on your needs. MailerLite is better for beginners and small e-commerce businesses that prioritize simplicity and built-in tools like landing pages. Sendinblue excels if you need multi-channel communication (SMS, WhatsApp) and transactional email with a generous free tier for high-volume senders.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Sendinblue?

For paid plans, MailerLite is cheaper at $9/month vs. Sendinblue's $25/month. However, Sendinblue offers a significantly more generous free tier with 100,000 subscribers and 300 free emails daily, making it the better choice if you want to avoid paid plans entirely.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms support subscriber list exports and imports. Most email marketing platforms allow you to export your contacts in standard formats (CSV). You can then import your list into MailerLite, though you may need to re-setup your campaigns and automations manually.
