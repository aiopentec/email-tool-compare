MailerLite vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

MailerLite and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are two popular freemium email marketing platforms, but they serve different needs. MailerLite excels at simplicity and affordability for small businesses, while Sendinblue offers a broader all-in-one CRM suite with SMS and transactional email capabilities. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right tool for your marketing strategy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than Sendinblue?

It depends on your needs. MailerLite is better for small businesses seeking simplicity and e-commerce tools, while Sendinblue (Brevo) excels for those needing transactional email, SMS, and omnichannel marketing. Both have strong ratings, with MailerLite slightly ahead on G2 (4.6 vs 4.5) and Trustpilot (4.5 vs 4.3).

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Sendinblue?

MailerLite is cheaper for small subscriber lists, starting at $9/month vs Sendinblue's $25/month. However, Sendinblue's free tier accommodates up to 100,000 subscribers with 300 free emails daily, making it better for growing businesses that don't need paid features immediately.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to MailerLite?

Yes, switching is straightforward. Both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and set up redirects for existing campaigns. MailerLite provides import tools to migrate contacts quickly. Plan your transition timing to avoid email delivery gaps during the switch.
