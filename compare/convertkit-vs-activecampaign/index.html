Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertKit better than ActiveCampaign?

It depends on your needs. ConvertKit is better for creators and bloggers who want a free tier with commerce tools, while ActiveCampaign is superior for businesses needing CRM, advanced automation, and SMS capabilities. ConvertKit has lower Trustpilot ratings (3.2 vs 4.1), suggesting user satisfaction concerns, but remains excellent for its target audience.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or ActiveCampaign?

ConvertKit offers a free tier for up to 1,000 subscribers, making it cheaper to start. However, ActiveCampaign begins at just $15/month, undercutting ConvertKit's paid plans which start at $29/month. For small budgets, ConvertKit's free tier wins; for paid plans, ActiveCampaign offers better value.

Can I switch from ActiveCampaign to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and email sequences, though you may need to manually rebuild automations as they use different builders. ConvertKit's visual editor is simpler, so complex ActiveCampaign workflows may need redesigning. Plan for migration time and test thoroughly before switching.
