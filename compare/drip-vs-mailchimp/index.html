Mailchimp vs Drip: Email Marketing Platform Comparison

Mailchimp and Drip are both popular email marketing platforms, but they cater to different business types and budgets. Mailchimp offers a generous free tier ideal for beginners and small businesses, while Drip specializes in e-commerce integration with advanced automation. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right tool for your marketing goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than Drip?

It depends on your needs. Mailchimp is better for beginners and budget-conscious businesses due to its free tier and simplicity. Drip is better for e-commerce businesses that need advanced tracking, revenue attribution, and store integration. While both have strong ratings, Drip edges out with slightly better G2 reviews and significantly higher Trustpilot satisfaction.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Drip?

Mailchimp is significantly cheaper, with a free plan supporting up to 500 subscribers and paid plans starting at $13/month. Drip has no free tier and starts at $39/month. For budget-conscious businesses, Mailchimp is the clear winner on price, though Drip's premium features may justify the cost for e-commerce sellers.

Can I switch from Drip to Mailchimp?

Yes, most email marketing platforms support contact list exports, allowing you to migrate your subscriber data. Both Mailchimp and Drip provide standard export options, though you may lose some automation history during the transition. It's recommended to back up your data and test the import before fully switching.
