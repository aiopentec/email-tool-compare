Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than ActiveCampaign?

Neither is universally 'better'—it depends on your needs. Mailchimp excels for beginners and small businesses due to its free tier and simplicity, earning a lower Trustpilot rating (1.5) but strong G2 score (4.3). ActiveCampaign is better for businesses needing advanced automation and CRM, with superior customer satisfaction ratings (4.1 Trustpilot, 4.5 G2) but no free option.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or ActiveCampaign?

Mailchimp is cheaper overall, offering a completely free tier for up to 500 contacts and starting paid plans at $13/month. ActiveCampaign has no free tier and starts at $15/month. For cost-conscious startups, Mailchimp is the clear winner.

Can I switch from ActiveCampaign to Mailchimp?

Yes, you can export your contacts and data from ActiveCampaign and import them into Mailchimp. However, some advanced automation workflows and CRM data may not transfer perfectly. Most users can successfully migrate their email lists and basic campaign data between the two platforms.
