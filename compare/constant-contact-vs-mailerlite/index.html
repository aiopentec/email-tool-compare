Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than Constant Contact?

MailerLite generally offers better value for small businesses and beginners due to its generous free tier and lower pricing. However, Constant Contact may be preferable if you specifically need SMS marketing and event management features. The best choice depends on your specific feature requirements and budget.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Constant Contact?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to Constant Contact's $12/month. More importantly, MailerLite offers a free plan for up to 1,000 subscribers, while Constant Contact has no free tier. This makes MailerLite the clear winner for budget-conscious businesses.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms support list imports and exports, making migration relatively straightforward. Most email marketing platforms can import CSV files or connect via integrations, though you'll need to manually recreate automation workflows and templates in your new platform.
