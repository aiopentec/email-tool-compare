Your choice depends on whether you need enterprise marketing automation or a simple way to monetize your writing.

Frequently asked questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Substack?

Neither is universally better—they solve different problems. ActiveCampaign excels for businesses needing marketing automation, CRM, and advanced segmentation. Substack is superior for creators seeking simplicity and direct subscriber monetization. Choose based on your primary goal: business growth through automation or content monetization.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Substack?

Substack is cheaper upfront, offering unlimited free use with no subscription required. ActiveCampaign starts at $15/month with no free tier. However, if you monetize on Substack, the platform takes a 10% cut of paid subscriptions, which can become expensive at scale compared to ActiveCampaign's fixed pricing.

Can I switch from Substack to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber list from Substack to ActiveCampaign, though the process requires manual export and import. ActiveCampaign accepts CSV uploads of your contacts. However, you'll lose Substack's built-in monetization features and discovery mechanisms, so ensure ActiveCampaign's capabilities align with your new strategy.
