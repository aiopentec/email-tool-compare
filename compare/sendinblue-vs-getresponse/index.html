GetResponse vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

Both GetResponse and Sendinblue (now Brevo) offer freemium email marketing platforms with distinct strengths. GetResponse excels for course creators and webinar hosts, while Sendinblue focuses on transactional email and multi-channel communication. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize marketing automation or comprehensive CRM and SMS capabilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GetResponse better than Sendinblue?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. GetResponse excels for digital marketers and course creators needing webinars and landing pages, while Sendinblue (Brevo) is superior for businesses needing SMS, transactional email, and CRM features. Sendinblue has higher user ratings overall.

Which is cheaper, GetResponse or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue offers better free tier value with 100,000 free contacts versus GetResponse's 500. However, paid plans are similarly priced ($19 vs $25), so your total cost depends on contact volume and features needed rather than the base subscription price.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to GetResponse?

Yes, both platforms support contact list exports and imports. Most email marketing platforms allow you to export your subscriber data in CSV format and import it elsewhere. Plan the migration during a low-activity period and test your automations thoroughly after switching.
