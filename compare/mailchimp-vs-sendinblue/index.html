Mailchimp vs Sendinblue: Complete Comparison Guide

Mailchimp and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are two of the most popular email marketing platforms, each with distinct strengths. Mailchimp excels in ease-of-use and automation for small businesses, while Sendinblue offers more advanced features like SMS, WhatsApp, and transactional email at scale. Choosing between them depends on your specific needs, budget, and whether you require multi-channel marketing capabilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than Sendinblue?

It depends on your needs. Mailchimp is better for beginners due to its intuitive interface and strong automation features, while Sendinblue excels for businesses needing multi-channel marketing (email, SMS, WhatsApp) and transactional capabilities. Sendinblue has higher overall ratings on review platforms, suggesting better customer satisfaction.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue offers better free-tier value with 100,000 subscribers versus Mailchimp's 500, though both are free. For paid plans, Mailchimp starts at $13/month while Sendinblue begins at $25/month. Your actual cost depends on subscriber count and features needed.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Mailchimp?

Yes, most email marketing platforms allow you to export your contact lists and migrate to competitors. Both platforms support standard CSV/data export formats. You may need to rebuild automation workflows and templates manually, as these typically don't transfer between platforms.
