Beehiiv vs Flodesk: Detailed Comparison

Beehiiv and Flodesk are both modern email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and business models. Beehiiv targets newsletter publishers with growth-focused monetization features, while Flodesk emphasizes beautiful design and visual branding. Choosing between them depends on whether you prioritize revenue generation or aesthetic email experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Flodesk?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your priorities. Beehiiv excels at newsletter monetization and growth tracking, making it ideal for publishers. Flodesk is superior for design-focused brands and offers better value with unlimited subscribers at a flat rate. Consider your specific business model before deciding.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Flodesk?

Beehiiv is cheaper for small publishers with a free tier supporting 2,500 subscribers at no cost. However, Flodesk's $38/month flat-rate pricing becomes more economical if you have thousands of subscribers, since Beehiiv's paid plans start at $39/month with higher costs for larger lists.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Beehiiv?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber list from Flodesk to Beehiiv, as both platforms support standard email list exports and imports. However, you'll need to recreate your email templates and workflows in the new platform, as design files don't transfer directly between them.
