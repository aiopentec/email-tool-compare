Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than MailerLite?

Neither is objectively "better" — it depends on your needs. MailerLite offers superior value for budget-conscious users with lower prices, a larger free tier, and significantly higher customer satisfaction ratings (4.5 vs 1.5 on Trustpilot). Mailchimp excels for businesses needing an all-in-one platform with advanced enterprise features, though it has received more negative recent reviews.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or MailerLite?

MailerLite is cheaper overall. It starts at $9/month compared to Mailchimp's $13/month, and offers double the free subscriber limit (1,000 vs 500). For growing teams, MailerLite's pricing structure typically remains more affordable across all tiers.

Can I switch from MailerLite to Mailchimp?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list exports and imports. You can export your contacts and campaign history from MailerLite and import them into Mailchimp. Most modern email marketing tools support CSV or API-based migrations, though you may lose some automation workflows that need to be recreated.
