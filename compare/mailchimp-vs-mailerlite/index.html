Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than MailerLite?

MailerLite generally offers better value, with lower pricing, higher subscriber limits on the free tier, and superior customer reviews on Trustpilot (4.5 vs 1.5). However, Mailchimp may be preferable for larger enterprises needing extensive integrations. For most small businesses and freelancers, MailerLite is the better choice.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or MailerLite?

MailerLite is cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to Mailchimp's $13/month. Additionally, MailerLite offers a larger free tier with 1,000 subscribers versus Mailchimp's 500, making it more budget-friendly for startups and small businesses.

Can I switch from MailerLite to Mailchimp?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber lists between the two platforms. Most email marketing tools support CSV exports and imports, allowing you to transfer your contacts, campaigns, and data. Both Mailchimp and MailerLite provide documentation for migration, though you may need to rebuild automation workflows manually.
