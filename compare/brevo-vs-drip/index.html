Brevo vs Drip: Detailed Comparison

Brevo and Drip are both established email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and use cases. Brevo offers a freemium all-in-one CRM suite with multi-channel capabilities, while Drip specializes in e-commerce automation with deep store integrations. Choosing between them depends on your budget, business type, and feature requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo better than Drip?

It depends on your needs. Brevo excels for businesses needing a free tier and multi-channel marketing, while Drip is superior for e-commerce-specific automation and revenue attribution. Brevo has higher overall ratings on G2 and Trustpilot, but Drip's features are more specialized for online stores.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Drip?

Brevo is significantly cheaper, offering a robust free tier supporting 100,000 contacts with no payment required. Drip has no free tier and starts at $39/month. If you need paid features, Brevo's paid plans begin at $25/month compared to Drip's $39/month minimum.

Can I switch from Drip to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms typically support contact list exports and integrations with third-party migration tools. However, Drip's e-commerce-specific automation workflows may not have direct equivalents in Brevo, so you may need to reconfigure some automations. It's recommended to plan your migration carefully to avoid losing campaign data.
