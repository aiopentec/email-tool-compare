Your choice depends on whether you prioritize cost-effectiveness and multi-channel capabilities or advanced e-commerce tracking and revenue attribution.

Frequently asked questions

Is Brevo better than Drip?

It depends on your needs. Brevo is better for cost-conscious businesses needing multi-channel marketing, while Drip excels for e-commerce stores requiring advanced sales tracking. Brevo rates slightly higher on Trustpilot (4.3 vs 3.5) and offers a free tier, making it more accessible to startups.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Drip?

Brevo is significantly cheaper, with a free tier supporting up to 100,000 contacts and paid plans starting at $25/month. Drip has no free option and starts at $39/month. For small businesses, Brevo can be used completely free indefinitely.

Can I switch from Drip to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms support contact list imports via CSV or API connections. The migration process typically involves exporting your contacts and automation workflows from Drip and rebuilding automations in Brevo. Most data transfers smoothly, though workflow logic may need adjustment due to interface differences.
