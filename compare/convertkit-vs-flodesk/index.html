Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertKit better than Flodesk?

Neither is objectively 'better'—they excel in different areas. Flodesk has superior design capabilities and higher customer satisfaction ratings, while ConvertKit offers more automation power and ecommerce features. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize visual design or functionality.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Flodesk?

ConvertKit is cheaper if you're starting out, thanks to its free tier supporting up to 1,000 subscribers. Once paid plans start, ConvertKit begins at $29/month while Flodesk starts at $38/month, but Flodesk includes unlimited subscribers at that price point whereas ConvertKit charges based on list size.

Can I switch from Flodesk to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and email data, making migration straightforward. Most email marketing tools support standard import/export formats, though you may need to rebuild automations and customize templates in your new platform.
