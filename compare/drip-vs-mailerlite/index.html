Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than Drip?

It depends on your needs. MailerLite is better for budget-conscious small businesses thanks to its free tier and lower pricing, while Drip is superior for e-commerce stores requiring advanced tracking and revenue attribution. MailerLite has higher customer satisfaction ratings overall.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Drip?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, offering a free plan for up to 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $9/month. Drip has no free tier and starts at $39/month, making it a premium option best suited for established e-commerce businesses.

Can I switch from Drip to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list imports and exports, making migration relatively straightforward. However, complex automation workflows may require manual reconfiguration in MailerLite, so plan accordingly for the transition.
