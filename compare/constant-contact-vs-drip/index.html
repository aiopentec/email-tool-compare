Drip vs Constant Contact: Detailed Comparison

Both Drip and Constant Contact are established email marketing platforms, but they cater to different business needs. Drip specializes in e-commerce automation with advanced revenue tracking, while Constant Contact offers broader digital marketing features for local businesses and nonprofits. Understanding their strengths will help you choose the right tool for your specific goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Drip better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your needs. Drip is superior for e-commerce businesses with its advanced tracking and automation, earning higher ratings (4.4 vs 4.0 on G2). However, Constant Contact is better for nonprofits and local businesses seeking simplicity and affordability. Consider your primary business model when deciding.

Which is cheaper, Drip or Constant Contact?

Constant Contact is significantly cheaper, starting at $12/month compared to Drip's $39/month baseline. If budget is your primary concern and you don't need advanced e-commerce features, Constant Contact offers better value. However, Drip's higher price reflects its specialized e-commerce capabilities.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Drip?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list exports and imports. You can export your contacts from Constant Contact as a CSV file and import them into Drip. However, automation workflows and segmentation rules will need to be rebuilt manually in your new platform.
