Frequently Asked Questions

Is Moosend better than AWeber?

Moosend generally offers better value with unlimited emails, advanced automation, and lower starting prices. However, AWeber may suit you better if simplicity and RSS-to-email features are priorities. Your choice depends on whether you need powerful automation or straightforward, easy-to-use functionality.

Which is cheaper, Moosend or AWeber?

Moosend is cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to AWeber's $15/month for paid plans. Additionally, Moosend's free tier supports 1,000 subscribers versus AWeber's 500, making Moosend more cost-effective for growing businesses.

Can I switch from AWeber to Moosend?

Yes, both platforms support importing subscriber lists and email data. Most email marketing platforms allow CSV exports, making migration straightforward. Contact each platform's support team to ensure a smooth data transfer process.
