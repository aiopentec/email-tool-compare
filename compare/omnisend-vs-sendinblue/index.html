Omnisend vs Sendinblue: Complete Comparison

Omnisend and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are both freemium email marketing platforms with SMS capabilities, but they serve different audiences. Omnisend specializes in e-commerce with omnichannel automation, while Sendinblue offers broader CRM and transactional email features. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right tool for your business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Omnisend better than Sendinblue?

It depends on your needs. Omnisend is better for e-commerce brands needing omnichannel automation and SMS, while Sendinblue excels for transactional email and businesses wanting a broader CRM suite. Both have strong ratings (G2: 4.5 each), so choose based on your specific use case.

Which is cheaper, Omnisend or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue offers better free-tier value with 100,000 contacts versus Omnisend's 250. However, Omnisend's paid plans start at $16/month compared to Sendinblue's $25/month. Your actual cost depends on subscriber count and feature needs.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Omnisend?

Yes, both platforms support contact list exports, making migration straightforward. Most email marketing tools allow you to import CSV files with your existing subscriber lists. Check both platforms' migration guides for detailed steps to ensure a smooth transition.
