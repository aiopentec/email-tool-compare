Which Should You Choose?

Choose ActiveCampaign if…

  • You need advanced automation, lead scoring, and CRM capabilities for B2B or complex workflows
  • You want the most affordable option to start email marketing on a tight budget
  • You require SMS marketing, site tracking, and multi-channel campaigns integrated in one platform
  • You're building complex sales funnels and need sophisticated customer journey automation

Choose Flodesk if…

  • You're a visual brand, photographer, designer, or creator who prioritizes beautiful email aesthetics
  • You want unlimited subscriber access with flat-rate pricing regardless of list size
  • You prefer simplicity and ease of use over advanced technical features and automation
  • You need integrated checkout forms and link-in-bio features for selling directly through email

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Flodesk?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign excels in automation, CRM, and advanced features for growing B2B businesses, while Flodesk wins for visual appeal and simplicity. ActiveCampaign has higher customer ratings overall, but Flodesk users report greater satisfaction with design capabilities and ease of use.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Flodesk?

ActiveCampaign starts at $15/month, making it cheaper upfront than Flodesk's $38/month entry price. However, if you have a large subscriber list, ActiveCampaign's per-subscriber model may become expensive, while Flodesk's flat-rate pricing remains constant regardless of list size.

Can I switch from Flodesk to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, both platforms typically support data export and import functions. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your contact lists and campaign data. Check with both platforms' support teams for specific migration instructions to ensure a smooth transition.

Does Flodesk have CRM features like ActiveCampaign?

Flodesk offers basic contact management but lacks the advanced CRM capabilities that ActiveCampaign provides. If you need sophisticated lead scoring, detailed customer tracking, and complex relationship management, ActiveCampaign is the better choice.

Which tool is better for ecommerce?

ActiveCampaign is better for complex ecommerce operations due to its advanced automation, CRM, and integrations. However, Flodesk's checkout forms and visual design capabilities make it suitable for simpler ecommerce operations like selling digital products or services directly from emails.
