Klaviyo vs AWeber: Detailed Comparison for 2025

Klaviyo and AWeber are both established email marketing platforms offering free tiers, but they serve different audiences. Klaviyo excels for e-commerce brands needing advanced automation and integrations, while AWeber appeals to bloggers and solopreneurs seeking simplicity and affordability. Understanding their key differences will help you choose the right platform for your marketing goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Klaviyo better than AWeber?

It depends on your needs. Klaviyo is superior for e-commerce businesses requiring advanced automation and integrations, while AWeber is better for bloggers and small businesses seeking affordability and simplicity. Klaviyo has higher G2 and Trustpilot ratings, reflecting its strength in feature-rich marketing automation.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or AWeber?

AWeber is significantly cheaper, starting at $15/month compared to Klaviyo's $45/month for paid plans. Both offer identical free tiers (500 subscribers), making AWeber the clear choice for budget-conscious users. However, Klaviyo's higher cost reflects its enterprise-grade features.

Can I switch from AWeber to Klaviyo?

Yes, most email marketing platforms support list imports via CSV files. You can export your contacts from AWeber and import them into Klaviyo. However, automation workflows, landing pages, and other assets won't transfer automatically, so you'll need to rebuild those in Klaviyo.
