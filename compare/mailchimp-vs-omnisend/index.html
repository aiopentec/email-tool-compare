Frequently asked questions

Is Mailchimp better than Omnisend?

It depends on your use case. Mailchimp wins for beginners and small businesses, while Omnisend is stronger for e-commerce brands wanting email and SMS together. Neither is objectively better — the right choice depends on your specific needs.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Omnisend?

Mailchimp starts at a lower price point ($13/mo vs $16/mo). However, pricing scales with subscriber count, so the total cost depends on your list size.

Can I switch from Omnisend to Mailchimp?

Yes, switching from Omnisend to Mailchimp is possible. Most platforms support CSV import/export for subscriber lists. Plan for some time to recreate automations and templates, as these rarely transfer directly between platforms.
