Which Should You Choose?

Choose Klaviyo if…

  • You run an e-commerce store on Shopify or WooCommerce and need advanced automation
  • You want SMS marketing, review collection, and predictive analytics built-in
  • You need sophisticated segmentation and customer journey mapping tools

Choose Buttondown if…

  • You're starting with a smaller budget and want an affordable email solution
  • You prefer writing in markdown and need API access for customization
  • You want to launch a simple newsletter or offer paid subscriptions to readers

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Klaviyo better than Buttondown?

It depends on your needs. Klaviyo is more powerful for e-commerce brands requiring advanced automation, segmentation, and SMS capabilities. Buttondown is better for newsletter creators and developers who value simplicity, markdown support, and affordability. Neither is objectively 'better'—they serve different purposes.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or Buttondown?

Buttondown is significantly cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to Klaviyo's $45/month. Buttondown also offers a more generous free tier for scaling from 100 to larger subscriber counts at a lower cost, making it ideal for budget-conscious creators.

Can I switch from Buttondown to Klaviyo?

Yes, Buttondown offers import/export functionality, so you can export your subscriber lists and content. Both platforms support standard email formats, making migration relatively straightforward. However, you'll need to rebuild automation workflows in Klaviyo as the platforms have different automation architectures.
