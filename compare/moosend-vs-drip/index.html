Which Should You Choose?

Choose Moosend if…

  • You run an e-commerce store and need revenue attribution and product tracking
  • You want SMS marketing integrated with your email campaigns
  • You need onsite campaigns and visual automation builders tailored for online retailers

Choose Drip if…

  • You're a small business on a tight budget or want to start free
  • You need unlimited emails and landing pages included in your plan
  • You want strong A/B testing, analytics, and a platform rated highly for ease of use

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Drip better than Moosend?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Drip excels for e-commerce businesses with specific revenue tracking and SMS requirements, while Moosend offers better value for small businesses and non-e-commerce use cases. Moosend has higher user ratings (4.7 vs 4.4 on G2), but Drip's specialized features justify its higher cost for online retailers.

Which is cheaper, Drip or Moosend?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, starting at free for up to 1,000 subscribers with unlimited emails, then $9/month for paid plans. Drip has no free tier and starts at $39/month. For bootstrapped startups and small businesses, Moosend is the clear budget winner.

Can I switch from Moosend to Drip?

Yes, both platforms support list imports and migrations. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your subscriber list and campaign history. Contact support for both platforms to ensure a smooth transition and to verify any specific technical requirements for your migration.
