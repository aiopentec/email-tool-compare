Frequently asked questions

Is Beehiiv better than GetResponse?

Neither is universally 'better'—it depends on your use case. Beehiiv excels for newsletter monetization and publishing, with higher user ratings (4.7 on G2). GetResponse is better for those needing webinars, courses, and marketing automation all-in-one. Choose based on your primary business model.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or GetResponse?

GetResponse is cheaper at $19/month for paid plans, compared to Beehiiv's $39/month. However, Beehiiv offers a more generous free tier (2,500 vs 500 subscribers), which may eliminate costs longer for smaller publishers.

Can I switch from GetResponse to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list exports and imports. Most email marketing tools allow CSV or API-based data migration, though you may lose some automation workflows. Plan your transition carefully to maintain subscriber engagement during the switch.
