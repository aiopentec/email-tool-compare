Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than AWeber?

MailerLite edges ahead with better ratings (4.6 vs 4.2 on G2), more generous free tier (1,000 vs 500 subscribers), and superior e-commerce features. However, AWeber remains solid for bloggers needing RSS-to-email functionality and has deeper industry experience since 1998.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or AWeber?

MailerLite is cheaper with paid plans starting at $9/month compared to AWeber's $15/month. Additionally, MailerLite's free tier supports twice as many subscribers (1,000 vs 500), making it the more budget-friendly choice overall.

Can I switch from AWeber to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and import them into MailerLite. Most migrations are straightforward, though you'll need to manually recreate campaigns and automations. Both platforms offer support to help guide the transition process.
