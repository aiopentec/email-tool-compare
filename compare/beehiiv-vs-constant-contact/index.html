Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your needs. Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers seeking monetization and has higher user satisfaction ratings (4.7 vs 4.0 on G2). Constant Contact is better for small businesses and nonprofits that need e-commerce, SMS, and social media marketing features integrated into one platform.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Constant Contact?

Beehiiv is cheaper if you're just starting out, offering a free tier for up to 2,500 subscribers with no credit card required. Constant Contact starts at $12/month with no free option. However, Beehiiv's paid plans begin at $39/month, so costs depend on your subscriber count and feature needs.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support email list imports, making migration straightforward. You can export your contacts from Constant Contact and import them into Beehiiv. However, you'll need to rebuild any advanced automation workflows, as the platforms have different architecture and feature sets.
