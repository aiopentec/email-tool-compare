Frequently asked questions

Is Beehiiv better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your use case. Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers with monetization needs and has higher user satisfaction ratings. Constant Contact offers broader marketing features like SMS, event management, and e-commerce tools that make it better for small businesses. Choose based on whether you prioritize newsletter growth (Beehiiv) or multi-channel marketing (Constant Contact).

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Constant Contact?

Beehiiv is cheaper initially because it offers a free tier for up to 2,500 subscribers with no payment required. Constant Contact has no free tier and starts at $12/month. However, once you exceed Beehiiv's free limits, pricing becomes competitive, and the best value depends on your subscriber count and feature needs.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms typically support email list imports, making migration straightforward. You can export your subscriber list from Constant Contact and import it into Beehiiv. Before switching, ensure Beehiiv has all the features you need, particularly if you rely on Constant Contact's SMS or e-commerce tools.
