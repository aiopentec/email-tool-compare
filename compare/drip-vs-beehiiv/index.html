Choosing between them depends on your business model and primary use case.

Which Should You Choose?

Choose Drip if…

  • You're launching a newsletter or publication and want to monetize through ads and sponsorships
  • You're starting out and need a free tier with room to grow to 2,500 subscribers
  • You value built-in audience engagement features like web reader and referral programs

Choose Beehiiv if…

  • You run an e-commerce store and need revenue attribution and purchase tracking
  • You want advanced automation workflows with a visual builder for complex customer journeys
  • You need SMS capabilities in addition to email marketing

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Drip?

It depends on your use case. Beehiiv scores higher overall (4.7 vs 4.4 on G2) and offers better value for newsletter publishers with its free tier and monetization features. However, Drip is purpose-built for e-commerce and offers more sophisticated automation and revenue tracking that's essential for online stores.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Drip?

Beehiiv is significantly cheaper because it offers a free tier supporting up to 2,500 subscribers with no payment required. Drip has no free option and starts at $39/month. For budget-conscious publishers, Beehiiv is the clear winner on price.

Can I switch from Drip to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support CSV exports of your subscriber lists and email content, making migration possible. However, you'll need to manually recreate any complex automation workflows in Beehiiv's builder, and note that Beehiiv's strengths are in newsletter publishing rather than e-commerce features.
