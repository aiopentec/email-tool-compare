ConvertKit vs Moosend: Detailed Comparison

ConvertKit and Moosend are both freemium email marketing platforms, but they cater to different audiences. ConvertKit focuses on creators and course sellers with built-in commerce features, while Moosend emphasizes affordability and unlimited emails for small businesses. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize creator-focused tools or low-cost scaling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertKit better than Moosend?

Neither is objectively 'better'—it depends on your needs. ConvertKit excels for creators with e-commerce needs and superior user experience, while Moosend wins on price and unlimited email sending. Moosend has higher ratings on G2 (4.7 vs 4.4) and Trustpilot (4.4 vs 3.2), suggesting strong overall satisfaction.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Moosend?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to ConvertKit's $29/month. Additionally, Moosend includes unlimited emails on all plans, while ConvertKit restricts email volume by tier. For budget-conscious small businesses, Moosend offers better value.

Can I switch from Moosend to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber list, making migration possible. Most email marketing tools support CSV imports, so transferring your contacts and automation workflows between platforms is straightforward, though you may need to manually recreate complex automations.
