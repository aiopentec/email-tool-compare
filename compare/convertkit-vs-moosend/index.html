Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertKit better than Moosend?

It depends on your needs. ConvertKit is better for creators and course sellers who need commerce and advanced automation features, while Moosend is better for cost-conscious small businesses. Moosend actually has higher ratings on G2 (4.7 vs 4.4) and Trustpilot (4.4 vs 3.2), suggesting better overall customer satisfaction.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Moosend?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to ConvertKit's $29/month. Both offer free tiers with 1,000 subscriber limits, but Moosend's affordable pricing makes it ideal for budget-conscious teams. ConvertKit's higher price reflects its focus on creator-specific features.

Can I switch from Moosend to ConvertKit?

Yes, most email marketing platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and migrate to another service. However, you may need to manually rebuild your automation workflows and landing pages, as these don't always transfer directly. It's recommended to plan your migration carefully to avoid losing data or email sequences.
