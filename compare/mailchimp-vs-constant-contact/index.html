Frequently asked questions

Is Mailchimp better than Constant Contact?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Mailchimp excels for budget-conscious beginners with its free tier and strong automation, scoring higher on G2 (4.3 vs 4.0). Constant Contact offers more advanced features like SMS, social ads, and e-commerce, making it better for established businesses needing comprehensive marketing tools.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Constant Contact?

Mailchimp is significantly cheaper if you're starting out, with a free tier supporting 500 subscribers at no cost. Constant Contact has no free tier and starts at $12/month. For paid plans, Mailchimp begins at $13/month, making both competitively priced once you exceed Mailchimp's free limit.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Mailchimp?

Yes, both platforms support email list exports and imports. You can export your contacts from Constant Contact as a CSV file and import them into Mailchimp. However, you'll lose any automation workflows or advanced segmentation, so plan the migration carefully to minimize disruption.
