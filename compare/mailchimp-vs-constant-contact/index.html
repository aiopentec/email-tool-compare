Which Should You Choose?

Choose Mailchimp if…

  • You're a beginner or startup looking for a free email marketing option with no credit card required
  • You want advanced automation and A/B testing capabilities to optimize your campaigns
  • You prefer an all-in-one platform with landing pages and audience segmentation built-in

Choose Constant Contact if…

  • You need SMS marketing and social media advertising features in one platform
  • You run events or manage local business promotions requiring event management tools
  • You operate an e-commerce store and want integrated shopping cart features

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your needs. Mailchimp is better for beginners because of its free tier and user-friendly interface, earning a higher G2 rating of 4.3 vs 4.0. Constant Contact is better for established businesses needing advanced features like SMS, event management, and e-commerce integration.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Constant Contact?

Mailchimp is cheaper overall because it offers a free tier supporting up to 500 subscribers, making it ideal for testing before you pay. If you need paid plans, Constant Contact starts at $12/month while Mailchimp starts at $13/month, but Mailchimp's free option provides significant savings for small businesses.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Mailchimp?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber list and campaigns from Constant Contact to Mailchimp. Both platforms support standard CSV exports and imports, though you may need to recreate custom automation workflows. Plan the transition during a low-traffic period to minimize disruption.
