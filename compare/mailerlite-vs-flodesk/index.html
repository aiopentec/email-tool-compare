MailerLite vs Flodesk: Complete Comparison

MailerLite and Flodesk are two popular email marketing platforms with very different approaches. MailerLite offers an affordable freemium model ideal for budget-conscious small businesses, while Flodesk uses flat-rate pricing and focuses on design-first email creation. Your choice depends on your budget, design priorities, and subscriber count.

Which Should You Choose?

Choose MailerLite if…

  • You want to start for free with up to 1,000 subscribers
  • You need advanced features like automation, landing pages, and surveys
  • You require e-commerce integrations for selling products

Choose Flodesk if…

  • You prioritize beautiful, professionally designed email templates
  • You want unlimited subscribers included in your flat monthly fee
  • You're a designer, photographer, or visual brand focused on aesthetics

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than Flodesk?

It depends on your priorities. MailerLite offers better overall value with a free tier and more features like automation and e-commerce integrations, making it ideal for general small businesses. Flodesk excels at design and is better for visual brands willing to pay a premium for stunning templates.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Flodesk?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, with a free plan supporting up to 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $9/month. Flodesk has no free tier and starts at $38/month flat-rate, but this includes unlimited subscribers compared to MailerLite's subscriber-based pricing.

Can I switch from Flodesk to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms support subscriber list exports and imports. Most email marketing tools, including MailerLite, can import your subscriber data from Flodesk. You may lose some design elements or template formatting in the migration, so plan accordingly.
