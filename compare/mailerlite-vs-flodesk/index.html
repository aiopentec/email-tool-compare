MailerLite vs Flodesk: Which Email Marketing Tool Should You Choose?

MailerLite and Flodesk are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and business models. MailerLite is built for budget-conscious startups and small businesses, while Flodesk caters to visual creators who prioritize design and unlimited scalability. The right choice depends on your budget, design priorities, and subscriber growth plans.

Frequently asked questions

Is MailerLite better than Flodesk?

It depends on your priorities. MailerLite is better for budget-conscious businesses, automation needs, and e-commerce integrations. Flodesk excels at design and visual appeal with unlimited subscribers. MailerLite has a broader feature set and is more versatile for general marketing, while Flodesk is purpose-built for visually-focused brands.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Flodesk?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, with a free tier up to 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $9/month. Flodesk charges a flat $38/month with no free tier. However, Flodesk's flat-rate model becomes more economical if you have thousands of subscribers, since MailerLite's pricing increases with subscriber count.

Can I switch from Flodesk to MailerLite?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber list and campaigns between platforms. Both tools support standard email list exports and imports. However, you may lose some design elements or advanced workflows during migration, so test the import process carefully before fully switching.

Which has better customer support?

Both platforms offer solid support with responsive teams. MailerLite provides 24/7 support across multiple channels, while Flodesk offers email and chat support. MailerLite's longer track record (founded 2010) means more community resources and documentation available online.

Does MailerLite have templates like Flodesk?

MailerLite has professionally designed templates with a drag-and-drop editor, but Flodesk is specifically known for stunning, visually-premium templates. If template aesthetics are critical to your brand, Flodesk has a clear advantage with more design-focused options.
