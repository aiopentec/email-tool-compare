Frequently Asked Questions

Is Moosend better than Constant Contact?

Moosend edges out Constant Contact overall due to superior customer ratings (4.7 vs 4.0 on G2) and a more generous free tier with unlimited emails. However, Constant Contact offers more integrated features like e-commerce and SMS, making it better for certain use cases. The best choice depends on your specific needs and budget.

Which is cheaper, Moosend or Constant Contact?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, offering a free plan with up to 1,000 subscribers and unlimited emails, then starting at just $9/month for paid plans. Constant Contact has no free tier and begins at $12/month. For budget-conscious businesses, Moosend is the clear winner.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Moosend?

Yes, both platforms typically allow you to export your contact lists and email templates. Most email marketing tools support standard CSV and import formats, though you may need to recreate some automation workflows manually. Check each platform's documentation for specific migration guidelines.
