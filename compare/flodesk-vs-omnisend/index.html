Omnisend vs Flodesk: Detailed Feature & Price Comparison

Omnisend and Flodesk are both strong email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences. Omnisend excels for e-commerce businesses needing SMS and omnichannel automation, while Flodesk prioritizes beautiful design and visual storytelling. Choose based on your industry, budget, and whether you need multichannel capabilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Omnisend better than Flodesk?

It depends on your needs. Omnisend is better for e-commerce and multi-channel marketing with SMS and push capabilities, offering more features and a free tier. Flodesk is better if you prioritize beautiful email design and want unlimited subscribers at a flat price. Both score highly on customer satisfaction (Omnisend 4.5-4.6 stars, Flodesk 4.7-4.8 stars).

Which is cheaper, Omnisend or Flodesk?

Omnisend is cheaper for small lists, starting free and scaling from $16/month. Flodesk has a flat $38/month regardless of subscriber count. For small businesses with under 1,000 subscribers, Omnisend is more economical. For large lists, Flodesk's flat pricing becomes more attractive.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Omnisend?

Yes, both platforms typically support CSV exports of your contact list and email history. The process involves exporting your subscribers from Flodesk and importing them into Omnisend. Check each platform's support documentation for specific migration steps to ensure you preserve segmentation and subscriber data.
