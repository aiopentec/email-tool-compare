GetResponse vs Omnisend: Complete Comparison

GetResponse and Omnisend are both freemium email marketing platforms, but they cater to different audiences. GetResponse positions itself as a complete marketing suite for course creators and webinar hosts, while Omnisend specializes in ecommerce with built-in SMS and omnichannel automation. Frequently asked questions

Is GetResponse better than Omnisend?

It depends on your use case. Omnisend has higher customer ratings (4.6 vs 3.7 on Trustpilot) and is better for ecommerce with SMS capabilities. GetResponse is more versatile for course creators and offers a larger free tier, making it better for non-ecommerce businesses.

Which is cheaper, GetResponse or Omnisend?

Omnisend starts at $16/month compared to GetResponse's $19/month. However, GetResponse offers 500 free subscribers versus Omnisend's 250, so the free tier is more generous with GetResponse. Your actual costs depend on subscriber count and features needed.

Can I switch from Omnisend to GetResponse?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your contact lists and email data. Most migrations are straightforward, though you'll need to rebuild automations and workflows in your new platform. Consider planning the migration during a low-traffic period.
