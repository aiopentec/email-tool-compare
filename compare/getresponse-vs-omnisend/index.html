Frequently Asked Questions

Is GetResponse better than Omnisend?

It depends on your use case. Omnisend has higher customer satisfaction ratings (4.5 G2, 4.6 Trustpilot) and better features for e-commerce with SMS and push notifications. GetResponse is superior for online course creators and webinar hosts who need more comprehensive content delivery tools.

Which is cheaper, GetResponse or Omnisend?

Omnisend starts at $16/month compared to GetResponse's $19/month, making it $3 cheaper monthly. However, GetResponse offers a larger free tier with 500 contacts versus Omnisend's 250, which may be better value if you're just starting out.

Can I switch from Omnisend to GetResponse?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your contact lists and email campaigns. Most email marketing tools support CSV imports, making migration straightforward. However, you may lose automation workflows and SMS functionality if switching from Omnisend.
