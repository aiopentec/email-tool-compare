Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than ActiveCampaign?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. MailerLite excels in simplicity, affordability, and e-commerce features, making it ideal for small businesses. ActiveCampaign offers more advanced automation, CRM, and SMS capabilities, making it better suited for growing companies with complex sales processes.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or ActiveCampaign?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, with a free tier for up to 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $9/month. ActiveCampaign has no free option and starts at $15/month. For small businesses, MailerLite's free plan can save substantial costs.

Can I switch from ActiveCampaign to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms support data export and import. Most email service providers allow you to export your subscriber lists and automation workflows. However, you may need to recreate some complex automations manually, as each platform has different automation builders.
