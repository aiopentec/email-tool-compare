Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than ActiveCampaign?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. MailerLite wins for simplicity, affordability, and e-commerce features, making it ideal for small businesses. ActiveCampaign is better for companies needing advanced automation, CRM, and sophisticated marketing workflows. Choose based on your budget and feature requirements.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or ActiveCampaign?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, offering a free tier for up to 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $9/month. ActiveCampaign has no free tier and starts at $15/month. For budget-conscious businesses, MailerLite is the clear winner.

Can I switch from ActiveCampaign to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms support data exports and integrations with migration tools. However, some advanced ActiveCampaign features (like complex CRM workflows) may not have direct equivalents in MailerLite. Plan your migration carefully to ensure you retain essential functionality.
