Beehiiv vs AWeber: Detailed Comparison for Email Marketers

Both Beehiiv and AWeber are established email marketing platforms offering freemium models, but they serve different audiences and use cases. Beehiiv is purpose-built for newsletter publishers seeking monetization and growth, while AWeber caters to bloggers and solopreneurs wanting straightforward automation. Choosing between them depends on your primary goals and business model.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than AWeber?

It depends on your needs. Beehiiv excels for publishers monetizing newsletters and has higher user ratings (4.7 vs 4.2 on G2), but AWeber is simpler and cheaper for basic email marketing. Beehiiv is the better all-around platform for growth-focused publishers, while AWeber wins for budget-conscious beginners.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or AWeber?

AWeber has a lower entry point at $15/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month. However, Beehiiv's free tier allows 2,500 subscribers versus AWeber's 500, making Beehiiv better value if you need to scale before paying. Your actual costs depend on your subscriber count and feature needs.

Can I switch from AWeber to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support importing subscriber lists, though the process varies by tool. You should export your contacts from AWeber in CSV format and import them into Beehiiv. Review each platform's migration guides before switching to ensure no data loss during the transition.
