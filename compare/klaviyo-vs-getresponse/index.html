Frequently Asked Questions

Is Klaviyo better than GetResponse?

It depends on your needs. Klaviyo excels for e-commerce businesses with superior predictive analytics and SMS integration, while GetResponse is better for content creators and course sellers. Both have strong automation, but Klaviyo has higher user ratings (4.6 vs 4.2 on G2).

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or GetResponse?

GetResponse is more affordable, starting at $19/month compared to Klaviyo's $45/month. Both offer free tiers with 500 subscribers, making them equally accessible for testing. However, pricing differences narrow as you scale.

Can I switch from GetResponse to Klaviyo?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list exports and imports. Most contact data can be migrated, though automation workflows will need to be recreated in your new platform. Plan the transition during a low-campaign period for best results.
