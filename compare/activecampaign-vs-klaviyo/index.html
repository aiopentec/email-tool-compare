Frequently asked questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Klaviyo?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign is better for businesses requiring advanced automation, CRM, and lead scoring capabilities. Klaviyo is superior for e-commerce brands that need predictive analytics and specialized Shopify integrations. Both have excellent ratings, with Klaviyo slightly ahead on G2 (4.6 vs 4.5), but ActiveCampaign leads on Trustpilot.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Klaviyo?

Klaviyo is cheaper for small businesses, offering a free tier up to 500 subscribers before paid plans start at $45/month. ActiveCampaign has no free tier and starts at $15/month, making it competitive for businesses ready to pay immediately. For e-commerce startups, Klaviyo's free option provides better value.

Can I switch from Klaviyo to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, you can migrate between platforms. Both support standard CSV exports and integrations with popular apps, making data transfer feasible. However, you may lose some automation workflows and custom segmentation during the transition, so plan the migration carefully and test thoroughly before switching.
