Brevo offers a comprehensive all-in-one CRM suite with a generous free tier, while Constant Contact focuses on simplicity for nonprofits and local businesses with paid plans starting at just $12/month. Frequently asked questions

Is Brevo better than Constant Contact?

Brevo offers more features and a more generous free tier, making it better for businesses wanting all-in-one marketing automation. However, Constant Contact may be preferable if you specifically need e-commerce integration and social media advertising. The best choice depends on your specific business requirements and budget.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Constant Contact?

Brevo is significantly cheaper because it offers a free plan supporting up to 100,000 subscribers with full access to core features. Constant Contact has no free tier and charges $12/month minimum. If you need paid features, Brevo starts at $25/month, making it the more affordable option overall.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Brevo?

Yes, you can switch from Constant Contact to Brevo. Most email marketing platforms allow you to export your contact lists in standard formats like CSV. Both Brevo and Constant Contact support importing contacts, making migration straightforward. You may need to recreate automation workflows and templates in your new platform.
