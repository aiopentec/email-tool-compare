Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your needs. Brevo offers better value with its free tier and more comprehensive feature set including CRM and multiple communication channels. However, Constant Contact may be better for beginners who prefer simplicity and for e-commerce businesses needing integrated shopping features.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Constant Contact?

Brevo is significantly cheaper overall. It offers a free tier for up to 100,000 subscribers, while Constant Contact has no free option and starts at $12/month. Even Brevo's paid plans begin at $25/month but include more features than Constant Contact's entry-level offering.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list imports and exports. You can export your contacts and campaigns from Constant Contact and import them into Brevo. Most migrations are straightforward, though you may need to reconfigure some automation rules depending on complexity.
