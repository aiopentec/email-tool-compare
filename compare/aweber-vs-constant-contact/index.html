Which Should You Choose?

Choose AWeber if…

  • You want a free email marketing option with no subscriber limits during trial
  • You're a blogger or solopreneur prioritizing affordability and simplicity
  • You need strong automation features like autoresponders and RSS-to-email campaigns

Choose Constant Contact if…

  • You need SMS marketing and social media ad integration
  • You run an e-commerce business requiring built-in e-commerce tools
  • You're a nonprofit or local business needing event management features

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AWeber better than Constant Contact?

Neither is objectively 'better'—it depends on your needs. AWeber wins for simplicity and free-tier access, making it ideal for solopreneurs. Constant Contact excels in feature breadth with SMS, social ads, and e-commerce tools, making it better for growing businesses. Compare based on your specific requirements.

Which is cheaper, AWeber or Constant Contact?

AWeber offers a free plan for up to 500 subscribers, making it cheaper for startups. Constant Contact starts at $12/month with no free tier. For paid plans, Constant Contact at $12/month slightly undercuts AWeber's $15/month entry price, but AWeber's free option gives it the edge for budget-conscious users.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to AWeber?

Yes, both platforms support list imports and exports. You can export your contacts from Constant Contact as a CSV file and import them into AWeber. However, campaign history and detailed analytics won't transfer, so plan your migration accordingly to avoid losing important data.
