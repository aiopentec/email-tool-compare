AWeber vs Constant Contact: Detailed Comparison

Both AWeber and Constant Contact are established email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and business models. AWeber targets solopreneurs and bloggers with a generous free tier, while Constant Contact caters to nonprofits and local businesses with a paid-first approach. This comparison will help you determine which platform aligns best with your marketing needs and budget.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AWeber better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your needs. AWeber wins for solopreneurs who want a free tier and simplicity, while Constant Contact excels for businesses needing advanced features like SMS, event management, and e-commerce tools. AWeber has stronger customer ratings overall, particularly on Trustpilot.

Which is cheaper, AWeber or Constant Contact?

AWeber's free tier makes it cheaper for small lists (under 500 subscribers), costing nothing to start. Constant Contact's lowest paid plan is $12/month, so it's more cost-effective than AWeber's $15/month tier once you outgrow the free option. For growing businesses, the pricing becomes comparable.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to AWeber?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list imports and exports. You can download your contacts from Constant Contact and upload them to AWeber with minimal friction. The migration process typically takes a few minutes, though it's wise to test your automation rules after switching.
