Frequently asked questions

Is Brevo better than Sendinblue?

Brevo is the current iteration of Sendinblue following its 2022 rebrand. Brevo offers the same core functionality with additional features like enhanced live chat capabilities and an expanded multi-channel approach. If you're considering a new platform, Brevo is the recommended choice as it represents the actively maintained version.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Sendinblue?

Both platforms have identical pricing structures starting at $0 for the free tier and $25/month for paid plans. The cost difference is negligible, making pricing a tie between the two. Your choice should be based on features and platform maturity rather than cost.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Brevo?

Since Brevo is the rebranded version of Sendinblue, existing Sendinblue users have already transitioned to Brevo. If you're currently on Sendinblue, your account is now managed through the Brevo platform. New users should sign up directly with Brevo for the best experience and access to the latest features.
