Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertKit better than Beehiiv?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your goals. ConvertKit excels for course creators and e-commerce businesses, while Beehiiv is superior for newsletter publishers wanting monetization options. ConvertKit has lower customer satisfaction ratings (3.2 vs 4.4 on Trustpilot), so consider your specific needs carefully.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Beehiiv?

ConvertKit's paid plans start at $29/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month, making ConvertKit cheaper. However, Beehiiv offers a more generous free tier with 2,500 subscribers versus ConvertKit's 1,000, which may delay paid upgrades longer depending on your growth.

Can I switch from Beehiiv to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and migrate to another service. The migration process varies by provider, so contact their support teams for detailed instructions. Most email platforms support standard CSV exports to facilitate switching.
