ConvertKit vs Beehiiv: Detailed Comparison for Email Marketers

ConvertKit and Beehiiv are two of the most popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences. ConvertKit is built for creators selling courses and digital products, while Beehiiv targets newsletter publishers focused on growth and monetization. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right platform for your goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertKit better than Beehiiv?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your use case. ConvertKit excels for creators selling digital products with its commerce features, while Beehiiv is superior for newsletter publishers seeking growth and monetization tools. Beehiiv has higher user satisfaction ratings overall, but ConvertKit remains the better choice for course creators.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Beehiiv?

ConvertKit's paid plans start at $29/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month. However, Beehiiv offers a larger free tier (2,500 vs 1,000 subscribers), which may keep you in the free plan longer. The actual cost depends on your subscriber count and feature needs.

Can I switch from Beehiiv to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists, making migration possible. Most email marketing tools support CSV imports, so switching between ConvertKit and Beehiiv is straightforward. You'll want to test automations and landing pages on your new platform before fully migrating.
