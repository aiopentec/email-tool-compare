Frequently Asked Questions

Is Campaign Monitor better than Constant Contact?

Campaign Monitor edges ahead for agencies and teams managing multiple brands, with superior automation features and higher customer ratings. However, Constant Contact better serves nonprofits and local businesses needing SMS, social ads, and e-commerce tools. The better choice depends on your specific business model and feature requirements.

Which is cheaper, Campaign Monitor or Constant Contact?

Campaign Monitor is slightly cheaper at $11/month compared to Constant Contact's $12/month starting price. However, the difference is minimal, and your overall costs will depend on subscriber count and additional features needed. Both platforms offer affiliate programs if cost is a concern.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Campaign Monitor?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your contact lists and email templates. Most email marketing tools support standard CSV or similar formats for data migration. You may need to manually recreate some automation workflows, but your core subscriber data will transfer smoothly between platforms.
