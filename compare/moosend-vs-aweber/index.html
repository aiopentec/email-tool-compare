Frequently asked questions

Is Moosend better than AWeber?

Moosend offers better value for most users with unlimited emails, lower pricing, and more advanced features, reflected in its higher G2 (4.7 vs 4.2) and Trustpilot (4.4 vs 4.0) ratings. However, AWeber may be better if you prioritize simplicity and ease of use over feature depth.

Which is cheaper, Moosend or AWeber?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to AWeber's $15/month. Additionally, Moosend's free tier supports 1,000 subscribers with unlimited emails, while AWeber's free tier covers only 500 subscribers with sending restrictions.

Can I switch from AWeber to Moosend?

Yes, both platforms support email list imports and exports, making migration straightforward. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your subscriber lists in CSV format and import them into Moosend without losing your contacts or data.
