Understanding their key differences will help you choose the right tool for your business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Campaign Monitor better than Flodesk?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. Campaign Monitor is superior for agencies managing multiple brands, offering more sophisticated segmentation and transactional capabilities. Flodesk wins for visual-focused brands that prioritize design and want unlimited subscribers at a flat rate. Check your specific requirements to determine which better suits your business.

Which is cheaper, Campaign Monitor or Flodesk?

Campaign Monitor starts at $11/month, making it cheaper upfront than Flodesk's $38/month. However, Campaign Monitor's pricing scales with contact count, so costs can increase significantly as your list grows. Flodesk's flat-rate pricing offers better value long-term if you have large subscriber lists, since you pay the same regardless of audience size.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Campaign Monitor?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and email history. Most email marketing tools support standard CSV exports, making migration straightforward. You'll need to recreate your email templates and workflows in Campaign Monitor, but your contact data transfers easily. Plan the migration during a low-activity period to minimize disruption.
