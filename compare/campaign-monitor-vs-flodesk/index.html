Which Should You Choose?

Choose Campaign Monitor if…

  • You manage multiple client brands and need dedicated multi-brand management tools
  • You need transactional email capabilities built-in
  • You're starting small with under 500 subscribers and want the lowest entry price

Choose Flodesk if…

  • You're a visual creator, designer, or photographer prioritizing beautiful email aesthetics
  • You have unlimited subscriber growth plans and want predictable flat-rate pricing
  • You need e-commerce features like checkout forms and link-in-bio functionality

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Campaign Monitor better than Flodesk?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Campaign Monitor wins for agencies managing multiple brands with advanced segmentation, while Flodesk excels for creative professionals seeking simplicity and unlimited subscribers. Flodesk has significantly higher customer satisfaction ratings (4.8 G2, 4.7 Trustpilot vs 4.1 and 3.2).

Which is cheaper, Campaign Monitor or Flodesk?

Campaign Monitor starts at just $11/month, making it cheaper for small lists. However, Flodesk's $38/month flat-rate includes unlimited subscribers, making it better value if you grow beyond 2,500-5,000 subscribers. The true cost comparison depends entirely on your subscriber count.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Campaign Monitor?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list exports and imports. Most subscribers can migrate their contact lists easily, though you'll need to recreate workflows and templates in your new platform. Campaign Monitor provides documentation to help with the migration process.
