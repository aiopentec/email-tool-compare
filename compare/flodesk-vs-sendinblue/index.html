Flodesk vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

Flodesk and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and use cases. Flodesk excels at beautiful email design with flat-rate pricing, while Sendinblue offers a comprehensive CRM suite with SMS and transactional capabilities. Understanding their strengths will help you choose the right tool for your business.

Frequently asked questions

Is Flodesk better than Sendinblue?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Flodesk excels for design-focused email marketing with higher customer satisfaction ratings (4.8 on G2), while Sendinblue offers broader functionality as an all-in-one CRM with SMS, WhatsApp, and transactional features. Choose Flodesk for email design, Sendinblue for omnichannel marketing.

Which is cheaper, Flodesk or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is cheaper if you're starting out, offering a free tier with up to 100,000 subscribers and 300 daily emails. Flodesk has no free tier and starts at $38/month but includes unlimited subscribers. For budget-conscious beginners, Sendinblue wins; for growing businesses needing unlimited contacts, Flodesk offers better long-term value.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Flodesk?

Yes, most email platforms allow you to export your contact lists and subscriber data. Both tools support standard import/export formats, though you'll need to recreate workflows and campaigns manually in your new platform. Plan the migration carefully to avoid losing email history or campaign data.
