Omnisend vs Constant Contact: Detailed Comparison

Omnisend and Constant Contact are both established email marketing platforms, but they serve different business needs. Omnisend specializes in omnichannel e-commerce marketing with SMS and push notifications, while Constant Contact focuses on simplicity for nonprofits and local businesses. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right tool for your marketing strategy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Omnisend better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your business type. Omnisend is superior for e-commerce brands needing omnichannel marketing with SMS and push notifications, while Constant Contact better serves nonprofits and local businesses seeking simplicity and event management. Omnisend has higher customer ratings (4.5-4.6) compared to Constant Contact's mixed reviews (2.8-4.0).

Which is cheaper, Omnisend or Constant Contact?

Constant Contact starts at $12/month, slightly cheaper than Omnisend's $16/month paid plans. However, Omnisend offers a free tier for up to 250 subscribers, making it the better value for businesses just starting out. The actual cost depends on your subscriber count and feature needs.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Omnisend?

Yes, most email marketing platforms allow you to export your contact lists and migrate to another service. Both Omnisend and Constant Contact support standard CSV imports, so you can transfer your subscriber data relatively easily. You may need to recreate automations and templates in the new platform.
