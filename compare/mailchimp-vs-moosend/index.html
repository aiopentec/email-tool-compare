Frequently asked questions

Is Mailchimp better than Moosend?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your priorities. Mailchimp excels in brand recognition and ecommerce features, while Moosend offers superior pricing, more generous free plans, and significantly higher customer satisfaction ratings. Moosend scores 4.7 on G2 versus Mailchimp's 4.3, and vastly outperforms on Trustpilot (4.4 vs 1.5).

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Moosend?

Moosend is more affordable overall. It starts at $9/month compared to Mailchimp's $13/month, and offers 1,000 free subscribers with unlimited emails versus Mailchimp's 500 subscribers. For cost-conscious businesses, Moosend delivers better value at every tier.

Can I switch from Moosend to Mailchimp?

Yes, switching is straightforward. Both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and email templates. Most migrations take minimal time, though you'll need to reconfigure automations and integrations in your new platform. Both tools provide import functionality to facilitate the transition.
