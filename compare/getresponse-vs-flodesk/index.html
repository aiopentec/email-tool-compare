GetResponse vs Flodesk: Complete Comparison

GetResponse and Flodesk are both popular email marketing platforms, but they cater to different types of users. GetResponse is a comprehensive marketing suite ideal for course creators and webinar hosts, while Flodesk specializes in beautiful email design for visual brands. Choosing between them depends on your priorities: all-in-one functionality versus design-focused simplicity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GetResponse better than Flodesk?

It depends on your needs. GetResponse is better for course creators, webinar hosts, and businesses needing extensive automation and marketing tools. Flodesk excels if you're a visual brand prioritizing beautiful email design and simplicity. Both have strong ratings, with Flodesk slightly higher on G2 (4.8 vs 4.2).

Which is cheaper, GetResponse or Flodesk?

GetResponse is cheaper overall, offering a free tier up to 500 subscribers and paid plans starting at $19/month. Flodesk charges a flat $38/month with no free option but includes unlimited subscribers. For small businesses or those testing email marketing, GetResponse's freemium model wins on price.

Can I switch from Flodesk to GetResponse?

Yes, both platforms support email list imports and exports. Most modern email marketing tools allow you to export your subscriber list as a CSV file and import it into GetResponse. You may need to recreate automation workflows and templates, but your core data transfers easily.
