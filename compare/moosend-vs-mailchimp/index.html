Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than Moosend?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your priorities. Mailchimp excels for ecommerce integration and brand recognition, while Moosend offers better value with unlimited free emails and lower paid pricing. Moosend also has significantly higher user satisfaction ratings on both G2 and Trustpilot.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Moosend?

Moosend is cheaper overall. Its paid plans start at $9/month compared to Mailchimp's $13/month, and Moosend offers unlimited emails on the free tier while Mailchimp limits free accounts. For budget-conscious businesses, Moosend provides better value.

Can I switch from Moosend to Mailchimp?

Yes, both platforms support exporting and importing subscriber lists, making migration relatively straightforward. Most email marketing platforms allow CSV exports of your contacts, so you can transfer your audience between tools. Plan the migration carefully to maintain your email schedules and automation workflows.
