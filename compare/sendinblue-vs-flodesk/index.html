Which Should You Choose?

Choose Sendinblue if…

  • You prioritize beautiful email design and templates
  • You want unlimited subscribers at a flat monthly price
  • You run a visual brand (photography, design, fashion)

Choose Flodesk if…

  • You need a free tier with generous limits
  • You want SMS, WhatsApp, and CRM features included
  • You require transactional email capabilities
  • You need multi-channel communication in one platform

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Flodesk better than Sendinblue?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Flodesk excels for design-focused brands with beautiful templates and unlimited subscribers. Sendinblue offers more features, better pricing for startups, and multi-channel capabilities. Choose based on your priority: aesthetics vs. functionality and affordability.

Which is cheaper, Flodesk or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is significantly cheaper. It offers a free tier with 100,000 subscribers and 300 emails daily at no cost, while paid plans start at $25/month. Flodesk has no free tier and starts at $38/month. For budget-conscious businesses, Sendinblue wins on price.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Flodesk?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list exports and imports. However, note that Flodesk focuses on visual email design, so your workflow and template strategy may need adjustment. Migration is straightforward, but the user experience and feature set differ significantly between the two.
