Which Should You Choose?

Choose Moosend if…

  • You need e-commerce integrations for an online store and want a platform built with sellers in mind
  • You prefer a user-friendly interface designed for beginners with excellent drag-and-drop functionality
  • You're looking for pop-ups and survey features to boost engagement alongside email marketing

Choose MailerLite if…

  • You want unlimited emails sent to your contacts without worrying about sending limits
  • Built-in A/B testing is important for optimizing your email campaigns and improving performance
  • You need real-time analytics to track campaign results and subscriber behavior as it happens

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than Moosend?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Moosend edges ahead overall with unlimited emails and A/B testing, earning a slightly higher G2 rating (4.7 vs 4.6). However, MailerLite excels for e-commerce businesses with native integrations and offers superior beginner-friendly design. Choose based on your specific requirements rather than general superiority.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Moosend?

Both platforms are equally priced, starting at $0 for free tiers with 1,000 subscribers and $9/month for paid plans. The real cost difference emerges if you send high email volumes—Moosend's unlimited emails feature could be more economical at scale, while MailerLite's pricing remains tied to sending limits.

Can I switch from Moosend to MailerLite?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber list and campaign data between platforms. Most email marketing tools support CSV exports and imports, making the transition relatively straightforward. However, you may lose some historical data or formatting during the migration, so plan accordingly and test thoroughly before switching completely.
