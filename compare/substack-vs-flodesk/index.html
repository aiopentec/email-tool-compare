Substack vs Flodesk: Detailed Comparison

Substack and Flodesk serve different niches within email marketing. Substack excels at newsletter monetization with a built-in audience, while Flodesk focuses on visually stunning email design for creative professionals. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize direct subscriber revenue or beautiful, brand-aligned campaigns.

Frequently asked questions

Is Substack better than Flodesk?

Neither is universally better—they serve different purposes. Substack excels for writers monetizing content directly, while Flodesk is superior for visual brands and ecommerce. Choose based on your primary goal: subscriber revenue or beautiful campaign design.

Which is cheaper, Substack or Flodesk?

Substack is free to start with no upfront costs, though you pay 10% of revenue from paid subscriptions. Flodesk requires a $38/month minimum investment. Substack is cheaper if you have no monetization, but Flodesk offers better value if you need advanced features without revenue concerns.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Substack?

Yes, most email lists can be exported and imported between platforms. However, Substack's focus on paid subscriptions and Flodesk's design-centric approach mean migrating may require restructuring your strategy rather than a simple technical transfer.
