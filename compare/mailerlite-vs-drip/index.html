Frequently asked questions

Is MailerLite better than Drip?

It depends on your needs. MailerLite is better for budget-conscious startups and small businesses due to its free tier and lower pricing. Drip is better for established e-commerce businesses needing advanced revenue tracking and store integration. MailerLite scores higher overall for versatility, while Drip excels specifically for online sellers.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Drip?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, offering a free plan for up to 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $9/month. Drip has no free tier and starts at $39/month. For budget-conscious teams, MailerLite's freemium model provides excellent value.

Can I switch from Drip to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms support subscriber list exports and integrations with common email marketing tools. Most migrations involve exporting contacts and setting up automation rules in MailerLite. Both tools have support resources to help with the transition process.
