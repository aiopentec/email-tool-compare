Frequently asked questions

Is Klaviyo better than Campaign Monitor?

It depends on your use case. Klaviyo excels for e-commerce brands with superior integrations, automation, and ratings (4.6 vs 4.1 on G2). Campaign Monitor is better for agencies managing multiple client accounts. Choose based on whether you need deep e-commerce functionality or multi-brand management.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or Campaign Monitor?

Campaign Monitor has a lower entry price at $11/month and no free tier requirement. However, Klaviyo offers a free plan for up to 500 subscribers, making it cost-free to start. For most growing businesses, both are competitively priced once you move beyond the free tier.

Can I switch from Campaign Monitor to Klaviyo?

Yes, switching is straightforward. Both platforms support contact list imports and email template migration. Most users can transition their subscriber lists and campaign data within hours. We recommend exporting your contacts and templates from Campaign Monitor before importing them into Klaviyo.
