Frequently asked questions

Is Drip better than Moosend?

It depends on your needs. Drip excels for e-commerce businesses requiring advanced product tracking and revenue attribution. Moosend is better for budget-conscious teams needing flexible automation and unlimited email sends. Both have strong ratings, with Moosend slightly higher on G2 (4.7 vs 4.4).

Which is cheaper, Drip or Moosend?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, offering a free plan for up to 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $9/month. Drip has no free tier and starts at $39/month. For small businesses, Moosend is the more affordable choice.

Can I switch from Moosend to Drip?

Yes, both platforms support data export and import features. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your contact lists and create migration plans. Contact each platform's support team for specific guidance on transferring your automations and settings.
