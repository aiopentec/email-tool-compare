Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertKit better than Klaviyo?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your use case. Klaviyo scores higher on G2 (4.6 vs 4.4) and excels for e-commerce businesses, while ConvertKit is better suited for creators and course sellers. Choose based on your business type and specific feature needs.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Klaviyo?

ConvertKit is more affordable, starting at $29/month compared to Klaviyo's $45/month. Additionally, ConvertKit's free tier supports up to 1,000 subscribers versus Klaviyo's 500, making ConvertKit the budget-friendly choice.

Can I switch from Klaviyo to ConvertKit?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber list from Klaviyo to ConvertKit. Most email platforms allow CSV exports of your contacts. However, you'll need to manually recreate your automations and sequences in ConvertKit's builder.
