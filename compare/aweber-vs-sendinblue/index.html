Frequently Asked Questions

Is AWeber better than Sendinblue?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. AWeber wins for simplicity and ease of use, making it ideal for beginners and content creators. Sendinblue excels in features and versatility, offering SMS, CRM, and transactional capabilities at a competitive price. Compare based on your specific requirements.

Which is cheaper, AWeber or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue offers better free tier value with 100,000 subscribers versus AWeber's 500. For paid plans, AWeber starts at $15/month while Sendinblue begins at $25/month. However, Sendinblue's free tier may eliminate the need for a paid plan entirely for smaller businesses.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to AWeber?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and campaigns. Most email marketing tools support standard import formats (CSV, etc.), making migration straightforward. Plan ahead for any downtime and test your lists after import to ensure data integrity.
