AWeber vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

AWeber and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are both popular email marketing platforms with freemium models, but they serve different audiences. AWeber focuses on simplicity for bloggers and solopreneurs, while Sendinblue emphasizes multi-channel communication and CRM integration. This comparison will help you determine which platform best fits your business needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AWeber better than Sendinblue?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. AWeber excels for bloggers seeking simplicity, while Sendinblue wins for businesses needing multi-channel communication and CRM features. Sendinblue's higher G2 (4.5 vs 4.2) and Trustpilot (4.3 vs 4.0) ratings suggest broader user satisfaction.

Which is cheaper, AWeber or Sendinblue?

For free plans, Sendinblue is significantly cheaper—it allows 100,000 free subscribers versus AWeber's 500. Paid plans start at $15 (AWeber) vs $25 (Sendinblue), making AWeber slightly cheaper when you need paid features, but Sendinblue's free tier often eliminates the need to pay.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to AWeber?

Yes, most email platforms allow you to export subscriber lists in standard formats (CSV). However, you may lose some data like automation workflows and custom fields during migration. Both platforms offer import tools to help transition your contacts, though you'll need to recreate campaigns and automations manually.
