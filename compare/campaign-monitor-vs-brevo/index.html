Which Should You Choose?

Choose Campaign Monitor if…

  • You want a free email marketing plan with high contact limits
  • You need multi-channel marketing (SMS, WhatsApp, live chat) in one platform
  • You require CRM and transactional email API capabilities
  • You're a startup or small business with a tight budget

Choose Brevo if…

  • You manage multiple brands and need white-label capabilities
  • You prioritize email design and template quality
  • You need advanced journey designer and smart segmentation
  • You work in an agency setting with team collaboration needs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo better than Campaign Monitor?

It depends on your needs. Brevo excels for budget-conscious businesses seeking multi-channel marketing with a free tier, while Campaign Monitor is better for agencies managing multiple client brands. Brevo has higher user ratings (4.5 vs 4.1 on G2) and stronger overall value.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Campaign Monitor?

Brevo is significantly cheaper with a free plan supporting up to 100,000 contacts and paid plans starting at $25/month. Campaign Monitor has no free tier and starts at $11/month, making it more expensive for most users since you'll need higher-tier plans for robust features.

Can I switch from Campaign Monitor to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list imports and exports, making migration straightforward. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your contacts and import them into Brevo, though you may need to recreate campaigns and automations manually.
