Which Should You Choose?

Choose ConvertKit if…

  • You're a creator, blogger, or course seller looking for a dedicated email platform with built-in landing pages and commerce tools
  • You value a beginner-friendly interface optimized specifically for email marketing and audience building
  • You want visual automation builders and simple subscriber management without overwhelming features

Choose Brevo if…

  • You need a comprehensive CRM suite with multi-channel marketing (email, SMS, WhatsApp, live chat)
  • You require transactional email capabilities or need an API for technical integrations
  • You want the largest free tier (100,000 subscribers) to scale without paying immediately

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertKit better than Brevo?

Neither is objectively 'better'—it depends on your needs. ConvertKit excels for creators and course sellers with its specialized features and beginner-friendly interface, while Brevo is superior for businesses needing multi-channel marketing, transactional email, and CRM functionality. ConvertKit has better creator-focused tools; Brevo has broader business capabilities.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Brevo?

Brevo has a slight price advantage starting at $25/month versus ConvertKit's $29/month. More significantly, Brevo's free tier supports 100,000 subscribers compared to ConvertKit's 1,000, making Brevo dramatically more cost-effective for growing audiences before you pay. For paid plans, the difference is minimal.

Can I switch from Brevo to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms support subscriber list exports, allowing you to migrate your contacts. However, you'll lose any advanced automations or multi-channel sequences unique to Brevo. Most users can export CSV files from Brevo and import them into ConvertKit without major issues, though you may need to manually recreate complex workflows.
