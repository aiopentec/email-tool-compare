Frequently asked questions

Is ConvertKit better than Brevo?

It depends on your needs. ConvertKit is better for creators and course sellers who want specialized features like landing pages and visual automations. Brevo is better if you need multi-channel marketing (SMS, WhatsApp) and a larger free tier. Both are solid platforms with high ratings.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Brevo?

Brevo has a lower paid plan starting at $25/month compared to ConvertKit's $29/month. However, Brevo's free tier is much more generous with 100,000 free subscribers versus ConvertKit's 1,000. For most users, Brevo offers better value.

Can I switch from Brevo to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms support email list exports in standard formats. Most email marketing platforms allow you to import subscriber lists, though you may lose some data fields during migration. Plan your switch carefully and test the import process before fully committing.
