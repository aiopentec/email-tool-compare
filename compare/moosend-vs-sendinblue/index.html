Moosend vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

Both Moosend and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are freemium email marketing platforms with strong automation capabilities, but they serve different business needs. Moosend excels at unlimited email sending at low costs, while Sendinblue focuses on multi-channel marketing with SMS, WhatsApp, and CRM integration. Choosing between them depends on your budget, feature requirements, and whether you need channels beyond email.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Moosend better than Sendinblue?

It depends on your needs. Moosend is better for pure email marketing at minimal cost, offering unlimited sends from just $9/month. Sendinblue is better if you need multi-channel marketing (SMS, WhatsApp), transactional email APIs, and CRM features integrated into one platform.

Which is cheaper, Moosend or Sendinblue?

Moosend is significantly cheaper at $9/month for paid plans versus Sendinblue's $25/month. However, Sendinblue's free tier accommodates 100,000 subscribers compared to Moosend's 1,000, making it better for early-stage businesses with large contact lists who don't need unlimited sending.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Moosend?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your contact lists and email templates. Most email marketing tools support CSV imports, making migration straightforward. Plan the transition during a low-activity period and test your automation workflows after importing to ensure everything functions correctly.
