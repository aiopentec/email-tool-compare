Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Klaviyo?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your use case. Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers with its monetization features and generous free tier, while Klaviyo is superior for e-commerce businesses needing advanced automation and SMS. Choose based on your primary business model.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Klaviyo?

Beehiiv is slightly cheaper at $39/month versus Klaviyo's $45/month for paid plans. More importantly, Beehiiv's free tier supports 2,500 subscribers compared to Klaviyo's 500, making it more affordable for growing publishers.

Can I switch from Klaviyo to Beehiiv?

Yes, you can migrate your email list and contacts to Beehiiv, though automation flows and complex segmentation will need to be rebuilt manually. Both platforms support CSV imports and have data migration resources available to simplify the transition.
