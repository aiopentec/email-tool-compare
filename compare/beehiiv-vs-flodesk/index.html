Beehiiv focuses on newsletter publishers with monetization capabilities, while Flodesk emphasizes beautiful design and unlimited subscribers at a flat rate. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize revenue generation or visual aesthetics.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Flodesk?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. Beehiiv excels at newsletter monetization and has a free tier, making it ideal for publishers. Flodesk wins for visual design, unlimited subscribers at any tier, and e-commerce features like checkout forms. Both have strong ratings and active affiliate programs.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Flodesk?

Beehiiv is cheaper if you're just starting, thanks to its free tier supporting up to 2,500 subscribers. For paid plans, both start around $38-39/month, but Flodesk offers unlimited subscribers at that price, while Beehiiv's pricing scales with subscriber growth. Beehiiv becomes more economical only if you stay under 2,500 subscribers.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Beehiiv?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber list and campaigns between platforms, though you'll need to set up designs and workflows again since they work differently. Both platforms typically support CSV exports and imports. Plan your migration carefully to minimize downtime, and test your campaigns before going live with your audience.
