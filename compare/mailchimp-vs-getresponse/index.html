Frequently asked questions

Is Mailchimp better than GetResponse?

It depends on your needs. Mailchimp is better for beginners and small businesses due to its simpler interface and lower pricing, with a G2 rating of 4.3. However, GetResponse offers more specialized features for webinar hosts and course creators, backed by stronger customer satisfaction (3.7 Trustpilot rating).

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or GetResponse?

Mailchimp is the cheaper option, starting at $13/month compared to GetResponse's $19/month. Both offer identical free tiers with 500 subscriber limits, making Mailchimp the more budget-friendly choice for businesses scaling up from free plans.

Can I switch from GetResponse to Mailchimp?

Yes, both platforms support email list exports, allowing you to migrate your contacts relatively easily. Most email marketing platforms provide CSV export options, though you may need to manually reconfigure automations and templates in your new platform.
