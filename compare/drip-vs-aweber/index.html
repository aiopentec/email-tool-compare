Frequently asked questions

Is Drip better than AWeber?

It depends on your needs. Drip is superior for e-commerce businesses requiring revenue tracking and store integration. AWeber is better for beginners and content creators who want affordability and ease of use. Both have strong ratings, though Drip edges out on G2 (4.4 vs 4.2) while AWeber scores higher on Trustpilot (4.0 vs 3.5).

Which is cheaper, Drip or AWeber?

AWeber is significantly cheaper, offering a free tier with 500 subscribers and unlimited features. Paid plans start at $15/month. Drip has no free tier and starts at $39/month. For budget-conscious users, AWeber is the clear winner, though Drip's pricing reflects its premium e-commerce features.

Can I switch from AWeber to Drip?

Yes, both platforms typically support contact list imports and exports. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your subscribers and import them elsewhere. Check both platforms' migration guides or contact their support teams for step-by-step assistance during the transition.
