Which Should You Choose?

Choose ConvertKit if…

  • You're a creator, blogger, or course seller needing advanced automation and sequences
  • You want built-in commerce features and subscriber tagging capabilities
  • You're willing to pay more for creator-focused tools with strong feature sets

Choose MailerLite if…

  • You're on a tight budget and want affordability without sacrificing core features
  • You prefer an intuitive drag-and-drop editor and beginner-friendly interface
  • You need pop-ups and surveys for lead generation and audience engagement

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertKit better than MailerLite?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. ConvertKit excels for creators with advanced automation and commerce features, while MailerLite offers superior value for budget-conscious small businesses. MailerLite has higher customer satisfaction ratings, but ConvertKit provides more specialized creator tools.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or MailerLite?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to ConvertKit's $29/month. Both offer free plans with 1,000 subscribers, but MailerLite's paid tier offers better value for cost-conscious users and small businesses.

Can I switch from MailerLite to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms support email list exports, making it possible to migrate your subscribers. Most email marketing tools allow CSV exports, so you can transfer your contacts to ConvertKit if needed. Review each platform's migration guides for a smooth transition.
