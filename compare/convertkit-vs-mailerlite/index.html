Frequently asked questions

Is ConvertKit better than MailerLite?

It depends on your needs. ConvertKit excels for creators with advanced automation and commerce features, while MailerLite is superior for budget-conscious users who need simplicity. MailerLite has higher customer satisfaction ratings overall, but ConvertKit offers more specialized creator-focused functionality.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or MailerLite?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to ConvertKit's $29/month. Both offer free tiers supporting up to 1,000 subscribers, but MailerLite provides better value for small businesses and those testing email marketing.

Can I switch from MailerLite to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms support contact list exports and imports. Most email marketing platforms allow CSV exports, making migration straightforward. However, you may need to manually recreate automations and sequences on your new platform, as these often don't transfer directly between systems.
