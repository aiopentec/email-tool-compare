MailerLite and Constant Contact are both established email marketing platforms, but they target different business types and budgets. MailerLite emphasizes affordability and ease of use with a free tier, while Constant Contact focuses on comprehensive features for local businesses and nonprofits. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right platform for your marketing needs.

Frequently asked questions

Is MailerLite better than Constant Contact?

MailerLite scores higher on user reviews and offers a free tier, making it better for budget-conscious businesses. However, Constant Contact has more advanced features like SMS and event management. The best choice depends on your specific needs and budget.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Constant Contact?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, with a free plan supporting up to 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $9/month. Constant Contact has no free tier and starts at $12/month, making MailerLite the more affordable option overall.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms support list imports and exports, making migration straightforward. You can typically export your contacts and campaign history from Constant Contact and import them into MailerLite. Most integrations and automations will need to be reconfigured on the new platform.
