Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than Klaviyo?

It depends on your needs. MailerLite is better for budgets and ease of use, particularly for small businesses. Klaviyo is better for e-commerce brands needing advanced automation and analytics. Both have the same G2 rating (4.6/5), but MailerLite scores higher on Trustpilot (4.5 vs 3.8).

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or Klaviyo?

MailerLite is significantly more affordable. It offers a free plan for up to 1,000 subscribers and starts at just $9/month for paid plans. Klaviyo's free plan caps at 500 subscribers and paid plans begin at $45/month, making it 5x more expensive at entry level.

Can I switch from Klaviyo to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms support standard email marketing data exports. You can export your subscriber lists and import them into MailerLite. However, complex automation workflows and historical campaign data may require manual setup, so plan migration carefully to avoid losing important information.
