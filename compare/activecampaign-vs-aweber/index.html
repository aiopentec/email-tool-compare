Frequently asked questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than AWeber?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign is better for businesses requiring advanced automation, CRM, and lead scoring capabilities. AWeber is better for beginners and solopreneurs who prioritize simplicity and affordability. Both have strong ratings, but they serve different market segments.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or AWeber?

AWeber is cheaper to start with—it offers a free tier for up to 500 subscribers. Both platforms begin paid plans at $15/month, but AWeber's free option makes it the more affordable choice for budget-conscious small businesses. ActiveCampaign has no free tier but offers more advanced features at its entry price.

Can I switch from AWeber to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, both platforms support contact list exports and imports, making migration relatively straightforward. However, you may lose some AWeber-specific features (like RSS-to-email) if switching to ActiveCampaign, and vice versa. Plan ahead to ensure your workflows are compatible with your new platform.
