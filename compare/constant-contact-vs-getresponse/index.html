GetResponse vs Constant Contact: Detailed Comparison

GetResponse and Constant Contact are both established email marketing platforms, but they serve different business needs. GetResponse excels for course creators and webinar hosts with advanced automation, while Constant Contact focuses on nonprofits and local businesses with straightforward tools. Choose based on your primary use case and required feature depth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GetResponse better than Constant Contact?

GetResponse offers more comprehensive features for course creators, webinar hosts, and marketers seeking advanced automation and funnel-building. However, Constant Contact is simpler for nonprofits and local businesses, and includes SMS marketing. The better choice depends on your specific business model and technical comfort level.

Which is cheaper, GetResponse or Constant Contact?

Constant Contact starts at $12/month, making it $7 cheaper than GetResponse's $19/month entry point. However, GetResponse's free tier for up to 500 subscribers provides a cost-free option for testing before committing to paid plans, which Constant Contact doesn't offer.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to GetResponse?

Yes, both platforms support email list imports, allowing you to migrate your contacts. Most modern email marketing tools can import CSV files or connect via integrations. Plan for some setup time to recreate campaigns and automation workflows in your new platform.
