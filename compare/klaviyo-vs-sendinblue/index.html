Klaviyo and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are two popular email marketing platforms with different strengths. Klaviyo excels at e-commerce automation, while Sendinblue offers broader CRM capabilities and a more generous free tier. Your choice depends on your business type, budget, and feature priorities.

Frequently asked questions

Is Klaviyo better than Sendinblue?

Klaviyo scores higher on G2 (4.6 vs 4.5) and offers superior e-commerce features, making it better for online retailers. However, Sendinblue has a higher Trustpilot rating (4.3 vs 3.8) and provides better value for businesses needing transactional email and SMS at lower costs.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is significantly cheaper for small businesses, starting at $25/month compared to Klaviyo's $45/month for paid plans. Sendinblue also offers a more generous free tier with up to 100,000 subscribers plus 300 free emails daily, versus Klaviyo's 500-subscriber limit.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Klaviyo?

Yes, most email marketing platforms allow you to export your contact lists and migrate to another service. Both Klaviyo and Sendinblue typically support CSV exports, though you may lose some historical campaign data or automation workflows during the transition.
