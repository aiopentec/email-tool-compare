Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Brevo?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers seeking monetization and growth features, while Brevo is superior for businesses needing transactional email, SMS, and integrated CRM capabilities. Beehiiv has a higher G2 rating (4.7 vs 4.5), reflecting strong publisher satisfaction.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Brevo?

Brevo offers better value for most users. Its free tier supports 100,000 subscribers compared to Beehiiv's 2,500, and paid plans start at $25/month versus Beehiiv's $39/month. However, Beehiiv's pricing is more justifiable if you specifically need newsletter monetization features.

Can I switch from Brevo to Beehiiv?

Yes, switching is straightforward. Both platforms support CSV exports and API integrations, allowing you to migrate subscriber lists and campaign data. Plan your migration during a natural transition point and test imports thoroughly to ensure data integrity before fully switching.
