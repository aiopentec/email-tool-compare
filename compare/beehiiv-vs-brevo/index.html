Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Brevo?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers seeking monetization tools, while Brevo is superior for businesses needing multi-channel marketing and CRM capabilities. Beehiiv has slightly higher G2 and Trustpilot ratings, but Brevo offers more versatility for diverse marketing scenarios.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Brevo?

Brevo starts at $25/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month, making it more affordable for paid plans. However, Brevo's free tier allows 100,000 subscribers versus Beehiiv's 2,500, making Brevo substantially more generous for users staying on the free plan.

Can I switch from Brevo to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support email list imports through CSV files or API connections. Migration is typically straightforward, though you'll lose multi-channel features like SMS and WhatsApp if switching from Brevo. Plan your transition carefully to preserve subscriber data and campaign history.
