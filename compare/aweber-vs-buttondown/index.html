Frequently Asked Questions

Is Buttondown better than AWeber?

Neither is universally 'better'—it depends on your needs. Buttondown excels for developers and markdown enthusiasts with strong API features, while AWeber offers broader functionality and simplicity for general bloggers and solopreneurs. AWeber's higher G2 (4.2 vs 4.8) and Trustpilot (4.0 vs 4.7) ratings show Buttondown has higher user satisfaction, though both are well-regarded.

Which is cheaper, Buttondown or AWeber?

Buttondown is cheaper on paid plans at $9/month versus AWeber's $15/month. However, AWeber's free tier allows 500 subscribers compared to Buttondown's 100, making AWeber more cost-effective if you're starting out and want to grow without paying immediately.

Can I switch from AWeber to Buttondown?

Both platforms support import/export functionality, making migration possible. You can export your subscriber list from AWeber and import it into Buttondown. However, you'll need to manually recreate campaigns and automations, as these typically don't transfer between platforms.
