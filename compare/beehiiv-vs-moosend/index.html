Frequently asked questions

Is Beehiiv better than Moosend?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Beehiiv excels for newsletter creators wanting monetization features and audience growth tools, while Moosend is superior for small businesses needing affordable automation and unlimited emails. Both have identical 4.7 G2 and 4.4 Trustpilot ratings.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Moosend?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month. However, Beehiiv offers a larger free tier (2,500 vs 1,000 subscribers), so the best choice depends on your subscriber count and budget.

Can I switch from Moosend to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms typically allow you to export your subscriber lists and contact data. Most email marketing platforms support standard CSV or API-based migrations, though you may need to manually recreate campaigns, automation workflows, and templates in your new platform.
