Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Moosend?

It depends on your use case. Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers with monetization needs, while Moosend is better for small businesses seeking affordable automation. Both have identical G2 and Trustpilot ratings (4.7 and 4.4), so the choice comes down to your specific features and budget requirements.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Moosend?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, starting at just $9/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month. However, Beehiiv offers a higher free tier with 2,500 subscribers versus Moosend's 1,000. For budget-conscious users, Moosend provides exceptional value for automation features.

Can I switch from Moosend to Beehiiv?

Yes, you can migrate your contacts and subscriber lists between platforms using standard email export/import features. Most email marketing tools support CSV exports, making the transition relatively straightforward. However, you may need to manually reconfigure automations and designs in your new platform.
