Brevo excels as an all-in-one CRM with multi-channel capabilities, while GetResponse focuses on course creators and webinar hosts. This comparison will help you decide which platform aligns best with your marketing goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo better than GetResponse?

It depends on your use case. Brevo is better for multi-channel marketing, CRM, and transactional emails, earning higher ratings (4.5 vs 4.2 on G2). GetResponse excels for webinar hosts and course creators with specialized tools. Brevo's superior overall features and larger free tier make it the better choice for most businesses.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or GetResponse?

GetResponse's paid plans start at $19/month compared to Brevo's $25/month, making it $6 cheaper monthly. However, Brevo's free tier is dramatically larger (100,000 vs 500 subscribers), making it more cost-effective for growing businesses that want to stay free longer.

Can I switch from GetResponse to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms support contact list exports and imports, making migration possible. Most email marketing platforms allow you to export subscriber lists in standard formats like CSV. You should plan your migration to preserve automation workflows and segment data where applicable.
