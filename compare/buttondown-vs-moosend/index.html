Buttondown caters to developers and technical writers who value markdown editing, while Moosend targets small businesses seeking unlimited emails and advanced automation. Frequently asked questions

Is Buttondown better than Moosend?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. Buttondown excels for technical users who love markdown and API access, while Moosend is superior for small businesses wanting unlimited emails and more marketing features. Moosend's larger free tier (1,000 vs 100 subscribers) gives it an edge for budget-conscious startups.

Which is cheaper, Buttondown or Moosend?

Both start at $9/month for paid plans, making them equally affordable. However, Moosend's free tier supports 10x more subscribers (1,000 vs 100), so it's more cost-effective if you're growing an audience slowly. Moosend also offers unlimited emails even on paid plans, while Buttondown's pricing scales with your subscriber count.

Can I switch from Moosend to Buttondown?

Yes, both platforms support import/export functionality, making migration straightforward. Buttondown explicitly offers import/export features, and Moosend provides data export capabilities. However, you'll need to reconfigure automations and landing pages manually, as these features don't always transfer automatically between platforms.
