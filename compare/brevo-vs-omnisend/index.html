Brevo vs Omnisend: Detailed Comparison

Brevo and Omnisend are both freemium email marketing platforms with SMS capabilities, but they serve different business needs. Brevo positions itself as an all-in-one CRM suite with broad multi-channel support, while Omnisend specializes in e-commerce automation. This comparison helps you choose the right platform based on your business model and priorities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo better than Omnisend?

It depends on your use case. Brevo is better for businesses needing multi-channel marketing, CRM integration, and transactional email capabilities. Omnisend is better for e-commerce brands specifically wanting specialized automation and SMS together. Both have strong ratings (Brevo 4.5/5 on G2, Omnisend 4.6/5 on Trustpilot).

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Omnisend?

Omnisend has a lower paid entry point at $16/month compared to Brevo's $25/month. However, Brevo's free tier is significantly more generous with 100,000 free subscribers versus Omnisend's 250, making Brevo the better choice if you're testing for extended periods without payment.

Can I switch from Omnisend to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms support data export and most email service providers offer migration assistance. You can export your contacts and campaign data from Omnisend and import them into Brevo. Most integrations will require reconfiguration, so plan migration during a low-activity period to minimize disruption.
