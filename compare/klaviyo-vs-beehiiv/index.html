Beehiiv focuses on newsletter creators and publishers seeking monetization options, while Klaviyo specializes in e-commerce marketing automation. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Klaviyo?

Neither is universally better—they excel in different areas. Beehiiv is superior for newsletter publishers with its monetization features and larger free tier, while Klaviyo dominates e-commerce marketing with advanced automation and native Shopify integration. Your choice depends on your specific business model.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Klaviyo?

Beehiiv offers a better free tier with 2,500 subscribers compared to Klaviyo's 500, making it more accessible initially. Paid plans start at $39 for Beehiiv versus $45 for Klaviyo, giving Beehiiv a slight pricing advantage at entry level.

Can I switch from Klaviyo to Beehiiv?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber lists and email data between platforms, though the process may require manual work depending on your data structure. Most email platforms support CSV exports and imports, making transitions feasible, though workflow automation rules typically need to be recreated manually.
