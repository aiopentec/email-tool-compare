Drip and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are both established email marketing platforms, but they serve different priorities. Drip focuses on e-commerce automation with deep store integration, while Sendinblue offers a broader all-in-one CRM suite with generous free tier limits. Frequently asked questions

Is Drip better than Sendinblue?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Drip excels for e-commerce businesses requiring advanced store integrations and revenue tracking. Sendinblue (Brevo) is superior for general marketing, multi-channel campaigns, and businesses on tight budgets. Sendinblue also has better overall user ratings on both G2 and Trustpilot.

Which is cheaper, Drip or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is significantly cheaper, offering a free tier with up to 100k subscribers and 300 emails daily. Paid plans start at $25/month. Drip has no free option and starts at $39/month. For budget-conscious businesses, Sendinblue is the clear winner.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Drip?

Yes, both platforms support data export and import capabilities. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your contact lists, and many provide migration assistance. However, you'll need to manually rebuild automation workflows and customize settings to match Drip's e-commerce-focused features.
