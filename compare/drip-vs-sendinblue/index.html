Drip vs Sendinblue: Complete Comparison Guide

Drip and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are both powerful email marketing platforms, but they cater to different business needs. Drip specializes in e-commerce automation with deep store integration, while Sendinblue offers a broader all-in-one CRM suite with generous free tier limits. This comparison will help you determine which platform is the right fit for your business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Drip better than Sendinblue?

It depends on your needs. Drip is superior for e-commerce businesses requiring deep integration and revenue tracking, with higher G2 and Trustpilot ratings reflecting strong e-commerce performance. However, Sendinblue is better for startups and businesses needing an affordable, multi-channel platform with generous free tier limits.

Which is cheaper, Drip or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is significantly cheaper, offering a free plan with up to 100,000 subscribers and 300 free emails daily, while paid plans start at $25/month. Drip has no free tier and starts at $39/month, making it more expensive upfront but potentially better value for established e-commerce operations.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Drip?

Yes, most email marketing platforms allow data export and migration. You can typically export your subscriber lists and segments from Sendinblue and import them into Drip. However, you'll need to manually recreate automation workflows and campaigns, as each platform has different builders and features.
