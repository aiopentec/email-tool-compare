Beehiiv vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

Beehiiv and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different primary audiences. Beehiiv focuses on newsletter publishers seeking monetization and growth tools, while Sendinblue excels at transactional email and multi-channel communication. Understanding their strengths will help you choose the right platform for your specific use case.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Sendinblue?

Neither is objectively 'better'—it depends on your needs. Beehiiv excels for newsletter creators wanting monetization features, while Sendinblue is superior for businesses needing multi-channel communication and transactional email. Beehiiv's newer approach appeals to growth-focused publishers, whereas Sendinblue's maturity benefits enterprises.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue offers better value for most users: its free plan supports 100,000 subscribers compared to Beehiiv's 2,500, and paid plans start at $25 versus $39. However, Beehiiv's pricing becomes competitive if you need newsletter monetization features, which Sendinblue doesn't offer.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support subscriber list imports and exports. Most email marketing tools use standard CSV formats, making migration straightforward. However, you'll need to rebuild custom automations and segments, as these don't always transfer directly between platforms.
