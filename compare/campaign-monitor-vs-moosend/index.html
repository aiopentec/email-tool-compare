Frequently asked questions

Is Campaign Monitor better than Moosend?

Neither is objectively 'better'—it depends on your needs. Campaign Monitor excels for agencies managing multiple brands with sophisticated journey design, while Moosend offers superior value for small businesses with its free tier and unlimited sending. Moosend's higher customer ratings (4.7 on G2 vs 4.1) suggest broader user satisfaction overall.

Which is cheaper, Campaign Monitor or Moosend?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, especially for startups, with a free plan supporting up to 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $9/month. Campaign Monitor has no free tier and starts at $11/month. For small businesses, Moosend's unlimited emails at lower price points make it the more economical choice.

Can I switch from Moosend to Campaign Monitor?

Yes, most email marketing platforms allow subscriber list exports, making migration feasible. Both tools support standard email formats and integrations. However, you may lose advanced automation workflows during transfer, so it's wise to document your setup in Moosend before switching to avoid data loss.
