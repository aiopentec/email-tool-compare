Frequently Asked Questions

Is Campaign Monitor better than Moosend?

Neither is universally "better"—it depends on your needs. Campaign Monitor excels for agencies managing multiple brands with advanced automation, while Moosend offers superior value for small businesses through its free tier and unlimited emails. Moosend also has higher customer satisfaction ratings on both G2 and Trustpilot.

Which is cheaper, Campaign Monitor or Moosend?

Moosend is significantly cheaper. It offers a free plan for up to 1,000 subscribers, then starts at $9/month, whereas Campaign Monitor has no free tier and starts at $11/month. For small businesses, Moosend's freemium model provides excellent value.

Can I switch from Moosend to Campaign Monitor?

Yes, both platforms typically support standard email list exports and data migrations. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your subscriber lists and campaign data, though you may need to manually reconfigure automations and templates in Campaign Monitor. Check both platforms' migration documentation for specific details.
