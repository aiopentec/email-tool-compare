GetResponse vs AWeber: Complete Comparison

Both GetResponse and AWeber are established email marketing platforms founded in 1998, offering freemium models with similar free tiers. GetResponse positions itself as a comprehensive marketing suite with webinars and courses, while AWeber emphasizes simplicity for bloggers and solopreneurs. Your choice depends on whether you need advanced marketing automation or straightforward email campaigns.

Frequently asked questions

Is GetResponse better than AWeber?

It depends on your needs. GetResponse excels for creators building comprehensive marketing ecosystems with webinars and courses, while AWeber is better if you want straightforward email marketing with excellent simplicity. GetResponse has more features overall, but AWeber has better customer satisfaction ratings.

Which is cheaper, GetResponse or AWeber?

AWeber starts at $15/month compared to GetResponse's $19/month, making it the more budget-friendly option initially. However, both offer identical free tiers with 500 subscribers, so you can test either platform at no cost before committing to paid plans.

Can I switch from AWeber to GetResponse?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list exports and imports. You can export your subscriber list from AWeber and import it into GetResponse, though you may need to manually recreate automation workflows. Both platforms offer customer support during the migration process.
