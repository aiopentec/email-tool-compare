Frequently Asked Questions

Is Klaviyo better than AWeber?

Klaviyo is more powerful for e-commerce brands with advanced automation and predictive analytics, earning a higher G2 rating (4.6 vs 4.2). However, AWeber is better for beginners seeking simplicity and lower costs. The best choice depends on your specific business needs.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or AWeber?

AWeber is significantly cheaper, starting at $15/month compared to Klaviyo's $45/month. Both offer free tiers for up to 500 subscribers, making them accessible for testing before upgrading.

Can I switch from AWeber to Klaviyo?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists. Most email marketing tools support standard CSV imports, making migration relatively straightforward. However, you'll need to manually recreate automations and campaigns in the new platform.
