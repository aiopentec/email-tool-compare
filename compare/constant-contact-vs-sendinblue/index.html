Constant Contact vs Sendinblue: Complete Comparison

Constant Contact and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are two popular email marketing platforms with different strengths and pricing models. Constant Contact excels for nonprofits and local businesses seeking simplicity, while Sendinblue offers a more robust all-in-one CRM suite with a generous free tier. Your choice depends on budget, feature needs, and whether you require transactional email capabilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Constant Contact better than Sendinblue?

Neither is universally 'better'—it depends on your needs. Sendinblue has superior ratings (4.5 vs 4.0 on G2, 4.3 vs 2.8 on Trustpilot) and offers more comprehensive CRM features. However, Constant Contact is better suited for nonprofits and local businesses that need straightforward email marketing with event management and social media ad tools.

Which is cheaper, Constant Contact or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is significantly cheaper for budget-conscious users since it offers a free tier with 100k subscriber support and 300 daily emails at no cost. Constant Contact has no free option and starts at $12/month. However, Sendinblue's paid plans begin at $25/month, so Constant Contact is cheaper once you need paid features.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Constant Contact?

Yes, you can switch between platforms. Most email marketing services support list exports in standard formats (CSV). You'll need to import your contacts and recreate your campaigns in Constant Contact, though the process is straightforward for most users. Check both platforms' migration guides for detailed instructions.
