Constant Contact vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

Constant Contact and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are both established email marketing platforms, but they serve different needs. Constant Contact focuses on ease-of-use for nonprofits and local businesses, while Sendinblue excels at transactional email and omnichannel communication. This comparison helps you decide which tool aligns with your marketing goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Constant Contact better than Sendinblue?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Constant Contact excels for nonprofits and e-commerce with social media integration, while Sendinblue wins for transactional email, omnichannel communication, and those wanting a free tier. Sendinblue has significantly higher customer satisfaction ratings (4.3 vs 2.8 on Trustpilot).

Which is cheaper, Constant Contact or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is more affordable, especially for startups, because it offers a free plan with 300 emails daily and up to 100,000 subscribers. Constant Contact requires a paid subscription starting at $12/month with no free option. However, Constant Contact's pricing may become competitive for larger contact lists when comparing paid tiers.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Constant Contact?

Yes, both platforms support contact list exports, making migration feasible. Most email marketing platforms allow CSV imports, so transferring your subscriber data is straightforward. However, you'll need to manually recreate templates, automations, and campaign structures in the new platform.
