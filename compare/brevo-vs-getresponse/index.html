Brevo excels as an all-in-one CRM with multi-channel capabilities, while GetResponse is built for course creators and webinar hosts. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo better than GetResponse?

Brevo is better for general email marketing and multi-channel campaigns, offering superior CRM functionality and a much larger free tier. However, GetResponse is better if you specifically need webinar hosting and online course features. The choice depends on your business model.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or GetResponse?

GetResponse's paid plans start at $19/month versus Brevo's $25/month, making it cheaper for basic plans. However, Brevo's free tier supports 100,000 subscribers compared to GetResponse's 500, providing significantly better value for growing businesses without budget.

Can I switch from GetResponse to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms support contact list exports and imports. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your subscriber lists in CSV format and import them to Brevo. You should plan your email templates and automation flows in advance, as these typically require manual recreation.
