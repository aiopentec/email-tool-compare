Frequently Asked Questions

Is Drip better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your business type. Drip is superior for e-commerce businesses requiring advanced tracking and automation, with higher customer satisfaction ratings (4.4 vs 4.0 on G2). However, Constant Contact offers broader digital marketing features and is more budget-friendly for small nonprofits and local businesses.

Which is cheaper, Drip or Constant Contact?

Constant Contact is significantly cheaper, starting at $12/month compared to Drip's $39/month. If cost is your primary concern and you need basic email marketing, Constant Contact is the more affordable option. However, Drip's higher price reflects its specialized e-commerce features and stronger ratings.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Drip?

Yes, you can migrate your email lists and contacts from Constant Contact to Drip. Most email platforms support standard import formats like CSV files. However, Constant Contact's campaign history and detailed analytics won't transfer, so you'll start fresh with automation workflows in Drip. Check both platforms' migration guides for specific instructions.
