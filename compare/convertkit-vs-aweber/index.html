Frequently asked questions

Is ConvertKit better than AWeber?

ConvertKit offers more advanced features and better ratings on G2, making it superior for creators and course sellers. However, AWeber excels in simplicity and customer satisfaction, earning a higher Trustpilot rating. The better choice depends on your specific needs—choose ConvertKit for feature richness or AWeber for ease of use.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or AWeber?

AWeber is significantly cheaper, starting at $15/month compared to ConvertKit's $29/month. However, ConvertKit offers a more generous free tier with 1,000 subscribers versus AWeber's 500. For budget-conscious users, AWeber wins on pricing, but ConvertKit's free tier provides more runway before paid plans.

Can I switch from AWeber to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms support subscriber list exports and imports. Most email marketing tools allow CSV exports, making migration straightforward. Both ConvertKit and AWeber provide customer support to assist with the migration process, ensuring you can seamlessly transfer your contacts and maintain your email marketing continuity.
