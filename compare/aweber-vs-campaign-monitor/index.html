Frequently Asked Questions

Is Campaign Monitor better than AWeber?

It depends on your needs. Campaign Monitor is better for agencies and teams managing multiple brands with advanced automation requirements. AWeber is better for beginners and solopreneurs seeking simplicity and affordability. Campaign Monitor has stronger G2 reviews for features, while AWeber has better Trustpilot ratings for overall customer satisfaction.

Which is cheaper, Campaign Monitor or AWeber?

AWeber is cheaper overall, offering a free tier with up to 500 subscribers and paid plans starting at $15/month. Campaign Monitor has no free tier and starts at $11/month, making it more expensive from the beginning. However, Campaign Monitor's pricing is competitive for agencies needing multi-brand capabilities.

Can I switch from AWeber to Campaign Monitor?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your contacts and use standard CSV formats for migration. Most email marketing tools, including Campaign Monitor, can import subscriber lists from AWeber. Plan for some downtime and test your automation sequences in the new platform before switching your campaigns live.
