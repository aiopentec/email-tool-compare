Frequently asked questions

Is Substack better than Sendinblue?

It depends on your use case. Substack wins for writers monetising directly from subscribers, while Sendinblue is stronger for transactional email and SMS campaigns. Neither is objectively better — the right choice depends on your specific needs.

Which is cheaper, Substack or Sendinblue?

Substack starts at a lower price point ($0/mo vs $25/mo). However, pricing scales with subscriber count, so the total cost depends on your list size.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Substack?

Yes, switching from Sendinblue to Substack is possible. Most platforms support CSV import/export for subscriber lists. Plan for some time to recreate automations and templates, as these rarely transfer directly between platforms.
