Frequently Asked Questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than GetResponse?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign wins for CRM and automation-focused businesses with higher customer ratings (4.5 vs 4.2 on G2). However, GetResponse is better for online educators and webinar hosts who need those specific features included. Choose based on whether you prioritize CRM/automation or content delivery features.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or GetResponse?

GetResponse is cheaper to start since it offers a free tier supporting up to 500 subscribers, making it ideal for beginners or testing. ActiveCampaign has no free tier and starts at $15/month, but paid GetResponse plans begin at $19/month. For small businesses, GetResponse's free tier provides better value initially.

Can I switch from GetResponse to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, both platforms support data export and import, though you should plan the migration carefully. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your contact lists and create similar automation workflows in the new platform. ActiveCampaign's CRM features may require manual setup for full functionality since GetResponse doesn't have equivalent CRM depth.
