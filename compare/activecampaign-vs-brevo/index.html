Frequently asked questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Brevo?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign offers superior automation and lead scoring, making it better for complex B2B sales processes. Brevo excels in multi-channel communication and provides better value for small businesses or startups with its generous free tier. Both have similar G2 ratings (4.5), but ActiveCampaign edges ahead for advanced users.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Brevo?

Brevo is significantly cheaper for startups and small teams, with a free plan supporting up to 100,000 contacts. ActiveCampaign has no free option and starts at $15/month. However, at higher contact volumes and paid plans, pricing becomes more comparable, and ActiveCampaign's advanced features may justify the cost.

Can I switch from Brevo to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, you can migrate between platforms. Both support standard data exports and imports. Most migration involves exporting your contact list, email templates, and automation rules from Brevo and importing them into ActiveCampaign. Consider timing this during a low-activity period to ensure clean data transfer.
