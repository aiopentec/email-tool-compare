Beehiiv vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

Beehiiv and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences. Beehiiv focuses on newsletter publishers seeking monetization and growth tools, while Sendinblue excels at transactional email, SMS, and comprehensive CRM functionality. Understanding their strengths will help you choose the right platform for your specific needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than Sendinblue?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your use case. Beehiiv excels for newsletter publishers seeking monetization features, while Sendinblue is superior for businesses needing transactional email, SMS, and CRM tools. Sendinblue offers better value for most businesses due to its broader feature set and larger free tier.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is more affordable, starting at $25/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month. Additionally, Sendinblue's free tier accommodates 100,000 subscribers versus Beehiiv's 2,500, making it significantly cheaper for growing businesses.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Beehiiv?

Yes, you can migrate your subscriber lists and campaigns between platforms. Both tools support CSV exports and imports, though you may need to manually reconfigure segments, automation workflows, and design templates during the transition.
