Beehiiv vs Omnisend: Detailed Comparison

Beehiiv and Omnisend are both freemium email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences. Beehiiv is built for newsletter publishers and content creators seeking monetization options, while Omnisend caters to e-commerce brands wanting integrated email and SMS capabilities. Understanding their core strengths will help you pick the right tool for your business.

Frequently asked questions

Is Beehiiv better than Omnisend?

Neither is objectively 'better'—it depends on your needs. Beehiiv excels at newsletter monetization and serves publishers better, while Omnisend is superior for e-commerce brands. Both have strong ratings, with Beehiiv slightly ahead on G2 (4.7 vs 4.5) and Omnisend ahead on Trustpilot (4.6 vs 4.4).

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Omnisend?

Omnisend is cheaper for paid plans, starting at $16/month versus Beehiiv's $39/month. However, Beehiiv offers a larger free tier with 2,500 subscribers compared to Omnisend's 250. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize free features or paid affordability.

Can I switch from Omnisend to Beehiiv?

Yes, most email platforms allow you to export your subscriber list and switch. Both tools support standard CSV imports, so migrating your contacts is straightforward. However, note that Beehiiv lacks SMS functionality, so you may lose that capability if you're currently using Omnisend's SMS features.
