Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than AWeber?

It depends on your needs. Mailchimp offers more advanced features and better G2 ratings, making it ideal for businesses scaling quickly. However, AWeber has significantly better Trustpilot ratings and a simpler interface, making it preferable for users who value ease of use and customer support.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or AWeber?

Both platforms offer identical free tiers with 500 subscriber limits. Paid plans are nearly identical in price, with Mailchimp starting at $13/month and AWeber at $15/month. The difference is minimal, so pricing shouldn't be a deciding factor.

Can I switch from AWeber to Mailchimp?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists, making migration straightforward. Most email marketing tools support CSV exports that can be imported into Mailchimp. However, you'll need to recreate automations and campaigns manually in your new platform.
