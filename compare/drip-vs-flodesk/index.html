Drip vs Flodesk: Detailed Comparison

Drip and Flodesk are both premium email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences. Drip specializes in e-commerce automation with deep store integration, while Flodesk focuses on visual design and unlimited subscribers at a flat rate. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize sales tracking and commerce features or beautiful email design and subscriber flexibility.

Which Should You Choose?

Choose Drip if…

  • You run an e-commerce store and need revenue attribution and sales tracking
  • You require SMS marketing alongside email campaigns
  • You want onsite campaigns and deep store integration features

Choose Flodesk if…

  • You prioritize stunning email design and have a visually-focused brand
  • You have or expect to grow a large subscriber list and want predictable flat-rate pricing
  • You need a user-friendly platform with link-in-bio and checkout form features

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Drip better than Flodesk?

It depends on your needs. Drip is better for e-commerce businesses requiring detailed sales tracking and SMS integration, while Flodesk excels for visual brands prioritizing design quality. Flodesk has higher customer ratings on both G2 and Trustpilot, but Drip offers more commerce-specific features.

Which is cheaper, Drip or Flodesk?

Both start at approximately $38-39/month, but pricing diverges as you grow. Flodesk's flat-rate model means unlimited subscribers for one price, making it cheaper for scaling lists. Drip's per-subscriber model becomes more expensive as your audience grows, but offers more granular features for that cost.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Drip?

Yes, both platforms support standard email export and list migration. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your subscriber lists in CSV format. However, you may need to rebuild workflows and automation sequences in the new platform, as these don't always transfer directly.
