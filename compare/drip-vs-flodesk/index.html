Frequently asked questions

Is Drip better than Flodesk?

It depends on your needs. Drip excels for e-commerce businesses requiring advanced revenue tracking and SMS, while Flodesk is superior for visual brands prioritizing design quality and simplicity. Flodesk has higher customer ratings overall, but Drip offers more specialized commerce features.

Which is cheaper, Drip or Flodesk?

Both start at nearly the same price ($39 vs $38), but Flodesk's flat-rate pricing includes unlimited subscribers, while Drip's pricing scales with subscriber count. For larger lists, Flodesk becomes significantly more cost-effective.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Drip?

Yes, most email platforms support standard CSV exports of your subscriber list. However, you'll need to manually recreate workflows and campaigns in Drip. Consider using migration services or tools to streamline the process.
