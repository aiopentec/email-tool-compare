ConvertKit vs Klaviyo: Detailed Comparison for 2025

ConvertKit and Klaviyo are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and use cases. ConvertKit is purpose-built for creators and course sellers, while Klaviyo specializes in e-commerce marketing automation. Choosing between them depends on your business model, budget, and specific feature requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertKit better than Klaviyo?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your use case. ConvertKit excels for creators and course sellers with its landing pages and affordable pricing, while Klaviyo dominates for e-commerce businesses with superior integrations and predictive analytics. ConvertKit scores higher on Trustpilot (3.2 vs 3.8), though both are well-regarded.

Which is cheaper, ConvertKit or Klaviyo?

ConvertKit is more affordable overall, starting at $29/month compared to Klaviyo's $45/month. ConvertKit also offers a more generous free tier (1,000 subscribers vs 500), making it better for bootstrapped creators. However, Klaviyo's pricing may offer better value if you need e-commerce-specific features.

Can I switch from Klaviyo to ConvertKit?

Yes, both platforms support email list exports, making migration possible. However, you may lose some platform-specific features like Klaviyo's SMS flows or advanced segmentation. ConvertKit offers tools for importing subscribers, though complex automation sequences may require manual recreation.
