Frequently Asked Questions

Is Klaviyo better than Constant Contact?

It depends on your needs. Klaviyo is superior for e-commerce businesses requiring advanced automation and analytics, with higher user satisfaction scores (4.6 vs 4.0 on G2). However, Constant Contact may be better for nonprofits and local businesses seeking simplicity and lower starting costs.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or Constant Contact?

Constant Contact starts at $12/month, making it cheaper upfront. However, Klaviyo offers a free tier for up to 500 subscribers, making it the most cost-effective option for small businesses just starting out. Both platforms' pricing scales with your subscriber count.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Klaviyo?

Yes, you can switch between platforms. Both tools support contact list exports, and Klaviyo has integrations that can help with data migration. You should plan the transition carefully to avoid campaign disruption and ensure all historical data is properly transferred.
