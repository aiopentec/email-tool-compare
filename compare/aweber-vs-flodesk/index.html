AWeber and Flodesk are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and priorities. AWeber excels at affordability and ease-of-use for small businesses, while Flodesk focuses on design-first email creation with unlimited subscribers. Frequently asked questions

Is AWeber better than Flodesk?

Neither is universally 'better'—it depends on your priorities. AWeber wins for budget-conscious beginners with its free tier and low starting price of $15/month. Flodesk wins for visual brands and e-commerce with unlimited subscribers, superior design templates, and integrated checkout forms, despite its $38/month minimum cost.

Which is cheaper, AWeber or Flodesk?

AWeber is significantly cheaper, offering a free plan up to 500 subscribers and paid plans starting at $15/month. Flodesk has no free tier and starts at $38/month flat-rate for unlimited subscribers. For small lists (under 500 contacts), AWeber is free; for growing businesses, Flodesk's unlimited pricing may offer better long-term value.

Can I switch from Flodesk to AWeber?

Yes, you can export your subscriber lists and email content from Flodesk and import them into AWeber. Most email marketing platforms allow CSV exports, making migration straightforward. However, you may need to recreate some design elements or automations, as the platforms have different template and workflow systems.
