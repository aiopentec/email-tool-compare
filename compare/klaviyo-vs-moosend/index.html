Frequently Asked Questions

Is Klaviyo better than Moosend?

It depends on your use case. Klaviyo excels for e-commerce businesses needing sophisticated automation and analytics, with a higher G2 rating (4.6 vs 4.7) and strong Trustpilot score (3.8). Moosend wins on price and simplicity, making it better for small businesses and beginners seeking affordable email marketing.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or Moosend?

Moosend is significantly cheaper, starting at just $9/month compared to Klaviyo's $45/month. Moosend's free tier also includes 1,000 subscribers versus Klaviyo's 500, making it the clear choice for budget-conscious businesses.

Can I switch from Moosend to Klaviyo?

Yes, most email marketing platforms support contact list exports and imports. Both tools allow you to export your subscriber data, which you can then import into Klaviyo. Check with both platforms' support teams for detailed migration guides to ensure a smooth transition.
