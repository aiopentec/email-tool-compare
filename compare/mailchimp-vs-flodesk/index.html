Mailchimp vs Flodesk: Complete Comparison for 2025

Mailchimp and Flodesk are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and use cases. Mailchimp excels as an affordable, feature-rich option for beginners and growing businesses, while Flodesk targets visual creators with beautiful design templates and unlimited subscriber scaling. This guide compares their pricing, features, and performance to help you choose the right platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than Flodesk?

It depends on your needs. Mailchimp is better for beginners and those needing advanced automation features at a lower cost, while Flodesk excels for visually-driven brands and offers superior customer satisfaction ratings. Flodesk scores higher on G2 (4.8 vs 4.3) and Trustpilot (4.7 vs 1.5), suggesting better overall user experience.

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Flodesk?

Mailchimp is significantly cheaper upfront, offering a free plan for up to 500 subscribers and paid plans starting at $13/month. Flodesk starts at $38/month with no free tier, but includes unlimited subscribers from day one, making it more cost-effective for larger lists.

Can I switch from Flodesk to Mailchimp?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and email data. The migration process typically involves exporting contacts as CSV files from Flodesk and importing them into Mailchimp. Check both platforms' documentation for specific steps and any potential formatting adjustments needed.
