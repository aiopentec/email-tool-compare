Brevo vs Buttondown: Detailed Comparison for 2025

Brevo and Buttondown are both freemium email marketing platforms, but they serve very different audiences. Brevo is an all-in-one CRM suite built for businesses needing multi-channel marketing and transactional email, while Buttondown specializes in newsletter creation for developers and technical writers. Your choice depends on whether you need a comprehensive marketing platform or a focused, markdown-friendly newsletter tool.

Frequently asked questions

Is Brevo better than Buttondown?

It depends on your needs. Brevo is better if you need a comprehensive CRM with multi-channel marketing capabilities. Buttondown is better if you're a developer or writer wanting a simple, markdown-focused newsletter platform. Both excel in their respective niches.

Which is cheaper, Brevo or Buttondown?

Buttondown's paid plan starts at $9/month compared to Brevo's $25/month. However, Brevo's free tier supports 100,000 subscribers versus Buttondown's 100, making Brevo better value if you have many free contacts.

Can I switch from Buttondown to Brevo?

Yes, both platforms offer import/export functionality. Buttondown explicitly lists import/export as a feature, and Brevo's broader feature set makes it easy to migrate your subscriber list and campaigns. Check your current provider's documentation for specific migration steps.
