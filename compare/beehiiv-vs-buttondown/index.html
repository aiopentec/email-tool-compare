Frequently asked questions

Is Beehiiv better than Buttondown?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. Beehiiv excels at monetization and scaling with larger audiences, while Buttondown is better for technical users who prefer simplicity and markdown. Beehiiv has higher ratings on both G2 (4.7) and Trustpilot (4.4), though Buttondown ranks slightly higher on G2 (4.8) and significantly higher on Trustpilot (4.7).

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Buttondown?

Buttondown offers a more affordable paid plan starting at $9/month compared to Beehiiv's $39/month. However, Beehiiv's free tier accommodates 2,500 subscribers versus Buttondown's 100, making Beehiiv better value if you have a growing audience. Your choice depends on subscriber count and budget.

Can I switch from Buttondown to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support import and export functionality, making migration possible. Buttondown explicitly lists import/export as a key feature, and Beehiiv's analytics and segmentation tools can help you rebuild your audience structure. Plan ahead for any automation or workflow changes during the transition.
