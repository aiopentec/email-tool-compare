Frequently asked questions

Is Beehiiv better than Buttondown?

It depends on your use case. Beehiiv wins for newsletter publishers and media operators, while Buttondown is stronger for developers and technical writers who prefer markdown. Neither is objectively better — the right choice depends on your specific needs.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or Buttondown?

Buttondown starts at a lower price point ($9/mo vs $39/mo). However, pricing scales with subscriber count, so the total cost depends on your list size.

Can I switch from Buttondown to Beehiiv?

Yes, switching from Buttondown to Beehiiv is possible. Most platforms support CSV import/export for subscriber lists. Plan for some time to recreate automations and templates, as these rarely transfer directly between platforms.
