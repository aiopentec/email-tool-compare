Buttondown vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

Buttondown and Sendinblue (now Brevo) serve different email marketing needs, making the choice dependent on your specific use case. Buttondown excels for newsletter writers and developers who value simplicity and markdown support, while Sendinblue offers a comprehensive CRM suite with SMS and transactional email capabilities. Understanding their strengths will help you select the platform that best matches your business requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Buttondown better than Sendinblue?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Buttondown is superior for newsletter writers who love markdown and want a streamlined experience, with higher user satisfaction ratings (4.8 vs 4.5 on G2). Sendinblue excels for businesses needing omnichannel marketing, transactional email, and CRM features at a broader scale.

Which is cheaper, Buttondown or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue offers significantly better value on the free tier, supporting 100,000 subscribers versus Buttondown's 100. However, Buttondown's paid plans start at $9/month compared to Sendinblue's $25/month. For small projects, Sendinblue's free tier may eliminate upgrade costs entirely.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Buttondown?

Yes, both tools support import/export functionality, making migration possible. However, you'll lose Sendinblue-specific features like SMS and CRM data in the transfer. Buttondown is better suited for newsletter-focused workflows, so some transactional features may not transfer seamlessly.
