Buttondown vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

Buttondown and Sendinblue serve different email marketing needs despite both offering freemium models. Buttondown excels for newsletter writers and developers using markdown, while Sendinblue (now Brevo) is a comprehensive CRM platform with SMS, chat, and transactional email capabilities. Choosing between them depends on whether you prioritize simplicity or multi-channel marketing power.

Frequently asked questions

Is Buttondown better than Sendinblue?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. Buttondown excels for simple newsletter writing with markdown support, while Sendinblue wins for businesses needing comprehensive CRM features, SMS, and multi-channel marketing. Buttondown has higher user ratings (4.8 vs 4.5 on G2), but Sendinblue offers more functionality for larger operations.

Which is cheaper, Buttondown or Sendinblue?

Buttondown is cheaper for small lists, starting at $9/month versus Sendinblue's $25/month. However, Sendinblue's free tier accommodates 100,000 subscribers compared to Buttondown's 100, making Sendinblue more cost-effective for growing businesses. The best choice depends on your subscriber count and feature needs.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Buttondown?

Yes, both platforms support import/export functionality, making migration straightforward. You can export your subscriber list and campaigns from Sendinblue and import them into Buttondown. However, note that Buttondown focuses on newsletters while Sendinblue handles transactional email, so some features may not transfer directly.
