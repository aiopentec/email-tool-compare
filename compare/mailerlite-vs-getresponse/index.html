MailerLite vs GetResponse: A Detailed Comparison

Both MailerLite and GetResponse are freemium email marketing platforms that serve different audience needs. MailerLite focuses on simplicity for small businesses, while GetResponse positions itself as a comprehensive online marketing platform with webinar and course creation capabilities. Understanding their differences in pricing, features, and target use cases will help you choose the right tool for your business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than GetResponse?

MailerLite is better for most small businesses and e-commerce owners due to its simplicity, affordability, and superior ratings (4.6 on G2 vs 4.2). However, GetResponse is better if you specifically need webinar hosting, online course creation, or advanced funnel-building tools.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or GetResponse?

MailerLite is significantly cheaper, with a free plan supporting 1,000 subscribers and paid plans starting at $9/month. GetResponse offers only 500 free subscribers and starts at $19/month, making MailerLite the more budget-friendly choice for growing lists.

Can I switch from GetResponse to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms allow you to export your contact lists and email templates. Most email marketing tools support standard CSV or contact import formats, making migration relatively straightforward. You may need to manually recreate some automations depending on their complexity.
