Frequently asked questions

Is Campaign Monitor better than Constant Contact?

Campaign Monitor edges ahead for agencies and teams managing multiple brands, with stronger automation and segmentation tools. However, Constant Contact is better if you need SMS, social ads, and e-commerce features integrated into one platform. The choice depends on your specific business needs rather than overall superiority.

Which is cheaper, Campaign Monitor or Constant Contact?

Pricing is nearly identical, with Campaign Monitor starting at $11/month and Constant Contact at $12/month. Both platforms offer no free tier, so you'll need to commit to paid plans immediately. The actual cost difference will depend on your subscriber count and feature selections.

Can I switch from Constant Contact to Campaign Monitor?

Yes, switching between platforms is possible. Both tools typically support list export functions, allowing you to download your contacts and import them into Campaign Monitor. However, you'll need to rebuild automation sequences and redesign templates, as these features don't transfer directly between platforms.
