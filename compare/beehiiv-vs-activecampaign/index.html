Your choice depends on whether you prioritize newsletter growth and monetization or advanced marketing automation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than ActiveCampaign?

Neither is universally better—they serve different purposes. Beehiiv excels for newsletter creators seeking growth and monetization tools, while ActiveCampaign is superior for businesses needing enterprise-level automation and CRM features. Your choice should align with your primary use case.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or ActiveCampaign?

Beehiiv is cheaper upfront since it offers a free tier for up to 2,500 subscribers. ActiveCampaign has no free option and starts at $15/month. However, Beehiiv's paid plans start at $39/month, so ActiveCampaign becomes competitive for small teams needing automation.

Can I switch from ActiveCampaign to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support data export and import. However, ActiveCampaign's advanced automation workflows and CRM data may not fully transfer to Beehiiv, which is newsletter-focused. Plan your migration carefully and contact both support teams for detailed guidance.
