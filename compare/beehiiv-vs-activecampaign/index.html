Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beehiiv better than ActiveCampaign?

Neither is objectively better—they serve different needs. Beehiiv excels for newsletter creators seeking monetization and audience growth tools, while ActiveCampaign is superior for businesses requiring sophisticated automation and CRM features. Your choice should align with your primary use case.

Which is cheaper, Beehiiv or ActiveCampaign?

Beehiiv offers a free tier supporting up to 2,500 subscribers, making it cheaper for startups. ActiveCampaign has no free option but starts at $15/month. At scale, pricing depends on subscriber count and feature requirements for both platforms.

Can I switch from ActiveCampaign to Beehiiv?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list imports. However, you'll lose ActiveCampaign-specific features like CRM data and automation workflows during migration. Plan your transition carefully and export your contacts in CSV format for seamless transfer.
