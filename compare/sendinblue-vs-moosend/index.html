Moosend vs Sendinblue: Complete Comparison Guide

Both Moosend and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are popular freemium email marketing platforms, but they serve different business needs. Moosend excels at unlimited email campaigns with powerful automation, while Sendinblue focuses on omnichannel communication including SMS, WhatsApp, and transactional email. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right tool for your business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Moosend better than Sendinblue?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. Moosend wins for budget-conscious email-only campaigns with unlimited sends, while Sendinblue excels if you need omnichannel marketing (SMS, WhatsApp) or transactional email capabilities. Sendinblue's free tier is significantly more generous with 100,000 subscribers vs. Moosend's 1,000.

Which is cheaper, Moosend or Sendinblue?

Moosend is cheaper at $9/month compared to Sendinblue's $25/month. However, Sendinblue's free tier supports 100,000 subscribers (vs. 1,000 on Moosend), making it more cost-effective for growing businesses that don't need paid features yet. Compare your subscriber count and feature needs to determine the true cost.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Moosend?

Yes, both platforms support list imports and exports, making migration straightforward. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your contacts and import them into another platform. However, you'll need to recreate automation workflows and email templates, as these typically don't transfer between platforms.
