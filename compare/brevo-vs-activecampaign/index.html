Frequently Asked Questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Brevo?

ActiveCampaign excels in advanced automation and lead scoring, making it better for sophisticated B2B operations. However, Brevo offers superior value for startups and small businesses with its generous free plan and multi-channel capabilities. The "better" choice depends on your business size, budget, and specific automation needs.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Brevo?

Brevo is significantly cheaper for early-stage businesses, offering a free plan with up to 100,000 subscribers at no cost. ActiveCampaign has no free tier and starts at $15/month. For paid plans, Brevo begins at $25/month while ActiveCampaign starts at $15/month, though feature depth differs at each price point.

Can I switch from Brevo to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, switching is possible and both platforms support data migration. Most email marketing tools allow you to export your contacts and campaign history, which can then be imported into ActiveCampaign. However, you may need to manually recreate automation workflows, so plan accordingly for a smooth transition.
