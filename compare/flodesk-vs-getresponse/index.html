GetResponse vs Flodesk: Complete Comparison

GetResponse and Flodesk are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and use cases. GetResponse is a comprehensive all-in-one marketing suite built for course creators and webinar hosts, while Flodesk specializes in visually stunning emails designed for creative professionals and visual brands. Choosing between them depends on your priorities: versatility and affordability versus design-focused simplicity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GetResponse better than Flodesk?

It depends on your needs. GetResponse is better for feature-rich marketing automation, webinars, and affordability. Flodesk wins if you prioritize stunning email design and flat pricing with unlimited subscribers. GetResponse has higher overall ratings (4.2 G2, 3.7 Trustpilot) for versatility, while Flodesk excels in design-focused metrics (4.8 G2, 4.7 Trustpilot).

Which is cheaper, GetResponse or Flodesk?

GetResponse is cheaper for most users, starting at $0 with a free tier for up to 500 subscribers. Flodesk's minimum is $38/month with no free option, but you get unlimited subscribers and flat-rate pricing at that price point. If you have a small list under 500, GetResponse's free tier is unbeatable; for larger lists, compare your actual usage.

Can I switch from Flodesk to GetResponse?

Yes, most email marketing platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and campaign history. Both GetResponse and Flodesk support standard data export formats. You'll need to recreate your email templates and workflows in GetResponse, but your contact data can typically be imported without issues. Check both platforms' migration guides for specific steps.
