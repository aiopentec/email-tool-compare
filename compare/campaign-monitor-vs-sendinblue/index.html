Campaign Monitor vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

Campaign Monitor and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are two established email marketing platforms with different strengths. Campaign Monitor excels at multi-brand management for agencies, while Sendinblue offers a broader all-in-one CRM suite with SMS and chat capabilities. Choosing between them depends on your budget, team structure, and whether you need additional channels beyond email.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Campaign Monitor better than Sendinblue?

Neither is universally better—it depends on your needs. Campaign Monitor excels for agencies managing multiple brands with its specialized multi-brand features. Sendinblue offers better overall value with its free tier, broader feature set (SMS, chat, CRM), and higher customer satisfaction ratings.

Which is cheaper, Campaign Monitor or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is significantly cheaper for startups and small businesses thanks to its free tier supporting up to 100,000 subscribers and 300 daily emails at no cost. Campaign Monitor starts at $11/month with no free option, but may offer better value for large-scale agency operations.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Campaign Monitor?

Yes, both platforms support contact list exports and data migration. Most modern email marketing tools allow you to export your subscriber lists, automation workflows, and campaign history, though you may need to manually recreate complex automations in Campaign Monitor's journey designer.
