Frequently Asked Questions

Is MailerLite better than AWeber?

MailerLite edges ahead for most users with a more modern interface, better free tier limits, lower paid pricing, and superior e-commerce features. However, AWeber may be preferable if you specifically need RSS-to-email or AMP email capabilities. Both have strong ratings, with MailerLite at 4.6/5 on G2 versus AWeber's 4.2/5.

Which is cheaper, MailerLite or AWeber?

MailerLite is cheaper overall, with paid plans starting at $9/month compared to AWeber's $15/month. MailerLite also offers a more generous free tier with 1,000 subscribers versus AWeber's 500. For budget-conscious businesses, MailerLite provides better value at lower price points.

Can I switch from AWeber to MailerLite?

Yes, both platforms support subscriber list imports, making migration straightforward. MailerLite provides import tools for CSV files and direct integrations with other platforms. Contact MailerLite support to ensure a smooth transition of your contacts and campaign history if needed.
