Frequently asked questions

Is Klaviyo better than Omnisend?

Klaviyo offers more advanced features and predictive analytics, making it better for sophisticated ecommerce operations. However, Omnisend provides better value for budget-conscious users and has superior customer satisfaction scores on Trustpilot. The best choice depends on whether you prioritize feature depth or cost-effectiveness.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or Omnisend?

Omnisend is significantly cheaper, starting at $16/month compared to Klaviyo's $45/month. Omnisend also offers a larger free tier contact limit (250 vs 500 is actually Omnisend's smaller tier, but lower paid entry price offers better value). For budget-conscious startups, Omnisend is the more affordable option.

Can I switch from Omnisend to Klaviyo?

Yes, you can migrate between platforms, though the process requires exporting your contact lists and recreating workflows. Both platforms support standard data export formats, but you'll need to manually rebuild your automation sequences and campaigns in the new platform. Plan for migration time during the transition.
