Which Should You Choose?

Choose Klaviyo if…

  • You need advanced predictive analytics and behavioral insights for sophisticated segmentation
  • You prioritize deep e-commerce integrations, particularly with Shopify or WooCommerce
  • You want a more established platform (founded 2012) with higher G2 ratings (4.6 vs 4.5)

Choose Omnisend if…

  • You have a tight budget and want to start paid plans at just $16/month
  • You need push notifications alongside email and SMS for true omnichannel marketing
  • You prefer a platform with stronger Trustpilot ratings (4.6 vs 3.8) and an affiliate program

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Klaviyo better than Omnisend?

Klaviyo offers more advanced features like predictive analytics and deeper e-commerce integrations, making it better for sophisticated marketing automation. However, Omnisend excels in ease of use, affordability, and customer satisfaction scores, making it superior for budget-conscious businesses or beginners.

Which is cheaper, Klaviyo or Omnisend?

Omnisend is significantly cheaper, starting at $16/month compared to Klaviyo's $45/month for paid plans. Both offer free tiers, though Klaviyo provides more free contacts (500 vs 250), which may offset the lower entry price for very small businesses.

Can I switch from Omnisend to Klaviyo?

Yes, you can migrate your contact lists and data between platforms, though the process requires manual export and import or API integration. Both platforms support standard data formats, making the transition feasible, though you may lose some workflow history and customizations during the switch.
