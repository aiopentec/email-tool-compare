Mailchimp vs Klaviyo: Detailed Comparison for 2025

Mailchimp and Klaviyo are both popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences and use cases. Mailchimp excels for beginners and small businesses seeking an affordable, all-in-one solution, while Klaviyo is purpose-built for e-commerce brands wanting advanced automation and customer intelligence. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right tool for your marketing needs.

Which Should You Choose?

Choose Klaviyo if...

  • You're a beginner or startup with a tight budget and need affordable email marketing
  • You want to build landing pages and run A/B tests without additional tools
  • You value simplicity and an intuitive drag-and-drop editor over advanced features

Choose Mailchimp if...

  • You run an e-commerce business on Shopify or WooCommerce and need specialized integrations
  • You want predictive analytics and advanced segmentation to maximize customer lifetime value
  • You need SMS marketing and customer review management alongside email campaigns

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mailchimp better than Klaviyo?

Neither is universally 'better'—it depends on your needs. Mailchimp wins for affordability and simplicity, making it ideal for beginners. Klaviyo excels for e-commerce brands needing advanced automation, predictive analytics, and integrated SMS marketing. Klaviyo also has significantly higher customer satisfaction ratings on Trustpilot (3.8 vs 1.5).

Which is cheaper, Mailchimp or Klaviyo?

Mailchimp is considerably cheaper at $13/month for paid plans versus Klaviyo's $45/month entry point. Both offer free tiers supporting 500 contacts. However, Klaviyo's pricing is designed for e-commerce businesses where higher costs are justified by revenue-driving features like predictive analytics and SMS.

Can I switch from Klaviyo to Mailchimp?

Yes, you can migrate your contact lists and campaign data from Klaviyo to Mailchimp, though the process requires manual export and import or using third-party migration tools. Keep in mind that some advanced Klaviyo features (like predictive analytics) don't have direct equivalents in Mailchimp, so you may lose functionality in the switch.
