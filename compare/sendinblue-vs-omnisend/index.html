Omnisend vs Sendinblue: Detailed Comparison

Both Omnisend and Sendinblue (now Brevo) are powerful email marketing platforms with free tiers and SMS capabilities, but they cater to different business needs. Omnisend specializes in e-commerce automation with push notifications and product pickers, while Sendinblue focuses on transactional email and broader CRM functionality. Understanding their differences will help you choose the right tool for your marketing strategy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Omnisend better than Sendinblue?

Neither is objectively better—it depends on your needs. Omnisend excels for e-commerce brands requiring SMS and push notifications together, while Sendinblue offers superior value with its massive free tier and integrated CRM features. Choose based on your specific use case and industry.

Which is cheaper, Omnisend or Sendinblue?

Sendinblue is cheaper overall. It offers 100,000 free contacts versus Omnisend's 250, making it better for startups. When comparing paid plans, Sendinblue starts at $25 versus Omnisend's $16, but Sendinblue's free tier often eliminates the need to pay immediately.

Can I switch from Sendinblue to Omnisend?

Yes, both platforms support standard email list imports and exports. Most marketing data can be migrated, though you may need to rebuild automations and workflows manually, as each platform has different automation builders and features.
