Frequently asked questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Campaign Monitor?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign excels in automation, CRM, and lead scoring, making it better for businesses needing sophisticated marketing workflows. Campaign Monitor is better suited for agencies managing multiple brands with a focus on design and ease of use. ActiveCampaign has higher G2 (4.5) and Trustpilot (4.1) ratings overall.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Campaign Monitor?

Campaign Monitor starts at $11/month compared to ActiveCampaign's $15/month, making it the more affordable initial option. However, pricing scales differently for each platform based on subscriber count, so the overall cost difference depends on your list size and feature needs.

Can I switch from Campaign Monitor to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, most email marketing platforms allow data migration between services. Both tools support importing contacts and subscriber lists, though you may need to manually recreate campaigns and automations. It's recommended to test ActiveCampaign's features with a small segment before fully migrating.
