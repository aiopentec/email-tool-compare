Frequently Asked Questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Campaign Monitor?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign excels in automation, CRM, and advanced features with higher customer satisfaction ratings (4.5 G2, 4.1 Trustpilot). Campaign Monitor is better if you're an agency needing multi-brand management and prefer a design-focused, user-friendly interface. Choose based on whether you need robust automation or elegant simplicity.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Campaign Monitor?

Campaign Monitor starts at $11/month, making it $4 cheaper than ActiveCampaign's $15/month entry point. However, pricing scales with subscriber count, so the final cost depends on your list size and feature requirements. Request quotes for your specific needs to compare total cost.

Can I switch from Campaign Monitor to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, most email platforms allow you to export subscriber lists and campaign data. Both ActiveCampaign and Campaign Monitor support standard export formats. Plan the migration carefully, test integrations, and consider notifying subscribers about the transition to maintain engagement during the switch.
