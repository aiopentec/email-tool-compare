GetResponse vs Moosend: Detailed Comparison

GetResponse and Moosend are both popular freemium email marketing platforms with strong automation capabilities. While GetResponse excels as a comprehensive all-in-one marketing platform with webinars and courses, Moosend focuses on delivering affordable, unlimited email sending for small businesses. Your choice depends on whether you need advanced features like webinars or prioritize budget-friendly unlimited emails.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GetResponse better than Moosend?

GetResponse is better overall due to its comprehensive feature set including webinars, landing pages, online courses, and conversion funnels, earning it a higher G2 rating (4.2 vs 4.7). However, Moosend has better customer satisfaction ratings on Trustpilot (4.4 vs 3.7) and excels for those seeking pure email marketing simplicity at the lowest cost.

Which is cheaper, GetResponse or Moosend?

Moosend is cheaper with paid plans starting at $9/month compared to GetResponse's $19/month. Additionally, Moosend offers unlimited emails on all paid plans, while GetResponse implements tiered email limits, making Moosend significantly more budget-friendly for high-volume senders.

Can I switch from Moosend to GetResponse?

Yes, both platforms typically support importing contact lists and email campaigns via CSV files or API integrations. You should export your subscriber data from Moosend and check GetResponse's import documentation to ensure a smooth migration, though some formatting adjustments may be needed.
