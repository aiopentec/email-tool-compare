Frequently Asked Questions

Is ActiveCampaign better than Omnisend?

It depends on your needs. ActiveCampaign is better for businesses requiring advanced CRM, complex automation, and lead scoring. Omnisend is better for e-commerce brands wanting simplicity and omnichannel features. Both have identical G2 ratings (4.5/5), so the choice depends on your specific use case.

Which is cheaper, ActiveCampaign or Omnisend?

Omnisend is cheaper to start because it offers a free tier for up to 250 subscribers, while ActiveCampaign starts at $15/month with no free option. For paid plans, both start around $15-16/month, but Omnisend's freemium model gives small businesses a zero-cost entry point.

Can I switch from Omnisend to ActiveCampaign?

Yes, switching is possible. Both platforms typically support data export and API integrations. However, you'll need to manually migrate automation workflows and test them in ActiveCampaign, as the platform structures differ. Contact support teams for migration assistance and timeline planning.
