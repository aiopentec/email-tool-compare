GetResponse vs Moosend: Detailed Comparison

GetResponse and Moosend are both popular freemium email marketing platforms, but they serve different business needs. GetResponse excels as an all-in-one marketing suite with webinars and online courses, while Moosend focuses on affordable email automation with unlimited sends. Choosing between them depends on your primary use case and budget.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GetResponse better than Moosend?

GetResponse is better if you need webinars, online courses, and advanced automation, making it ideal for course creators and digital marketers. Moosend is better for small businesses prioritizing affordability and simplicity. The choice depends on your specific needs rather than overall superiority.

Which is cheaper, GetResponse or Moosend?

Moosend is cheaper, starting at $9/month compared to GetResponse's $19/month. Moosend also offers a more generous free tier with 1,000 subscribers versus GetResponse's 500. However, GetResponse's additional features may provide better value if you need webinars and courses.

Can I switch from Moosend to GetResponse?

Yes, most email marketing platforms allow you to export your subscriber lists and migrate to another service. Both GetResponse and Moosend provide standard data export options. You may need to manually reconfigure automations and campaigns, but your core data can be transferred.
