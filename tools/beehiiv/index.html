Beehiiv Review: The Newsletter Platform Built for Growth

Freemium ✓ Free plan From $39/mo G2: 4.7★ 💰 50% first year

Beehiiv is a modern email marketing and newsletter platform designed specifically for publishers and content creators who want to build sustainable, monetizable audiences. Founded in 2021, it combines traditional email marketing tools with unique growth features like an integrated ad network and referral program. It's best suited for newsletter publishers and media operators looking to turn their subscriber base into revenue.

Our verdict: Beehiiv excels as a purpose-built newsletter platform with strong monetization options and excellent user experience, boasting a 4.7 G2 rating. However, it's primarily optimized for newsletter publishers rather than general email marketing, and its growth-focused features mean it may be overkill for simple email campaigns or lack advanced customization for enterprise-level needs.

Pros

  • Generous free tier supporting up to 2,500 subscribers with core features included
  • Built-in monetization options including ad network and referral program for revenue generation
  • User-friendly interface with modern design and intuitive navigation
  • Integrated web reader and strong analytics dashboard for audience insights
  • 50% first-year affiliate commission incentivizes referrals and partner promotion

Cons

  • Limited appeal for traditional email marketing use cases outside of newsletters
  • Higher starting price ($39/month) compared to some general email marketing competitors
  • Advanced customization and enterprise features may be limited compared to established platforms like Klaviyo or HubSpot

Pricing

Beehiiv offers a freemium model with a free tier supporting up to 2,500 subscribers, making it accessible for new publishers. Paid plans start at $39 USD per month for growing newsletters, with pricing scaling based on subscriber count and desired features.

Who is Beehiiv best for?

Beehiiv is best for independent newsletter creators, media operators, and content publishers who want to monetize their audience without relying on sponsorships alone. It's ideal for those building consumer-facing email products rather than B2B marketing departments or ecommerce brands using email for sales.

Frequently asked questions

Is Beehiiv free?

Yes, Beehiiv offers a free tier that supports up to 2,500 subscribers with access to core features like email sending, basic analytics, and segmentation. Paid plans begin at $39 per month for larger subscriber bases or users requiring advanced monetization features.

What is Beehiiv best used for?

Beehiiv is optimized for newsletter publishers and media operators who want to build engaged subscriber communities and generate revenue directly from their audience. It's particularly well-suited for independent creators, Substack alternatives users, and content-first publishers seeking monetization beyond sponsorships.

Is Beehiiv good for beginners?

Yes, Beehiiv is very beginner-friendly with an intuitive interface, helpful onboarding, and a free tier that lets new publishers start without financial investment. The platform's growth-focused features and built-in monetization guidance make it excellent for creators launching their first newsletter.

Does Beehiiv have an affiliate program?

Yes, Beehiiv offers an affiliate program with a 50% commission on first-year subscription revenue. This generous commission structure makes it attractive for creators and influencers looking to recommend the platform and earn recurring referral income.
