Beehiiv Review: The Newsletter Platform Built for Growth

Freemium ✓ Free plan From $39/mo G2: 4.7★ 💰 50% first year

Beehiiv is a modern email marketing and newsletter platform designed specifically for publishers and content creators looking to build engaged audiences. Founded in 2021, it combines essential email marketing tools with unique monetization features like ad networks and referral programs. The platform is best suited for independent newsletter publishers, media operators, and creators who want to grow their audience while generating revenue.

Our verdict: Beehiiv excels as a growth-focused newsletter platform with excellent monetization options and strong analytics, making it ideal for professional publishers. However, it's less suitable for traditional email marketing campaigns or small businesses prioritizing basic automation, and the free tier has limitations compared to some competitors. Overall, it's a solid choice for newsletter creators willing to leverage its growth and revenue features.

Pros

  • Strong monetization features including built-in ad network and referral program
  • Generous free tier supporting up to 2,500 subscribers with core features
  • Excellent analytics and segmentation tools tailored for newsletter growth
  • User-friendly interface designed specifically for creators and publishers

Cons

  • Limited traditional marketing automation features compared to general email platforms
  • Pricing starts at $39/month, which may be steep for hobby newsletters
  • Smaller feature set than established competitors like ConvertKit or Substack in some areas

Pricing

Beehiiv offers a free plan supporting up to 2,500 subscribers with basic features, followed by paid plans starting at $39/month. The pricing structure is transparent and designed to scale with newsletter growth, with additional revenue-sharing opportunities through their ad network.

Who is Beehiiv best for?

Beehiiv is best for professional newsletter publishers and media operators who prioritize audience growth and monetization over traditional email marketing. Independent creators, Substack competitors, and publishers seeking ad revenue and referral incentives will find the platform most valuable.

Frequently asked questions

Is Beehiiv free?

Yes, Beehiiv offers a free tier that supports up to 2,500 subscribers and includes core features like email sending, basic analytics, and segmentation. However, advanced features and higher subscriber limits require upgrading to a paid plan starting at $39/month.

What is Beehiiv best used for?

Beehiiv is purpose-built for newsletter creators and publishers who want to grow their audience and monetize their content. Its unique combination of email marketing tools, ad networks, and analytics make it ideal for independent writers, media operators, and content creators seeking revenue beyond subscriptions.

Is Beehiiv good for beginners?

Yes, Beehiiv is beginner-friendly with an intuitive interface designed for creators without technical expertise. The free tier lets new publishers test the platform risk-free, though beginners should note that maximizing monetization features requires some learning and audience size.

Does Beehiiv have an affiliate program?

Yes, Beehiiv offers an affiliate program with a 50% commission on the first year of referred customers' subscriptions. This makes it an attractive option for newsletter creators looking to earn additional revenue by recommending the platform to their audience.
