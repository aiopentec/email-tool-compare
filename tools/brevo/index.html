Brevo is all-in-one crm suite that positions itself for transactional email and multi-channel marketing. Founded in 2012, it has become one of the most recognised platforms in the email marketing space. In this review we cover what it does well, where it falls short, and who should actually use it.

Our verdict: Brevo is a solid choice for transactional email and multi-channel marketing. Its free tier is genuinely useful. The main trade-off is between its particular strengths and the limitations that make alternatives worth considering for some users.

Frequently asked questions

Is Brevo free?

Yes, Brevo has a free plan supporting up to 100000 subscribers. This makes it accessible for new senders testing the platform.

What is Brevo best used for?

Brevo is best for transactional email and multi-channel marketing. Its standout features include email campaigns, SMS, WhatsApp, which suit this use case well.

Is Brevo good for beginners?

Brevo is generally beginner-friendly. The interface is straightforward for basic campaigns, though advanced automation features have a learning curve.

Does Brevo have an affiliate program?

Yes, Brevo runs an affiliate program paying 5 EUR per free signup + 100 EUR per paid. This is worth considering if you plan to recommend the platform to your audience.
