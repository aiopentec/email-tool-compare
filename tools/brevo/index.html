Brevo Review: All-in-One CRM & Email Marketing Platform

Freemium Free plan available From $25/mo G2: 4.5★

Brevo is a comprehensive CRM and marketing automation suite that combines email campaigns, SMS, WhatsApp messaging, live chat, and transactional email capabilities in one platform. Founded in 2012, it serves businesses of all sizes looking to manage customer communications across multiple channels. It's particularly well-suited for companies that need integrated email and transactional email functionality without breaking the bank.

Our verdict: Brevo excels as an affordable, multi-channel marketing platform with a generous free tier that accommodates up to 100,000 contacts—a significant advantage for growing businesses. However, while it offers breadth across channels, some users report the depth of features and automation capabilities lag behind specialized competitors like Klaviyo or HubSpot, and the interface can feel cluttered for beginners.

Pros

  • Exceptionally generous free tier supporting 100,000 contacts with no feature limitations
  • True multi-channel platform integrating email, SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat in one suite
  • Strong transactional email API with reliable delivery rates (4.5/5 G2 rating)
  • Competitive pricing starting at just $25/month for paid plans with no setup fees

Cons

  • User interface is less intuitive than competitors, requiring a steeper learning curve
  • Automation and segmentation features are less advanced compared to higher-tier platforms
  • Customer support response times can be slow, particularly on lower-tier plans

Pricing

Brevo operates on a freemium model with a robust free plan supporting 100,000 subscribers and unlimited emails, making it ideal for startups. Paid plans begin at $25/month and scale based on contact volume and feature needs, with transparent pricing and no hidden fees.

Who is Brevo best for?

Brevo is best suited for e-commerce businesses and SaaS companies that need reliable transactional email combined with multi-channel marketing capabilities. It's particularly valuable for budget-conscious teams that want to avoid paying premium prices while still accessing SMS, WhatsApp, and CRM functionality.

Frequently asked questions

Is Brevo free?

Yes, Brevo offers a free plan that supports up to 100,000 contacts with unlimited email sends—one of the most generous free tiers available. The free plan includes most core features like email campaigns, basic automation, and CRM functionality, though some advanced features require a paid subscription.

What is Brevo best used for?

Brevo excels at transactional email delivery and multi-channel marketing automation, making it ideal for e-commerce, SaaS, and service-based businesses. Its integrated SMS, WhatsApp, and live chat features make it particularly valuable for companies wanting to manage customer communication across all touchpoints from one platform.

Is Brevo good for beginners?

Brevo is moderately beginner-friendly thanks to its free tier and straightforward email campaign builder, though the interface can feel overwhelming initially. The platform would benefit beginners even more with improved UI/UX, but their documentation and community support help offset this learning curve.

Does Brevo have an affiliate program?

Yes, Brevo offers an affiliate program providing €5 per free signup and €100 per paid subscription conversion. This makes it an attractive opportunity for marketers and agencies to generate passive revenue while recommending a cost-effective tool.
