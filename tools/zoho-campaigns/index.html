Zoho Campaigns is email marketing built into the zoho ecosystem that positions itself for Zoho users and budget-conscious businesses. Founded in 2008, it has become one of the most recognised platforms in the email marketing space. In this review we cover what it does well, where it falls short, and who should actually use it.

Our verdict: Zoho Campaigns is a solid choice for Zoho users and budget-conscious businesses. Its free tier is genuinely useful. The main trade-off is between its particular strengths and the limitations that make alternatives worth considering for some users.

Pros

Cons
