Ghost Review 2025: Everything You Need to Know

Flat Rate From $9/mo G2: 4.1★

Ghost is independent publishing for newsletters and blogs that positions itself for writers and creators running paid newsletter businesses. Founded in 2013, it has become one of the most recognised platforms in the email marketing space. In this review we cover what it does well, where it falls short, and who should actually use it.

Our verdict: Ghost is a solid choice for writers and creators running paid newsletter businesses. Its paid-only pricing reflects its focus on serious senders. The main trade-off is between its particular strengths and the limitations that make alternatives worth considering for some users.

Pros

  • Good fit for writers and creators running paid newsletter businesses
  • Competitive pricing starting at $9/mo
  • Key feature: Newsletter
  • Mature, well-supported platform

Cons

  • Can get expensive at higher subscriber counts
  • Learning curve for advanced automations
  • Some features locked to higher tiers

Pricing

Ghost starts at $0/month, with paid plans from $9/month. Pricing typically scales with subscriber count.
