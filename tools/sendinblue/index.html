Sendinblue (Brevo) Review: All-in-One Email Marketing & CRM Platform

Freemium Free plan available From $25/mo G2: 4.5★

Sendinblue, now operating under the brand Brevo, is a comprehensive marketing automation platform that combines email marketing, SMS, WhatsApp messaging, and CRM tools in a single suite. It's particularly well-suited for businesses looking to manage transactional emails and multi-channel campaigns without breaking the bank. The platform offers a generous free tier, making it an accessible choice for startups and small businesses.

Our verdict: Sendinblue delivers excellent value as an all-in-one platform, especially for users who need transactional email and SMS capabilities alongside traditional email marketing. The free tier is genuinely useful with 300 daily emails and support for up to 100,000 contacts, though interface complexity and limited automation features on lower tiers may frustrate advanced users. It's a solid choice for cost-conscious businesses, but those requiring sophisticated marketing automation may want to explore dedicated alternatives.

Pros

  • Generous free tier with 300 emails/day and 100,000 contact limit
  • Multi-channel support including email, SMS, WhatsApp, and chat
  • Robust transactional email API with reliable delivery
  • Integrated CRM eliminates the need for separate contact management tools
  • Affordable paid plans starting at just $25/month
  • Strong ratings on G2 (4.5) and Trustpilot (4.3)

Cons

  • User interface can be confusing and overwhelming for beginners despite the feature set
  • Automation capabilities are limited on lower-tier plans, requiring upgrades for advanced workflows
  • Template library and design tools lack sophistication compared to dedicated email design platforms
  • Customer support response times can be slow, particularly for lower-tier subscribers

Pricing

Sendinblue operates on a freemium model with a robust free tier offering 300 daily emails and support for up to 100,000 contacts. Paid plans start at $25/month and scale based on email volume and additional features needed.

Who is Sendinblue best for?

Sendinblue excels for businesses that need transactional email capabilities combined with marketing automation, SMS campaigns, or CRM functionality. It's ideal for ecommerce stores, SaaS companies, and startups that want an integrated platform without the premium pricing of enterprise solutions.

Frequently asked questions

Is Sendinblue free?

Yes, Sendinblue offers a legitimate free tier that allows you to send up to 300 emails per day and manage up to 100,000 contacts at no cost. The free plan includes access to most core features like basic automation, CRM, and chat, though some advanced features require a paid upgrade. This makes it one of the most generous free email marketing offerings available.

What is Sendinblue best used for?

Sendinblue is particularly strong for transactional email (order confirmations, password resets, notifications) and multi-channel campaigns combining email, SMS, and WhatsApp messaging. It's also excellent for small to medium-sized businesses seeking an integrated CRM and marketing automation platform without enterprise-level complexity or pricing. The platform works well for ecommerce, SaaS, and service-based businesses.

Is Sendinblue good for beginners?

Sendinblue is moderately beginner-friendly thanks to its free tier and drag-and-drop email editor, though the overall interface can feel cluttered and overwhelming initially. The platform includes helpful templates and documentation, but beginners may find the feature density slightly intimidating compared to simpler alternatives like Mailchimp. However, the generous free plan makes it worth trying before committing to paid options.

Does Sendinblue have an affiliate program?

Yes, Sendinblue offers an active affiliate program that provides 5 EUR credit for free tier sign-ups and 100 EUR credit for paid plan conversions. This makes it attractive for content creators and agencies looking to earn referral commissions while promoting a legitimate, well-reviewed platform. The program terms are straightforward and competitive within the email marketing space.
