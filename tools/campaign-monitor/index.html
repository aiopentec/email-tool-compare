Campaign Monitor Review: Email Marketing for Agencies & Teams

Paid From $11/mo G2: 4.1★ 💰 varies

Campaign Monitor is an email marketing platform founded in 2004 that specializes in helping agencies and teams manage multiple brands from a single dashboard. Known for its beautiful template builder and journey designer, it offers a comprehensive suite of marketing automation tools without requiring a free tier commitment. The platform is best suited for businesses that need multi-brand management capabilities and want to create visually stunning campaigns quickly.

Our verdict: Campaign Monitor is a solid choice for agencies managing multiple client accounts, with strong design tools and multi-brand features that justify its pricing starting at $11/month. However, the lower Trustpilot rating (3.2) compared to its G2 score (4.1) suggests some users experience support or implementation challenges, which potential customers should consider before committing.

Pros

  • Multi-brand management allows agencies to manage numerous client accounts from one dashboard
  • Beautiful, intuitive template builder with drag-and-drop design capabilities
  • Journey designer enables sophisticated automation workflows for customer engagement
  • Smart segments functionality provides advanced audience targeting and personalization

Cons

  • No free tier available—all users must pay to get started, limiting trial options
  • Trustpilot rating of 3.2 is notably lower than G2 rating, suggesting inconsistent user satisfaction
  • Starting price of $11/month is only for smallest subscriber counts; costs scale significantly with list growth

Pricing

Campaign Monitor uses a tiered pricing model starting at $11 USD per month for smaller lists, with costs increasing based on subscriber count. The exact pricing structure scales with your audience size, making it more affordable for new users but potentially expensive as your email list grows.

Who is Campaign Monitor best for?

Campaign Monitor is purpose-built for digital agencies and marketing teams managing campaigns for multiple clients or brands. If you need to keep client accounts separate while maintaining centralized oversight, this platform's multi-brand management capabilities make it the ideal choice.

Frequently asked questions

Is Campaign Monitor free?

No, Campaign Monitor does not offer a free tier. All users must subscribe to a paid plan starting at $11 USD per month. While there's no free option to test the platform, you can contact their sales team to discuss trial access or specific enterprise arrangements.

What is Campaign Monitor best used for?

Campaign Monitor excels at email marketing for agencies and teams managing multiple brands. The platform's multi-brand management, journey designer, and smart segments make it particularly valuable for creating automated customer journeys and personalized campaigns across numerous client accounts simultaneously.

Is Campaign Monitor good for beginners?

Campaign Monitor's intuitive interface and template builder make it relatively beginner-friendly, though the lack of a free tier means you'll need to commit financially from the start. Beginners with budget should consider the learning curve, but experienced marketers will likely appreciate the advanced features like journey designer and smart segments.

Does Campaign Monitor have an affiliate program?

Yes, Campaign Monitor offers an affiliate program with commission rates that vary. Interested partners should contact the company directly for specific commission details and program terms applicable to their situation.
