Frequently asked questions

Campaign Monitor does not offer a free plan. Paid plans start at $11/month. This makes it more suited to established senders with a budget.

Campaign Monitor is best for agencies and teams managing multiple brands. Its standout features include template builder, journey designer, smart segments, which suit this use case well.

Is Campaign Monitor good for beginners?

Campaign Monitor is aimed at users with some email marketing experience. The interface is straightforward for basic campaigns, though advanced automation features have a learning curve.
