Campaign Monitor Review: Email Marketing for Agencies & Teams

Paid From $11/mo G2: 4.1★

Campaign Monitor is an email marketing platform founded in 2004 that specializes in helping agencies and teams manage multiple brands from a single dashboard. Known for its beautiful email templates and robust multi-brand capabilities, it offers journey automation, smart segmentation, and comprehensive analytics. The platform is best suited for marketing agencies, teams, and businesses that need to manage campaigns across multiple client accounts or brand properties.

Our verdict: Campaign Monitor is a solid choice for agencies and larger teams seeking multi-brand management capabilities, with strong template design tools and automation features backed by a 4.1 G2 rating. However, the lack of a free tier, mixed customer satisfaction scores (3.2 on Trustpilot), and starting price of $11/month may give smaller businesses pause about overall value relative to competitors.

Pros

  • Excellent multi-brand management allows agencies to handle multiple clients from one platform
  • Intuitive drag-and-drop template builder creates visually appealing emails without coding
  • Journey designer and smart segments enable sophisticated marketing automation workflows
  • Transactional email capabilities support both marketing and operational communications

Cons

  • No free tier available, requiring paid commitment from the start with minimum $11/month investment
  • Lower Trustpilot rating (3.2) suggests some customer service or satisfaction concerns
  • Limited information on integration ecosystem compared to competitors in the space

Pricing

Campaign Monitor starts at $11/month and uses a straightforward paid model with no free tier option. Pricing scales based on subscriber count and features, making it accessible to small businesses while remaining practical for enterprise teams.

Who is Campaign Monitor best for?

Campaign Monitor is purpose-built for marketing agencies and teams managing multiple client accounts or brand properties simultaneously. The multi-brand management features and consolidated dashboard make it particularly valuable for agencies looking to streamline operations and reduce overhead from managing separate platforms.

Key features

  • Template Builder
  • Journey Designer
  • Smart Segments
  • Multi-Brand Management
  • Analytics
  • Transactional Email

Frequently asked questions

Is Campaign Monitor free?

No, Campaign Monitor does not offer a free tier. The platform requires a paid subscription starting at $11/month, which means there's no way to test the full feature set without committing financially. This differentiates it from competitors that offer limited free plans, though the starting price is relatively affordable for the capabilities offered.

What is Campaign Monitor best used for?

Campaign Monitor excels at helping agencies and marketing teams manage email campaigns across multiple brands or clients. Its multi-brand management system, journey designer, and smart segmentation features are specifically designed for complex marketing environments where managing separate platforms would be inefficient.

Is Campaign Monitor good for beginners?

Campaign Monitor can work for beginners thanks to its intuitive drag-and-drop template builder and visual journey designer. However, the lack of a free tier means beginners must commit financially immediately, and the Trustpilot rating suggests some users experience support or usability challenges that might frustrate those new to email marketing.

Does Campaign Monitor have an affiliate program?

Yes, Campaign Monitor offers an affiliate program with commission rates that vary based on partnership terms. Interested affiliates should contact the company directly for specific commission structures and program details, as rates are not publicly standardized.
