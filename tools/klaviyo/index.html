Klaviyo Review 2025: Everything You Need to Know

Freemium ✓ Free plan From $45/mo G2: 4.6★

Klaviyo is the platform for intelligent marketing automation that positions itself for e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce. Founded in 2012, it has become one of the most recognised platforms in the email marketing space. In this review we cover what it does well, where it falls short, and who should actually use it.

Our verdict: Klaviyo is a solid choice for e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce. Its free tier is genuinely useful. The main trade-off is between its particular strengths and the limitations that make alternatives worth considering for some users.

Frequently asked questions

Is Klaviyo free?

Yes, Klaviyo has a free plan supporting up to 500 subscribers. This makes it accessible for new senders testing the platform.

What is Klaviyo best used for?

Klaviyo is best for e-commerce brands on Shopify or WooCommerce. Its standout features include e-commerce integrations, predictive analytics, segmentation, which suit this use case well.

Is Klaviyo good for beginners?

Klaviyo is generally beginner-friendly. The interface is straightforward for basic campaigns, though advanced automation features have a learning curve.

Does Klaviyo have an affiliate program?

Klaviyo does not currently offer an affiliate program. This is worth considering if you plan to recommend the platform to your audience.
