ActiveCampaign Review 2025: Everything You Need to Know

Paid From $15/mo G2: 4.5★

ActiveCampaign is email, automation, and crm combined that positions itself for businesses needing advanced automation and CRM. Founded in 2003, it has become one of the most recognised platforms in the email marketing space. In this review we cover what it does well, where it falls short, and who should actually use it.

Our verdict: ActiveCampaign is a solid choice for businesses needing advanced automation and CRM. Its paid-only pricing reflects its focus on serious senders. The main trade-off is between its particular strengths and the limitations that make alternatives worth considering for some users.

Frequently asked questions

Is ActiveCampaign free?

ActiveCampaign does not offer a free plan. Paid plans start at $15/month. This makes it more suited to established senders with a budget.

What is ActiveCampaign best used for?

ActiveCampaign is best for businesses needing advanced automation and CRM. Its standout features include advanced automation, CRM, lead scoring, which suit this use case well.

Is ActiveCampaign good for beginners?

ActiveCampaign is aimed at users with some email marketing experience. The interface is straightforward for basic campaigns, though advanced automation features have a learning curve.

Does ActiveCampaign have an affiliate program?

Yes, ActiveCampaign runs an affiliate program paying 20-30% recurring. This is worth considering if you plan to recommend the platform to your audience.
