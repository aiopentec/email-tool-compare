Drip is ecommerce email marketing made easy that positions itself for e-commerce businesses wanting deep store integration. Founded in 2013, it has become one of the most recognised platforms in the email marketing space. In this review we cover what it does well, where it falls short, and who should actually use it.

Frequently asked questions

Is Drip free?

Drip does not offer a free plan. Paid plans start at $39/month. This makes it more suited to established senders with a budget.

What is Drip best used for?

Drip is best for e-commerce businesses wanting deep store integration. Its standout features include e-commerce tracking, revenue attribution, visual automation builder, which suit this use case well.

Is Drip good for beginners?

Drip is aimed at users with some email marketing experience. The interface is straightforward for basic campaigns, though advanced automation features have a learning curve.

Does Drip have an affiliate program?

Yes, Drip runs an affiliate program paying 15% recurring. This is worth considering if you plan to recommend the platform to your audience.
