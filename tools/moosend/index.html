Moosend Email Marketing Review: Unlimited Emails at Unbeatable Value

Freemium ✓ Free plan From $9/mo G2: 4.7★ 💰 30% recurring

Moosend is a cloud-based email marketing platform founded in 2011 that combines essential marketing tools with an aggressive pricing strategy. It's particularly well-suited for small businesses and startups that need unlimited email sending without breaking the bank, offering automation workflows, landing pages, and analytics all in one platform.

Our verdict: Moosend delivers exceptional value for budget-conscious marketers, especially with its unlimited email sending on paid plans and solid feature set across automation and landing pages. However, it faces stiff competition from more established players, and some users report that advanced customization options lag behind premium competitors—making it ideal for straightforward campaigns rather than complex enterprise workflows.

Pros

  • Unlimited emails on all paid plans starting at just $9/month
  • Free tier supports up to 1,000 subscribers with no sending limits
  • Comprehensive feature set including automation, A/B testing, and landing pages
  • Strong ratings (4.7 on G2, 4.4 on Trustpilot) indicate solid user satisfaction
  • 30% recurring affiliate commission attracts reseller interest

Cons

  • Advanced segmentation and personalization features trail industry leaders like HubSpot and Klaviyo
  • Template design customization can feel limited compared to premium competitors
  • Free tier limited to 1,000 subscribers, which restricts growing businesses

Pricing

Moosend operates on a freemium model starting at $0 for up to 1,000 subscribers with no email limits, with paid plans beginning at $9/month. All paid tiers offer unlimited email sending, making it one of the most cost-effective options for volume-heavy campaigns.

Who is Moosend best for?

Moosend shines for small businesses and solopreneurs who send high email volumes but have limited marketing budgets. It's also ideal for agencies and resellers leveraging the 30% recurring affiliate commission to build additional revenue streams.

Frequently asked questions

Is Moosend free?

Yes, Moosend offers a free tier supporting up to 1,000 subscribers with unlimited email sending. The free plan includes core features like automation workflows, basic analytics, and subscription forms, making it suitable for testing the platform before upgrading to a paid plan.

What is Moosend best used for?

Moosend excels at email marketing automation and campaigns for small-to-medium businesses prioritizing volume over complexity. It's particularly valuable for businesses needing unlimited emails, landing pages, and A/B testing without expensive enterprise pricing.

Is Moosend good for beginners?

Yes, Moosend is beginner-friendly with an intuitive interface, pre-built automation templates, and responsive customer support. New users can quickly set up campaigns and workflows, though some may need to explore documentation for advanced personalization features.

Does Moosend have an affiliate program?

Yes, Moosend offers a 30% recurring commission affiliate program, making it attractive for agencies and marketers looking to monetize referrals. Partners earn commissions on every referral's subscription for the lifetime of the customer relationship.
