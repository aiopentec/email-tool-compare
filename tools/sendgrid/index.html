SendGrid is email delivery you can trust at any scale that positions itself for developers and high-volume senders. Founded in 2009, it has become one of the most recognised platforms in the email marketing space. In this review we cover what it does well, where it falls short, and who should actually use it.

Our verdict: SendGrid is a solid choice for developers and high-volume senders. Its free tier is genuinely useful. The main trade-off is between its particular strengths and the limitations that make alternatives worth considering for some users.
