Mailchimp Review 2025: Everything You Need to Know

Freemium Free plan available From $13/mo G2: 4.3★

Mailchimp is the all-in-one marketing platform that positions itself for beginners and small businesses. Founded in 2001, it has become one of the most recognised platforms in the email marketing space. In this review we cover what it does well, where it falls short, and who should actually use it.

Our verdict: Mailchimp is a solid choice for beginners and small businesses. Its free tier is genuinely useful. The main trade-off is between its particular strengths and the limitations that make alternatives worth considering for some users.

Pros

  • Competitive pricing starting at $13/mo

  • Free tier available

Cons

  • Some features locked to higher tiers

Pricing

Mailchimp offers a free plan, with paid plans from $13/month. Pricing typically scales with subscriber count.

Frequently asked questions

Is Mailchimp free?

Yes, Mailchimp has a free plan supporting up to 500 subscribers. This makes it accessible for new senders testing the platform.

What is Mailchimp best used for?

Mailchimp is best for beginners and small businesses. Its standout features include drag-drop editor, automation, landing pages, which suit this use case well.

Is Mailchimp good for beginners?

Mailchimp is generally beginner-friendly. The interface is straightforward for basic campaigns, though advanced automation features have a learning curve.

Does Mailchimp have an affiliate program?

Yes, Mailchimp runs an affiliate program paying 30% recurring. This is worth considering if you plan to recommend the platform to your audience.
