Loops Review 2025: Everything You Need to Know

Freemium ✓ Free plan From $49/mo G2: 4.7★

Loops is email for modern saas companies that positions itself for SaaS companies sending product and lifecycle emails. Founded in 2022, it has become one of the most recognised platforms in the email marketing space. In this review we cover what it does well, where it falls short, and who should actually use it.

Our verdict: Loops is a solid choice for SaaS companies sending product and lifecycle emails. Its free tier is genuinely useful. The main trade-off is between its particular strengths and the limitations that make alternatives worth considering for some users.

Pros
