GetResponse Review: Complete Email Marketing & Webinar Platform

Freemium ✓ Free plan From $19/mo G2: 4.2★ 💰 33% recurring or $100 one-time

GetResponse is a comprehensive online marketing platform that combines email marketing, webinars, landing pages, and online course hosting in one integrated tool. Founded in 1998, it's particularly well-suited for online course creators, webinar hosts, and small to mid-sized businesses looking for an all-in-one solution. The platform offers both free and paid tiers, making it accessible to entrepreneurs at various stages of growth.

Our verdict: GetResponse delivers solid value as an all-in-one marketing platform with strong webinar and course creation capabilities, backed by reliable automation tools. However, it falls slightly behind specialized competitors in pure email marketing functionality, and its interface can feel cluttered for beginners. It's an excellent choice for course creators and webinar hosts who need integrated tools, but email-focused marketers might find better options.

Pros

  • All-in-one platform combining email marketing, webinars, landing pages, and online courses reduces tool switching
  • Generous free tier with 500 subscriber limit and core features makes it accessible for startups
  • Excellent webinar capabilities with native hosting and conversion tracking built-in
  • Strong automation workflows with visual builder and conditional logic for complex campaigns
  • Lucrative affiliate program offering 33% recurring commissions for resellers

Cons

  • Email marketing features less advanced than specialized platforms like Klaviyo or ConvertKit
  • Learning curve for beginners due to interface density and numerous feature options
  • Customer support response times can be slow during peak hours

Pricing

GetResponse offers a freemium model starting at $0 for up to 500 subscribers with core features, while paid plans begin at $19/month for additional contacts and advanced capabilities. The pricing scales based on subscriber count, making it cost-effective for small lists but potentially expensive as your audience grows.

Who is GetResponse best for?

GetResponse is ideal for online course creators and webinar hosts who need integrated tools for launching and marketing educational content. These users benefit most from the native webinar hosting, course platform, and automated funnel builders that are specifically designed for education and lead generation workflows.

Frequently asked questions

Is GetResponse free?

Yes, GetResponse offers a free tier that supports up to 500 subscribers with access to email marketing, basic automation, landing pages, and webinars. The free plan has limitations on advanced features like conditional automation and detailed reporting, but it's a solid option for testing the platform before upgrading.

What is GetResponse best used for?

GetResponse excels for online course creators, webinar hosts, and service-based entrepreneurs who need an integrated platform combining email marketing, live webinars, automated funnels, and course delivery. Its strength lies in coordinating these functions seamlessly rather than excelling at any single feature.

Is GetResponse good for beginners?

GetResponse can work for beginners thanks to its free tier and template library, but the platform's breadth of features creates a steeper learning curve compared to simpler tools. Beginners focused solely on email marketing might find more straightforward options, but those wanting an all-in-one solution will find helpful tutorials and support resources.

Does GetResponse have an affiliate program?

Yes, GetResponse offers a generous affiliate program providing either 33% recurring commissions or a $100 one-time payment per referral. This makes it attractive for content creators and marketers looking to generate passive income by recommending the platform.
