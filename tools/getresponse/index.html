GetResponse Review: Complete Email Marketing & Webinar Platform

Freemium Free plan available From $19/mo G2: 4.2★

GetResponse is a comprehensive online marketing platform founded in 1998 that combines email marketing, webinars, landing pages, and automation tools in a single dashboard. It's particularly well-suited for online course creators and webinar hosts who need an integrated solution rather than juggling multiple tools. The platform offers a freemium model with impressive functionality at entry-level pricing.

Our verdict: GetResponse is a solid all-in-one marketing platform that delivers genuine value, especially for creators and entrepreneurs building funnels and hosting webinars. Its main strength lies in integrated features and reasonable pricing, though user reviews suggest the interface could be more intuitive and customer support responsiveness varies. The platform strikes a good balance between functionality and ease of use, making it competitive for mid-market users who want to avoid tool sprawl.

Pros

  • Integrated webinar hosting, email marketing, landing pages, and courses in one platform
  • Strong freemium tier with up to 500 subscribers at no cost
  • Conversion funnel builder and automation workflows suitable for beginners
  • Generous affiliate program offering 33% recurring commission or $100 one-time payments

Cons

  • User interface has a steeper learning curve compared to specialized email-only competitors
  • Trustpilot rating of 3.7 suggests mixed customer satisfaction and inconsistent support experiences
  • Limited advanced customization options in templates compared to enterprise-level platforms

Pricing

GetResponse operates on a freemium model with a free tier supporting up to 500 subscribers, while paid plans start at $19/month for additional features and larger lists. Pricing scales with subscriber count, making it affordable for growing businesses but potentially costly at very large list sizes.

Who is GetResponse best for?

GetResponse is ideal for online course creators and webinar hosts who need more than email marketing alone and benefit from having landing pages, automation, and course hosting integrated. Entrepreneurs building marketing funnels and small business owners who want to consolidate tools will find the platform particularly valuable.

Frequently asked questions

Is GetResponse free?

Yes, GetResponse offers a free tier that supports up to 500 subscribers with core email marketing features included. Upgrading to paid plans unlocks advanced features like webinars, landing pages, and automation, starting at $19/month.

What is GetResponse best used for?

GetResponse excels as an all-in-one platform for online course creators, webinar hosts, and funnel builders who want email marketing, automation, and landing pages in one place. It's particularly strong for entrepreneurs building complete marketing ecosystems rather than using point solutions.

Is GetResponse good for beginners?

GetResponse is moderately beginner-friendly with a free tier and helpful onboarding, though the interface is more complex than specialized email tools like Mailchimp. Beginners will appreciate the integrated features and automation templates, but may need some time to learn the full platform.

Does GetResponse have an affiliate program?

Yes, GetResponse offers an affiliate program with competitive commissions of 33% recurring revenue or a $100 one-time payment per referral. This makes it attractive for content creators and marketers looking to generate passive income through product recommendations.
