Customer.io Review 2025: Everything You Need to Know

Paid From $100/mo G2: 4.4★

Customer.io is behavioral email and messaging for data-driven teams that positions itself for product and growth teams sending behaviour-triggered messages. Founded in 2012, it has become one of the most recognised platforms in the email marketing space. In this review we cover what it does well, where it falls short, and who should actually use it.

Our verdict: Customer.io is a solid choice for product and growth teams sending behaviour-triggered messages. Its paid-only pricing reflects its focus on serious senders. The main trade-off is between its particular strengths and the limitations that make alternatives worth considering for some users.
