Constant Contact Review 2025: Everything You Need to Know

Paid From $12/mo G2: 4.0★

Constant Contact is digital marketing made simple that positions itself for nonprofits and local businesses. Founded in 1995, it has become one of the most recognised platforms in the email marketing space. In this review we cover what it does well, where it falls short, and who should actually use it.

Our verdict: Constant Contact is a solid choice for nonprofits and local businesses. Its paid-only pricing reflects its focus on serious senders. The main trade-off is between its particular strengths and the limitations that make alternatives worth considering for some users.

Frequently asked questions

Is Constant Contact free?

Constant Contact does not offer a free plan. Paid plans start at $12/month. This makes it more suited to established senders with a budget.

What is Constant Contact best used for?

Constant Contact is best for nonprofits and local businesses. Its standout features include event management, social media ads, SMS, which suit this use case well.

Is Constant Contact good for beginners?

Constant Contact is aimed at users with some email marketing experience. The interface is straightforward for basic campaigns, though advanced automation features have a learning curve.

Does Constant Contact have an affiliate program?

Yes, Constant Contact runs an affiliate program paying $80 per paid signup. This is worth considering if you plan to recommend the platform to your audience.
