Constant Contact Email Marketing Review: Complete Feature & Pricing Breakdown

Constant Contact is a well-established email marketing platform founded in 1995, offering a comprehensive suite of tools including email campaigns, SMS marketing, social media ads, and event management. The platform is specifically designed for nonprofits and local businesses looking for an all-in-one digital marketing solution. With plans starting at $12/month, it provides accessible entry-level pricing for small organizations.

Our verdict: Constant Contact is a capable all-in-one marketing platform with solid features for nonprofits and small businesses, but the gap between its 4.0 G2 rating and 2.8 Trustpilot score suggests customer satisfaction varies significantly. Strengths include comprehensive event management, social media integration, and beginner-friendly templates, while weaknesses center on customer support responsiveness and pricing transparency as you scale.

Pros

  • Integrated event management tools ideal for nonprofits hosting fundraisers or community events
  • All-in-one platform combining email, SMS, social media ads, and e-commerce in one dashboard
  • Extensive library of professionally-designed email templates suitable for beginners
  • Affordable starting price of $12/month makes it accessible for budget-conscious organizations

Cons

  • Lower Trustpilot rating (2.8) indicates potential customer service or product satisfaction issues
  • No free tier available, which limits testing before commitment
  • Feature-rich approach may overwhelm beginners despite template library

Pricing

Constant Contact uses a tiered subscription model starting at $12/month, with pricing scaling based on subscriber count and feature requirements. The lack of a free trial means you must commit to a paid plan immediately to test the platform.

Who is Constant Contact best for?

Constant Contact is best suited for nonprofits needing event management capabilities and local businesses wanting an affordable, integrated marketing platform without separate tool subscriptions. Small organizations with limited marketing budgets and teams will find the all-in-one approach particularly valuable for managing campaigns across multiple channels.

Frequently asked questions

Is Constant Contact free?

No, Constant Contact does not offer a free tier. Plans start at $12/month with no free trial period available. This means you'll need to commit to a paid subscription to access the platform and test its features.

What is Constant Contact best used for?

Constant Contact excels at email marketing campaigns, event management, and SMS outreach, making it particularly valuable for nonprofits and local businesses. The platform's integrated social media advertising and e-commerce tools also make it suitable for businesses wanting a comprehensive marketing solution in one place.

Is Constant Contact good for beginners?

Yes, Constant Contact offers an extensive library of professional email templates and an intuitive interface designed for users without technical expertise. However, the breadth of features combined with no free trial means beginners should carefully evaluate whether they need all the included tools before committing.

Does Constant Contact have an affiliate program?

Yes, Constant Contact operates an active affiliate program offering $80 commission per paid signup. This makes it an attractive option for bloggers, agencies, and content creators looking to monetize their audience through marketing tool referrals.
