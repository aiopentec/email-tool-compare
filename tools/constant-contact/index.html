Constant Contact Review: Email Marketing Platform for Small Businesses

Paid From $12/mo G2: 4.0★ 💰 $80 per paid signup

Constant Contact is an established email marketing platform founded in 1995 that combines email campaigns with event management, SMS, and social media advertising tools. The platform is specifically designed for nonprofits and local businesses seeking an all-in-one digital marketing solution without steep learning curves or enterprise-level complexity.

Our verdict: Constant Contact offers solid core email marketing features with useful add-ons like event management and SMS, making it appealing for small organizations with modest budgets. However, the gap between its G2 rating (4.0) and Trustpilot score (2.8) suggests customer support and delivery reliability concerns that warrant careful consideration before committing.

Pros

  • Comprehensive feature set including email, SMS, events, and social media ads all in one platform
  • Affordable starting price of $12/month makes it accessible for budget-conscious small businesses
  • Built-in event management and e-commerce capabilities reduce need for multiple tools
  • Beginner-friendly interface with pre-designed email templates for quick campaign launches

Cons

  • No free tier available, which eliminates the opportunity to test the platform before paying
  • Significant disparity between review ratings (4.0 on G2 vs 2.8 on Trustpilot) raises questions about service reliability
  • Limited advanced automation and personalization compared to enterprise competitors like HubSpot or ActiveCampaign

Pricing

Constant Contact operates on a tiered paid subscription model starting at $12 per month with no free option available. Pricing scales based on subscriber list size, making it cost-effective for businesses just starting their email marketing efforts.

Who is Constant Contact best for?

Constant Contact is best suited for nonprofits and local businesses that need straightforward email marketing without complex automation or advanced segmentation capabilities. Organizations managing events or running small e-commerce operations will particularly benefit from the integrated features and affordable entry price point.

Frequently asked questions

Is Constant Contact free?

No, Constant Contact does not offer a free tier. The platform requires a paid subscription starting at $12 per month. While there's no trial period, you can cancel anytime if you determine it's not the right fit for your business.

What is Constant Contact best used for?

Constant Contact excels at helping nonprofits and local businesses manage email campaigns, coordinate events, send SMS messages, and run social media ads from one platform. It's ideal for organizations seeking an integrated digital marketing solution without needing enterprise-level automation.

Is Constant Contact good for beginners?

Yes, Constant Contact is beginner-friendly with an intuitive interface, pre-built email templates, and straightforward navigation. However, potential users should review the lower Trustpilot ratings before signing up, as some beginners have reported customer support difficulties.

Does Constant Contact have an affiliate program?

Yes, Constant Contact offers an affiliate program that pays $80 per paid signup. This makes it a viable monetization option for marketers, content creators, and agencies recommending the platform to their audiences.
