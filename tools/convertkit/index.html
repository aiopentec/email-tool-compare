ConvertKit Review: Email Marketing for Creators

Freemium ✓ Free plan From $29/mo G2: 4.4★ 💰 30% recurring for 24 months

ConvertKit is an email marketing platform specifically designed for creators, bloggers, and online course sellers who want to build direct relationships with their audiences. Founded in 2013, it combines essential email marketing tools with creator-focused features like landing pages and commerce integrations. ConvertKit works best for independent creators looking to monetize their audience without the complexity of enterprise-level platforms.

Our verdict: ConvertKit excels at providing creators with an intuitive, purpose-built email marketing solution featuring visual automations, landing pages, and built-in commerce capabilities. However, its higher starting price point ($29/month) and mixed reputation (4.4 on G2 but only 3.2 on Trustpilot) suggest it's premium-priced for its feature set compared to general-purpose email marketing competitors.

Pros

  • Creator-focused design with landing pages and commerce built-in
  • Powerful visual automation builder that doesn't require coding
  • Generous free tier supporting up to 1,000 subscribers
  • Attractive 30% recurring affiliate commission for 24 months

Cons

  • Higher entry price ($29/month) compared to some competitors
  • Mixed customer satisfaction with Trustpilot rating of only 3.2/5
  • Limited advanced segmentation compared to enterprise email tools

Pricing

ConvertKit offers a free tier supporting up to 1,000 subscribers with core features, while paid plans start at $29/month. The pricing structure scales with subscriber count, making it accessible for bootstrapping creators but potentially expensive as audiences grow.

Who is ConvertKit best for?

ConvertKit is purpose-built for creators, bloggers, podcasters, and course sellers who want an all-in-one platform combining email marketing with audience monetization tools. It's ideal for independent creators comfortable spending $29+ monthly for a platform designed specifically for their workflow, rather than adapting a generic email tool.

Frequently asked questions

Is ConvertKit free?

Yes, ConvertKit offers a free tier that supports up to 1,000 subscribers with access to core features including email broadcasts, sequences, and subscriber tagging. Once you exceed 1,000 subscribers or need additional features, you'll need to upgrade to a paid plan starting at $29/month.

What is ConvertKit best used for?

ConvertKit is best used by creators, bloggers, podcasters, and online course sellers who want to build and monetize their email list. Its creator-focused features like landing pages, commerce integration, and visual automations make it ideal for building direct customer relationships and selling digital products.

Is ConvertKit good for beginners?

ConvertKit is moderately beginner-friendly with an intuitive interface and visual automation builder that doesn't require coding. However, beginners should be aware that the paid plans start at $29/month, which may be steep for those just starting out compared to free alternatives from competitors.

Does ConvertKit have an affiliate program?

Yes, ConvertKit offers an affiliate program with 30% recurring commission for 24 months on referred customers. This is an attractive program for creators and reviewers looking to earn passive income by recommending ConvertKit to their audience.
