ConvertKit Review: Email Marketing Built for Creators

Freemium ✓ Free plan From $29/mo G2: 4.4★ 💰 30% recurring for 24 months

ConvertKit is an email marketing platform specifically designed for creators, bloggers, and online course sellers. Founded in 2013, it combines essential email marketing tools with creator-focused features like landing pages and commerce integrations. It's an excellent choice for independent content creators looking to monetize their audiences.

Our verdict: ConvertKit excels at serving creators with its intuitive visual automation builder, integrated landing pages, and commerce capabilities—making it a solid all-in-one solution for audience monetization. However, its mixed review scores (4.4 on G2 but only 3.2 on Trustpilot) suggest customer support and pricing concerns warrant careful consideration before committing to paid plans.

Pros

  • Creator-focused platform with built-in landing pages and commerce features
  • Visual automation builder makes creating complex sequences intuitive
  • Generous free tier supporting up to 1,000 subscribers at no cost
  • Excellent affiliate program offering 30% recurring commission for 24 months

Cons

  • Lower Trustpilot rating (3.2) suggests potential customer support issues
  • Paid plans start at $29/month, which may be steep for beginners
  • Limited advanced segmentation compared to enterprise competitors

Pricing

ConvertKit offers a free plan for up to 1,000 subscribers, with paid plans starting at $29 per month. The freemium model allows creators to get started risk-free before scaling to paid tiers.

Who is ConvertKit best for?

ConvertKit is ideal for independent creators, bloggers, podcasters, and online course sellers who want to build direct relationships with their audiences while monetizing through email and commerce. If you're an established creator or entrepreneur looking for creator-specific tools beyond standard email marketing, ConvertKit's integrated features justify its premium positioning.

Frequently asked questions

Is ConvertKit free?

Yes, ConvertKit offers a free tier that supports up to 1,000 subscribers with access to core features like email broadcasts and basic automation. When you exceed 1,000 subscribers, you'll need to upgrade to a paid plan starting at $29 per month.

What is ConvertKit best used for?

ConvertKit is best used for creators who want an integrated platform combining email marketing, landing pages, and commerce tools. It's particularly strong for bloggers, course creators, podcasters, and digital product sellers who need to nurture audiences and drive conversions.

Is ConvertKit good for beginners?

ConvertKit is beginner-friendly thanks to its intuitive visual interface and creator-focused design, with the free tier allowing risk-free exploration. However, beginners on tight budgets may find the $29+ monthly pricing steep compared to competitors like Mailchimp, which offers longer free plans.

Does ConvertKit have an affiliate program?

Yes, ConvertKit offers a generous affiliate program paying 30% recurring commission for 24 months on referred subscriptions. This makes it particularly attractive for creators and marketers looking to generate passive income by recommending the platform.
