MailerLite Review: Affordable Email Marketing for Growing Businesses

Freemium ✓ Free plan From $9/mo G2: 4.6★ 💰 30% recurring

MailerLite is a freemium email marketing platform that has served small businesses and freelancers since 2010. It combines essential marketing tools like automation, landing pages, and pop-ups with an intuitive interface that doesn't require coding knowledge. The platform is best suited for budget-conscious entrepreneurs who want professional email campaigns without enterprise complexity.

Our verdict: MailerLite delivers strong value for small businesses and freelancers, with solid automation capabilities, a generous free tier, and an easy-to-use drag-and-drop editor. However, advanced users may find feature limitations compared to platforms like ConvertKit or ActiveCampaign, and customer support responsiveness has mixed reviews. Overall, it's an excellent choice for bootstrapped teams starting their email marketing journey.

Pros

  • Extremely affordable pricing starting at just $9/month with a generous 1,000-subscriber free tier
  • Intuitive drag-and-drop editor makes creating professional campaigns accessible to non-technical users
  • All-in-one platform includes automation, landing pages, pop-ups, surveys, and e-commerce integrations
  • Strong customer ratings (4.6 on G2, 4.5 on Trustpilot) with positive user sentiment

Cons

  • Limited advanced segmentation and personalization compared to premium competitors
  • Customer support has inconsistent response times and availability issues according to some reviews
  • Fewer third-party integrations compared to larger platforms like Mailchimp or HubSpot

Pricing

MailerLite offers a truly free plan for up to 1,000 subscribers with unlimited campaigns, and paid plans start at $9/month. Pricing scales with subscriber count, making it one of the most affordable options for growing teams.

Who is MailerLite best for?

MailerLite is ideal for small business owners, freelancers, and solopreneurs who need professional email marketing without expensive software. It's particularly well-suited for creators, coaches, and e-commerce sellers who want to combine email campaigns with landing pages and automation on a tight budget.

Frequently asked questions

Is MailerLite free?

Yes, MailerLite offers a completely free plan supporting up to 1,000 subscribers with unlimited campaigns and basic automation. The free tier includes their drag-and-drop editor, pop-ups, and landing pages, making it genuinely useful for small businesses. Paid plans begin at $9/month when you need more subscribers.

What is MailerLite best used for?

MailerLite excels at email marketing, landing page creation, and subscriber pop-ups for small to medium-sized businesses. It's particularly strong for e-commerce businesses, course creators, and service providers who want to build audiences and automate customer communications. The platform's survey feature and e-commerce integrations add value for businesses gathering customer feedback or selling products.

Is MailerLite good for beginners?

Absolutely. MailerLite is one of the most beginner-friendly email marketing platforms available, with an intuitive interface and extensive template library. The drag-and-drop editor requires no coding knowledge, and the free tier provides a risk-free way to learn. Tutorials and customer support resources help newcomers get started quickly.

Does MailerLite have an affiliate program?

Yes, MailerLite offers an affiliate program with a generous 30% recurring commission on referred customers. This makes it attractive for bloggers, marketers, and agencies who want to earn passive income by recommending the platform. The recurring commission means you earn as long as referred customers remain subscribed.
