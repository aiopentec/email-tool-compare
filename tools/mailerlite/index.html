MailerLite Review: Simple Email Marketing for Budget-Conscious Businesses

Freemium Free plan available From $9/mo G2: 4.6★

MailerLite is a freemium email marketing platform designed to help small businesses and freelancers launch professional campaigns without breaking the bank. Founded in 2010, it combines ease of use with powerful features like automation, landing pages, and e-commerce integrations. The platform excels at providing an intuitive experience while keeping costs low for growing teams.

Our verdict: MailerLite stands out as an excellent choice for small businesses and freelancers seeking affordable email marketing with a gentle learning curve. Its generous free tier, intuitive drag-and-drop editor, and solid automation capabilities make it highly competitive, though it lacks some of the advanced segmentation and AI features found in enterprise-level competitors. With strong ratings on G2 (4.6) and Trustpilot (4.5), it delivers genuine value, particularly for bootstrapped startups and solopreneurs.

Pros

  • Generous free plan supporting up to 1,000 subscribers at no cost
  • Intuitive drag-and-drop email editor requiring no coding knowledge
  • Built-in landing pages, pop-ups, and survey tools reduce need for third-party apps
  • Automation workflows and e-commerce integrations support growing businesses
  • Transparent, affordable pricing starting at just $9/month for paid plans

Cons

  • Limited advanced segmentation options compared to Klaviyo or HubSpot
  • Smaller feature set may require integrations as your business scales
  • Free plan limitations may push growth-stage companies toward competitors

Pricing

MailerLite operates on a freemium model with unlimited contacts for free accounts up to 1,000 subscribers, then paid plans starting at $9/month based on subscriber count. The transparent, tiered pricing grows with your business without surprise fees or minimums.

Who is MailerLite best for?

MailerLite is ideal for small business owners, freelancers, and solopreneurs who prioritize affordability and ease of use over enterprise features. These users benefit most from the free tier, simple interface, and built-in tools that eliminate the need for expensive platform stacking.

Key features

  • Automation
  • Landing Pages
  • Pop-Ups
  • Surveys
  • E-Commerce Integrations

Frequently asked questions

Is MailerLite free?

Yes, MailerLite offers a free plan supporting up to 1,000 subscribers with access to core features including the drag-and-drop editor, automation, and landing pages. Paid plans begin at $9/month for businesses exceeding 1,000 subscribers, making it one of the most affordable options in the market.

What is MailerLite best used for?

MailerLite excels at building email lists, creating automated campaigns, and running promotional activities for small businesses. Its included landing pages, pop-ups, and survey tools make it particularly effective for lead generation, product launches, and customer engagement without requiring additional software.

Is MailerLite good for beginners?

Absolutely. MailerLite is specifically designed with beginners in mind, featuring an intuitive drag-and-drop editor, helpful templates, and straightforward navigation. Even users with no email marketing experience can build professional campaigns within minutes of signing up.

Does MailerLite have an affiliate program?

Yes, MailerLite offers an affiliate program paying 30% recurring commission on referred customers. This makes it attractive for content creators, agencies, and marketers looking to generate passive income by recommending the platform.
