Omnisend Review 2025: Everything You Need to Know

Freemium Free plan available From $16/mo G2: 4.5★

Omnisend is ecommerce email and sms platform that positions itself for e-commerce brands wanting email and SMS together. Founded in 2014, it has become one of the most recognised platforms in the email marketing space. In this review we cover what it does well, where it falls short, and who should actually use it.

Our verdict: Omnisend is a solid choice for e-commerce brands wanting email and SMS together. Its free tier is genuinely useful. The main trade-off is between its particular strengths and the limitations that make alternatives worth considering for some users.

Pros

  • Good fit for e-commerce brands wanting email and SMS together
  • Competitive pricing starting at $16/mo
  • Key feature: Omnichannel Automation
  • Free tier available

Cons

  • Can get expensive at higher subscriber counts
  • Learning curve for advanced automations
  • Some features locked to higher tiers

Pricing

Omnisend offers a free plan, with paid plans from $16/month. Pricing typically scales with subscriber count.

Omnisend works best for e-commerce brands wanting email and SMS together. If you fit that profile, the feature set aligns well. If you need something different, check the alternatives section.

Frequently asked questions

Is Omnisend free?

Yes, Omnisend has a free plan supporting up to 250 subscribers. This makes it accessible for new senders testing the platform.

What is Omnisend best used for?

Omnisend is best for e-commerce brands wanting email and SMS together. Its standout features include omnichannel automation, SMS, push notifications, which suit this use case well.

Is Omnisend good for beginners?

Omnisend is generally beginner-friendly. The interface is straightforward for basic campaigns, though advanced automation features have a learning curve.

Does Omnisend have an affiliate program?

Yes, Omnisend runs an affiliate program paying 20% recurring. This is worth considering if you plan to recommend the platform to your audience.
