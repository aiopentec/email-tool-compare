Omnisend: The Complete Review for Ecommerce Email & SMS Marketing

Freemium ✓ Free plan From $16/mo G2: 4.5★ 💰 20% recurring

Omnisend is an ecommerce-focused email and SMS marketing platform founded in 2014 that combines multiple communication channels into a single automation suite. It's best suited for e-commerce brands that want to reach customers through email, SMS, and push notifications without juggling separate tools.

Our verdict: Omnisend delivers solid value for ecommerce businesses seeking integrated email and SMS capabilities, with strong ratings (4.5 on G2, 4.6 on Trustpilot) and user-friendly automation. However, it may feel overwhelming for beginners due to its feature density, and pricing scales quickly beyond the free tier as your contact list grows.

Pros

  • True omnichannel approach combining email, SMS, and push notifications in one platform
  • Excellent pre-built workflows and product picker tool specifically designed for ecommerce
  • Generous free tier with up to 250 subscribers and core features included
  • Advanced segmentation capabilities enable highly targeted customer campaigns
  • Strong customer satisfaction with 4.5 G2 and 4.6 Trustpilot ratings

Cons

  • Learning curve can be steep for beginners due to extensive features and customization options
  • Paid plans start at $16/month but pricing increases significantly with subscriber growth
  • Limited information about integrations and third-party app ecosystem compared to competitors

Pricing

Omnisend operates on a freemium model with a free tier supporting up to 250 subscribers, with all core features included. Paid plans begin at $16/month and scale based on your subscriber count and feature needs.

Who is Omnisend best for?

Omnisend is ideal for ecommerce brands seeking an all-in-one platform to manage email, SMS, and push notifications without integrating multiple tools. Small to mid-sized online retailers will find the most value, particularly those prioritizing workflow automation and customer segmentation.

Frequently asked questions

Is Omnisend free?

Yes, Omnisend offers a free tier that supports up to 250 subscribers and includes access to core features like email, SMS, and basic automation. The free plan is fully functional, though paid plans starting at $16/month unlock additional features and higher subscriber limits.

What is Omnisend best used for?

Omnisend is purpose-built for ecommerce email and SMS marketing, excelling at abandoned cart recovery, product recommendations, and customer retention campaigns. Its pre-built workflows and product picker tool make it particularly powerful for online retailers managing multiple communication channels.

Is Omnisend good for beginners?

Omnisend can work for beginners thanks to its pre-built templates and workflows, but the platform's depth of features may feel overwhelming initially. Beginners should expect a learning curve, though the ecommerce focus means features align well with typical online store needs rather than generic marketing tasks.

Does Omnisend have an affiliate program?

Yes, Omnisend offers an affiliate program with 20% recurring commission on referred customers. This makes it attractive for agencies and marketing professionals looking to earn ongoing revenue from client referrals.
